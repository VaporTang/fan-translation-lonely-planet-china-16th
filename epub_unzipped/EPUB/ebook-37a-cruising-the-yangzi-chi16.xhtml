Taking a boat down the Yangzi River (长江, Cháng Jiāng) – China’s longest and most scenic waterway – is all about the journey rather than the destination. It isn’t just an escape from marathon train journeys and agonising bus rides, but a chance to kick back as an astonishing panorama slides by at a sedate pace which allows time for contemplation and relaxation. Cruising the Yangzi is a truly unique experience, one that gets you up close with mostly domestic travellers allowing time for real interaction. Jump aboard.
乘船沿着扬子江（ 长江 ，Cháng Jiāng）顺流而下——中国最长、风景最优美的水道——完全是旅程而不是目的地。这不仅是逃离马拉松式火车旅行和痛苦的巴士之旅，而且是一个放松的机会，因为令人惊叹的全景以平静的节奏滑过，让您有时间沉思和放松。游览扬子号是一种真正独特的体验，让您与大多数国内游客近距离接触，从而有时间进行真正的互动。跳上船。

The Route
路线

The journey puts you adrift on China’s mightiest – and the world’s third-longest – waterway, the gushing 6300km Yangzi River. Starting life as trickles of snowmelt in the Tanggula Mountains of southwestern Qinghai, the river then spills from Tibet, swells through seven Chinese provinces, sucks in water from hundreds of tributaries and rolls powerfully into the Pacific Ocean north of Shanghai.
这段旅程将带您漂流在中国最强大、也是世界第三长的水道上，即滔滔不绝的 6300 公里长的扬子江。这条河最初是青海西南部唐古拉山脉的融雪涓涓细流，然后从西藏溢出，流经中国七个省份，从数百条支流中吸水，强劲地流入上海以北的太平洋。

Apocryphally the handiwork of Yu the Great, a legendary architect of the river, the gorges – Qutang, Wu and Xiling – commence just east of Fengjie in Chongqing province and level out west of Yichang in Hubei province, a distance of around 200km. The principal route for those cruising the Yangzi River is therefore between the cities of Chongqing and Yichang.
曲塘峡谷、吴峡谷和西岭峡谷是传奇河流建筑师禹大帝的杰作，始于重庆省奉节以东，平坦于湖北宜昌以西，全长约 200 公里。因此，在扬子江上航行的主要路线是在重庆和宜昌之间。

The route can be travelled in either direction, but most passengers journey downstream from Chongqing. Travelling upstream from Yichang is slower and hence ensures a less crowded boat. Going downstream, cruises are typically four days, three nights; going upstream, cruises are five days, four nights. However, you can shorten the trip to as little as two or three days by starting (or ending) in Fengjie or Wanzhou – cruise operators will arrange your bus transport to/from Chongqing.
该路线可以向任一方向行驶，但大多数乘客从重庆顺流而下。从宜昌逆流而上的速度较慢，因此可以确保船只不那么拥挤。顺流而下，游轮通常为四天三夜;逆流而上，游轮是五天四夜。但是，您可以通过从奉节或万州开始（或结束）将行程缩短到两到三天——邮轮运营商将为您安排往返重庆的巴士交通。

If you buy your ticket from an agency, ensure you’re not charged upfront for the sights along the way, as you may not want to visit them all. Some of the sights are underwhelming and entrance fees are as steep as the surrounding inclines. The only ticket truly worth buying in advance is for the popular and worthwhile Little Three Gorges tour, which is often full.
如果您从旅行社购买门票，请确保您不会预先支付沿途景点的费用，因为您可能不想参观所有景点。有些景点平淡无奇，入场费与周围的斜坡一样陡峭。唯一真正值得提前购买的门票，就是人气爆棚、不虚此行的小三峡游，经常座无虚席。

Chongqing to Wanzhou
重庆到万州

The initial stretch is slow-going and unremarkable, although the dismal view of factories gradually gives way to attractive terraced countryside and the occasional small town.
最初的路段进展缓慢，平淡无奇，尽管工厂的惨淡景色逐渐让位于迷人的梯田乡村和偶尔的小镇。

Passing the drowned town of Fuling (涪陵), the first port of call is at Fengdu (丰都), 170km from Chongqing city. Long nicknamed the City of Ghosts (鬼城, Guǐchéng), the town is just that: inundated in 2009, its residents were moved across the river. This is the stepping-off point for crowds to clamber up Ming Mountain (名山; Míng Shān; adult ¥95, cable car ¥20), with its theme-park crop of ghost-focused temples.
经过溺水镇涪陵 ，第一个停靠港是距离重庆市 170 公里的丰都 。长期以来，该镇被昵称为鬼城（鬼城 ，Guǐchéng），它就是这样：2009 年被洪水淹没，居民被转移到河对岸。这里是人群攀登名山的下车点 （ 名山 ;明山;成人 95 日元，缆车 20 日元）， 其主题公园中的幽灵寺庙。

Drifting through the county of Zhongzhou, the boat takes around three hours to arrive at Shíbǎozhài (石宝寨; Stone Treasure Stockade; ¥150; h8am-4pm) on the northern bank of the river. A 12-storey, 56m-high wooden pagoda built on a huge, river-water-encircled rock bluff, the structure dates to the reign of Qing dynasty emperor Kangxi (1662–1722). Your boat may stop for rapid expeditions up to the tower and for climbs into its interior.
船漂流穿过中州县，大约需要三个小时才能到达石宝寨 ; 石宝寨;¥150; h 上午 8 点至下午 4 点） 在河北岸。一座 12 层、高 56 米的木塔建在巨大的河水环绕的岩石悬崖上，其历史可以追溯到清朝康熙皇帝（1662-1722 年）统治时期。您的船可能会停下来快速探险到塔楼并爬入塔楼内部。

Most morning boats moor for the night at partially inundated Wanzhou (万州, also called Wanxian). Travellers aiming to get from A to B as fast as possible while taking in the gorges can skip the Chongqing to Wanzhou section by taking a bus instead – this reduces the length of the cruise to three days.
大多数早晨的船只停泊在部分被淹没的万州 （ 万州 ，也称为万县）过夜。如果旅客希望在欣赏峡谷的同时尽快从 A 地到达 B 地，可以乘坐巴士跳过重庆至万州路段，从而将游轮时间缩短至三天。

Wanzhou to Yichang
万州到宜昌

Boats departing from Wanzhou soon pass the relocated Zhangfei Temple (张飞庙; Zhāngfēi Miào; ¥40). Quick disembarkations can be made here, allowing a visit to the ancient but much-restored temple which was moved 20 miles upstream in 2002 and now sits opposite Yunyang (云阳). A modern, utilitarian and unremarkable town strung out along the northern bank of the river, Yunyang is typical of many of the new settlements created in the wake of the building of the Three Gorges Dam. Past here, boats drift on past ragged islets, some carpeted with small patchworks of fields, and alongside riverbanks striated with terraced slopes, rising like green ribbons up the inclines.
从万州出发的船只很快就经过搬迁后的张飞庙 （ 张飞庙 ;张辉庙;¥40）。 可以在这里快速下船，参观这座古老但经过精心修复的寺庙，该寺庙于 2002 年向上游移动 20 英里，现在位于云阳对面。 云阳是一座现代化、实用主义且不起眼的小镇，沿着江河北岸延伸，是三峡大坝建成后创建的许多新定居点的典型代表。经过这里，船只在破烂的小岛上漂流，有些小岛上铺着小片田野，沿着河岸，河岸上布满了梯田斜坡，像绿带一样沿着斜坡上升。

The ancient town of Fengjie (奉节), capital of the state of Kui during the Spring and Autumn (722–481 BC) and Warring States (475–221 BC) periods, overlooks Qutang Gorge, the first of the three gorges. The town – where most ships berth – is also the entrance point to half-submerged White Emperor City (白帝城; Báidìchéng; ¥180), where the King of Shu, Liu Bei, entrusted his son and kingdom to Zhuge Liang, as chronicled in The Romance of the Three Kingdoms.
奉节古镇是春秋（公元前 722-481 年）和战国（公元前 475-221 年）夔国的首都，俯瞰着三峡中的第一座瞿塘峡。该镇——大多数船只停泊的地方——也是半淹没的白帝城 （ 白帝城 ;Báidìchéng;¥180）， 蜀王刘备将他的儿子和王国托付给诸葛亮，如《 三国演义 》中所记载的那样。

Qutang Gorge (瞿塘峡, Qútáng Xiá), also known as Kui Gorge (夔峡, Kuí Xiá), rises dramatically into view, towering into huge vertiginous slabs of rock, its cliffs jutting out in jagged and triangular chunks. The shortest and narrowest of the three gorges, 8km-long Qutang is over almost as abruptly as it starts, but is considered by many to be the most awe-inspiring. The gorge offers a dizzying perspective onto huge strata despite having some of its power robbed by the rising waters. On the northern bank is Bellows Gorge (风箱峡, Fēngxiāng Xiá), where nine coffins were discovered, possibly placed here by an ancient tribe.
瞿塘峡（瞿塘峡 ，Qútáng Xiá），又称夔峡（夔峡，Kuí Xiá），在视野中耸立，耸立在令人眩晕的巨大岩石板中，其悬崖以锯齿状和三角形块状突出。曲塘是三峡中最短、最窄的峡谷，全长 8 公里，几乎和开始时一样突然结束，但被许多人认为是最令人敬畏的。峡谷提供了令人眼花缭乱的巨大地层视角，尽管它的一些力量被上涨的海水所剥夺。北岸是风箱峡 ，在那里发现了九口棺材，可能是一个古老的部落放置在这里。

After Qutang Gorge the terrain folds into a 20km stretch of low-lying land before boats pull in at the riverside town of Wushan (巫山), situated high above the river. Most boats stop at Wushan for five to six hours so passengers can transfer to smaller boats for trips along the Little Three Gorges (小三峡; Xiǎo Sānxiá; ticket ¥240-280) on the Daning River (大宁河, Dàníng Hé). The landscape is gorgeous and you’re right up close to it, and many travellers insist that the narrow gorges are more impressive than their larger namesakes. Some tours include a 40-minute ride on local fishing boats here, or further excursions to the Little Little Three Gorges (小小三峡, Xiǎo Xiǎo Sānxiá).
过了瞿塘峡谷，地形折叠成绵延 20 公里的低洼地，然后船只停在位于河面高处的河边小镇巫山 。 大多数船只在巫山停留五到六个小时，因此乘客可以换乘较小的船，沿着小三峡 （ 小三峡 ;Xiǎo Sānxiá;门票 ¥240-280） 在大宁河 （Dàníng Hé）上。风景非常美丽，您就在附近，许多旅行者坚持认为狭窄的峡谷比它们更大的同名峡谷更令人印象深刻。有些旅行包括在这里乘坐当地渔船 40 分钟，或进一步游览小小三峡（Xiǎo Xiǎo Sānxiá）。

Back on the Yangzi River, boats pull away from Wushan to enter the middle Wu Gorge, under a bright-red bridge. Some of the cultivated fields on the slopes overhanging the river reach almost illogical angles.
回到扬子江，船只离开巫山，进入吴峡中部，在一座鲜红色的桥下。悬垂在河边的山坡上的一些耕地几乎达到了不合逻辑的角度。

Wu Gorge (巫峡; Wū Xiá) – the Gorge of Witches – is stunning, cloaked in green and carpeted in shrubs, its sides frequently disappearing into ethereal layers of mist. About 40km in length, its towering cliffs are topped by sharp, jagged peaks on the northern bank. A total of 12 peaks cluster on either side, including Goddess Peak (神女峰, Shénnǚ Fēng) and Peak of the Immortals (集仙峰, Jíxiān Fēng). If you’re fortunate, you’ll catch the sunrise over Goddess Peak.
巫峡 （ 巫峡 ;巫峡）——女巫峡谷——令人惊叹，被绿色覆盖，灌木覆盖，它的两侧经常消失在空灵的雾气中。全长约 40 公里，高耸的悬崖顶部是北岸尖锐的锯齿状山峰。两侧共有 12 座山峰聚集 ，包括神女峰 （神女峰 ， Shénnǚ Fēng） 和仙峰 （ 集仙峰 ， Jíxiān Fēng）。如果幸运的话，您还能看到女神峰的日出。

Boats continue floating eastward out of Wu Gorge and into Hubei province, along a 45km section before reaching the last of the three gorges. At this time, many boats offer the option of a two-hour trip on motorised dragon boats along Jiuwan Stream (九畹溪, Jiǔwǎn Xī) and nearby tributaries of the Yangzi. Some travellers enjoy the experience, although the scenery isn’t as inspiring as that of the Little Three Gorges.
船只继续从吴峡向东漂流，进入湖北省，沿着 45 公里的路段到达三个峡谷中的最后一个。此时，许多船只提供乘坐机动龙舟沿着九畹溪 （Jiǔwǎn Xī） 和附近扬子河支流航行两小时的选择。一些旅行者喜欢这种体验，尽管风景不如小三峡那么令人振奋。

At 80km, Xiling Gorge (西陵峡, Xīlíng Xiá) is the longest and perhaps least spectacular gorge; sections of the gorge in the west have been submerged. Note the slow-moving cargo vessels, including long freight ships loaded with mounds of coal, ploughing downriver to Shanghai. The gorge was traditionally the most hazardous, where hidden shoals and reefs routinely holed vessels, but it has long been tamed, even though river traffic slows when the fog reduces visibility.
西陵峡 （ 西陵峡 ，Xīlíng Xiá）全长 80 公里，是最长的峡谷，也许是最不壮观的峡谷;西部峡谷的部分地区已被淹没。请注意缓慢行驶的货船，包括满载煤炭的长货船，顺流而下前往上海。该峡谷传统上是最危险的峡谷，那里隐藏的浅滩和礁石经常会让船只穿洞，但它早已被驯服，尽管当雾降低能见度时河流交通会减慢。

Apart from the top-end luxury cruises, tour boats no longer pass through the monumental Three Gorges Dam, although some tours offer the option of a visit to the dam by bus. Once you disembark, it’s about an hour by bus to Yichang – check to see if this is included or if you’ll need to take public transport.
除了高端豪华游轮外，游船不再经过具有纪念意义的三峡大坝 ，尽管有些旅游团提供乘坐巴士参观大坝的选项。下车后，乘坐巴士大约需要一个小时到宜昌——检查是否包含这或是否需要乘坐公共交通工具。

Boats
船

There are two types of boat: luxury cruises and tourist boats. Cabins on the top-end cruises are like comfy hotel rooms, complete with TV, private balconies and daily maid service. Tourist boats are more basic, with simple private rooms.
船有两种类型：豪华游轮和旅游船。高端游轮上的客舱就像舒适的酒店客房，配有电视、私人阳台和每日清洁服务。旅游船比较基本，包间简单。

Luxury Cruises
豪华游轮

The most luxurious passage is on international-standard cruise ships (豪华游轮, háohuá yóulún), where maximum comfort and visibility accompany a leisurely agenda. Trips normally depart Chongqing and include shore visits to all the major sights (Three Gorges Dam, Little Three Gorges etc), allowing time to tour the attractions (often secondary to the scenery). Cabins have air-con, TV (perhaps satellite), fridge/minibar and sometimes wi-fi (generally unusable). These vessels are aimed at both Chinese and western tourists and are ideal for travellers with time, money and negligible Chinese skills. Top-end cruises feature daily buffet meals, generally including both western and Chinese food. Seating is assigned, and meals are included in your ticket price. The average duration for such a cruise going downstream is three nights and four days.
最豪华的通道是在国际标准游轮（ 豪华游轮 ，háohuá yóulún）上，在那里，最大的舒适度和能见度伴随着悠闲的议程。旅行通常从重庆出发，包括岸上游览所有主要景点（三峡大坝、小三峡等），以便有时间游览景点（通常次于风景）。客舱配有空调、电视（可能是卫星电视）、冰箱/迷你吧，有时还配有无线网络（通常无法使用）。这些船只面向中国和西方游客，非常适合时间、金钱和中国技能可以忽略不计的旅行者。高端游轮提供每日自助餐，通常包括西餐和中餐。座位是分配的，餐食包含在您的票价中。这种顺流而下的游轮的平均持续时间为三夜四天。

Fares range from about ¥2000 (from Chongqing) to ¥2800 (from Yichang), but will vary according to the season. You can also book online ahead of time; some of these trips will incorporate the cruise as part of an all-inclusive package and take in other must-see sights in China as well.
票价从约 2000 日元（从重庆出发）到 2800 日元（从宜昌出发）不等，但会根据季节而有所不同。您也可以提前在线预订;其中一些旅行将把游轮作为全包套餐的一部分，并参观中国的其他必看景点。

Tourist Cruise Ships
旅游游轮

Typically departing from Chongqing around 9pm, ordinary tourist cruise ships (普通游轮, pǔtōng yóulún) usually take just under 40 hours to reach Yichang (including three nights on board). Some boats stop at all the sights; others stop at just a few. They are less professional than the luxury tour cruises and are squarely aimed at domestic travellers (Chinese food, little English spoken).
普通游轮（普通游轮 ，pǔtōng yóulún）通常在晚上 9 点左右从重庆出发，通常需要不到 40 小时才能到达宜昌（包括船上三晚）。有些船停在所有景点;其他人则止步于少数。它们不如豪华游轮专业，而是直接针对国内旅客（中国菜，很少会说英语）。

Expect early starts: the public-address system starts going off after 6am. Cabins in all classes are fairly basic, but come with AC and a TV and usually have a small attached bathroom with a shower (although that doesn’t mean hot water).
预计会提前开始：公共广播系统在早上 6 点后开始关闭。所有舱室都相当基本，但配有空调和电视，通常有一个带淋浴的小连接浴室（尽管这并不意味着热水）。

Many travellers now book packages that take you first by bus from Chongqing to either Wanzhou or Fengjie, where you board a vessel for the rest of the trip. This reduces the journey by one or two nights – prices may be the same for either option when travelling downstream.
现在，许多旅行者预订的套餐首先带您乘坐巴士从重庆前往万州或奉节，然后在那里登船完成剩余的旅程。这会减少一到两晚的行程——顺流而下时，任一选择的价格可能相同。

In theory, you can buy tickets on the day of travel, but booking one or two days in advance is recommended. Fares vary, although not by much, depending whether you buy your ticket from a hostel, agency or direct from the ticket hall. Always ensure you know exactly what the price includes.
理论上，您可以在旅行当天购买车票，但建议提前一两天预订。票价差异不大，但幅度不大，具体取决于您是从旅馆、旅行社还是直接从售票大厅购买车票。始终确保您确切地知道价格包含什么。

Tickets
票

In Chongqing most hotels, travel agents and ferry-port ticket halls can sell you a trip on either the luxury cruise ships or the ordinary tourist boats. The process may become more centralised after the completion of the new dock area, however.
在重庆，大多数酒店、旅行社和渡口售票大厅都可以向您推销豪华游轮或普通旅游船的旅行。然而，在新码头区竣工后，该过程可能会变得更加集中。

In Yichang, it’s very simple – head to the Three Gorges Tourist Centre. It sells everything from day trips to luxury cruises upstream at a fair price.
在宜昌，这很简单——前往三峡游客中心 。它以合理的价格出售从一日游到上游豪华游轮的所有商品。

The price of your ticket should include the one-hour shuttle bus ride between Yichang and the cruise port about 45km upstream.
您的车票价格应包括宜昌和上游约 45 公里处的游轮港口之间的一小时班车费用。
