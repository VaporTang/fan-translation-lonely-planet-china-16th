Family Travel
家庭旅行

Taking your kids to China can be challenging, eye-opening, fun and memorable. Sure you’ll need to plan a bit, and at times be a super-patient parent, but you could also find yourself and your family on the journey of a lifetime.
带孩子去中国可能充满挑战、令人大开眼界、有趣且令人难忘。当然，您需要一些计划，有时还需要做一个超级耐心的父母，但您也可能会发现自己和您的家人正在踏上一生的旅程。

Keeping Costs Down
降低成本

Trains
火车

Request a child’s ticket for kids between 1.2m and 1.5m tall (ticket prices for children are according to height rather than age). These are half the price of adult tickets for seats, and about 25% off for sleeper berths. Children under 1.2m travel for free but don’t get their own seat or berth.
身高 1.2 米至 1.5 米之间的儿童请申请儿童票（儿童票价根据身高而非年龄而定）。这些座位价格是成人票的一半，卧铺可享受约 25% 的折扣。身高 1.2m 以下的儿童免费乘车，但没有自己的座位或铺位。

Tourist Sites
旅游景点

Kids can often get in for half price, or free if under 1.2 metres tall. It’s always worth asking at the ticket office.
孩子们通常可以半价入场，如果身高低于 1.2 米，则免费入场。在售票处询问总是值得的。

Dining
餐饮

Choose Chinese restaurants over western-style ones. Dishes are typically large and designed for sharing, and staples like plain cooked rice only cost a few yuan.
选择中餐厅而不是西式餐厅。菜肴通常很大，专为分享而设计，而像白米饭这样的主食只需几元钱 。

Hotels
酒店

It’s always worth enquiring about a family room (家庭房; jiātíng fáng), or failing that a suite (套房; tàofáng) where extra beds (加床; jiā chuáng) are permitted. And don’t forget, always haggle for a discount in local hotels.
家庭房（ 家庭房 ;jiātíng fáng），或未能达到套房（ 套房 ;tàofáng）其中加床（加床 ; 贾川 ）是允许的。别忘了，一定要讨价还价，在当地酒店享受折扣。

Parks
公园

Parks in larger cities are either free or very cheap to enter. Ideal for family picnics and outdoor activities like kite-flying, some also have swimming facilities and fairground rides.
大城市的公园要么免费，要么进入非常便宜。非常适合家庭野餐和放风筝等户外活动，部分客房还设有游泳设施和游乐场游乐设施。

Children Will Love…
孩子们会喜欢的......

Journeys
旅程

Star Ferry, Hong Kong Bundle the kids on the cross-harbour ferry for a dazzling, all-colour evening trip.
天星小轮 ， 香港带着孩子们乘坐过海渡轮，享受一次令人眼花缭乱的彩色夜间旅行。

Hong Kong tram Grab seats at the front of the upper deck for a sightseeing tour of north Hong Kong island; tots under three go for free!
香港电车在上层甲板的前部座位，进行香港岛北部的观光之旅;三岁以下的儿童免费！

Xi’an City Walls Get the kids cycling around China’s longest intact city wall.
习安城墙让孩子们骑自行车绕着中国最长的完整城墙。

The Great Outdoors
户外活动

The Great Wall Best accessed from Beijing, it’s a teenage kid’s mandatory social-media pilgrimage and selfie spot.
长城从北京出发，这里是十几岁的孩子必去的社交媒体朝圣和自拍地点。

Yangshuo Teenage kids will love cycling between the karst peaks and bamboo rafting along the Yulong River.
阳朔十几岁的孩子会喜欢在喀斯特山峰之间骑自行车和沿着玉龙江漂流。

Hainan Tots will have a great time at the beaches and splashing about in the warm waters.
海南孩子们将在海滩上度过愉快的时光，并在温暖的海水中嬉戏。

Excitement
兴奋

The Plank Walk on Hua Shan Teenagers will get the ultimate white-knuckle buzz above a 2000m drop.
华山的木板步道青少年将在 2000 米的落差上获得终极的紧张嗡嗡声。

Shanghai World Financial Center Three observation decks and superfast elevators will wow little ones at this iconic skyscraper.
上海环球金融中心三个观景台和超高速电梯将让小孩子们惊叹于这座标志性的摩天大楼。

Harbin Ice & Snow Festival This fairytale wonderland of snow, ice and wintry fun in the deep-freeze of Heilongjiang is perfect for kids.
哈尔滨冰雪节这个童话般的仙境，在黑龙江的冰雪和寒冷的乐趣中，非常适合孩子们。

Shows
显示

Beijing’s Acrobatics Shows Daredevil feats of strength, agility and skill will captivate kids, and shows are short.
北京的杂技表演勇敢的力量、敏捷性和技巧会吸引孩子们，而且表演很短。

Symphony of Lights Little ones will gaze in wonder at the nightly light show painted across Hong Kong’s iconic skyline.
幻彩咏香江小孩子们会惊叹地凝视着香港标志性天际线上绘制的夜间灯光秀。

Mickey’s Storybook Express The character parade is a highlight of a trip to Shanghai Disney, and unlike the rides there are no queues.
米奇的故事书快车角色游行是上海迪士尼之旅的一大亮点，与游乐设施不同，没有排队。

Educational
教育

Army of Terracotta Warriors Coming face to face with thousands of individually wrought soldiers from aeons ago will spark young imaginations.
兵马俑军团与亿万年前成千上万的单独训练的士兵面对面，将激发年轻人的想象力。

Giant Panda Breeding Research Base Kids can learn all about giant pandas in Chengdu, and even feed them by hand.
大熊猫繁育研究基地孩子们可以在成都了解大熊猫，甚至可以亲手喂它们。

Kite Flying Kids and kidults can take to the skies in Weifang, home of the International Kite Festival.
放 风筝孩子们和儿童可以在潍坊飞上天空，潍坊是国际风筝节的举办地。

Region by Region
按地区划分

Shanghai Kids’ big-ticket theme-park choices plus high-altitude observation decks, museums, delicious dumplings and much more.
上海孩子们的高价主题公园选择以及高海拔观景台、博物馆、美味的饺子等等。

Beijing The Great Wall is a mandatory stop, zipping across Beijing’s frozen lakes in winter is fun for teens and tots, and guided tours will help Beijing’s world-class historic sites to life.
北京长城是必经之地，冬季穿越北京结冰的湖泊对青少年和儿童来说很有趣，导游将帮助北京的世界级历史遗迹焕发生机。

Hong Kong The magic of the Star Ferry and the gravity-defying Peak Tram, Disneyland and dolphin watching, plus super parks and hiking trails.
香港天星小轮和反重力山顶缆车、迪士尼乐园和观赏海豚的魔力，以及超级公园和远足小径。

Chengdu Watch your kids go wide-eyed at the pandas, especially the playful infants frolicking adorably with their human minders.
成都看着您的孩子睁大眼睛看大熊猫，尤其是顽皮的婴儿与人类看护人一起可爱地嬉戏。

Hainan Island Surfing, swimming, China’s best beaches and mountains of healthy tropical fruit. Resort hotels in Sanya have excellent facilities for kids of all ages.
海南岛冲浪、游泳、中国最好的海滩和山脉的健康热带水果。三亚的度假酒店为所有年龄段的孩子提供一流的设施。

Xi’an Young Indiana Joneses will relish all the archeological discoveries in China’s ancient capital of Chang’an.
习安年轻的印第安纳琼斯将享受中国古都长安的所有考古发现。

Yangshuo Family activities abound in the karst landscape of Yangshuo, from bamboo rafting and off-road cycling to cooking classes, hot-air ballooning and rock climbing.
阳朔阳朔的喀斯特地貌中，家庭活动比比皆是，从竹筏漂流和越野自行车到烹饪课程、热气球和攀岩。

Pingyao Little ones will enjoy exploring the crooked alleyways, delving into courtyard museums and walking atop the perimeter walls of this time-warped Qing dynasty town.
平遥小孩子们会喜欢探索弯曲的小巷，深入庭院博物馆，并漫步在这座时间扭曲的清代小镇的围墙上。

Yunnan The perfect province for an intrepid family adventure: biking between minority villages, visiting Dali and Lijiang, and checking out the rainforests of Xishuangbanna.
云南这里是家庭冒险的完美省份：在少数民族村庄之间骑自行车，游览大理和丽江，并参观西双版纳的热带雨林。

Qingdao Miles of sandy bathing beaches, fairy-tale Bavarian architecture, and bags (literally) of Qingdao beer to soothe harassed parents.
青岛绵延数英里的沙滩、童话般的巴伐利亚建筑，以及一袋袋（字面意思）的青岛啤酒，以安抚被骚扰的父母。

Harbin Kids can hit the slopes in summer at the world’s biggest indoor ski centre. And when winter does arrive, ice and snow sculptures turn Harbin into a winter wonderland.
哈尔滨夏季，孩子们可以在世界上最大的室内滑雪中心滑雪。当冬天真的到来时，冰雪雕塑会把哈尔滨变成一个冬季仙境。

Good to Know
很高兴知道

Look out for the c icon for family-friendly suggestions throughout this guide.
在本指南中，请留意 适合家庭的 c 建议的图标。

APack any medicines your kids may need as you may not be able to find them in China. Make sure vaccinations are up to date.
A 带上您的孩子可能需要的任何药物，因为您可能无法在中国找到它们。确保疫苗接种是最新的。

AKids need to be prepared for squat toilets.
A 孩子们需要为蹲厕所做好准备。

AAlthough improving, air pollution can be caustic in China; consider investing in face masks for your young ones.
A 尽管空气污染有所改善，但中国的空气污染可能是腐蚀性的;考虑为您的孩子投资口罩。

AYour teenagers are going to need a VPN (virtual private network) on their smartphones to keep using Snapchat, Instagram or WhatsApp. Same applies to you. Download VPNs to devices before arriving in China.
A 您的青少年将需要在他们的智能手机上安装 VPN（虚拟专用网络）才能继续使用 Snapchat、Instagram 或 WhatsApp。这同样适用于您。在抵达中国之前将 VPN 下载到设备。

AFlights are largely painless, but expect delays.
A 航班基本上是无痛的，但预计会出现延误。

Dining Out
外出就餐

In big cities such as Shanghai, Beijing and Hong Kong you’ll find lots of choice with kids’ menus. In tourist towns and popular destinations, it’s a similar story. Off the beaten path you’ll have to dine as locals do and work through Chinese-language menus.
在上海、北京和香港等大城市，您会发现很多儿童菜单可供选择。在旅游城镇和热门目的地，情况也类似。在人迹罕至的地方，您将不得不像当地人一样用餐，并通过中文菜单工作。

Snacking on the hoof: order up steaming ròujiāmó (cooked meat sandwich, usually pork), lamb kebabs, Hong Kong–style egg waffles or watermelons. Look out for street-side sellers of popcorn, cooked up old-school with a bang in a traditional flame-heated popcorn machine.
马蹄小吃：点一份热气腾腾的 ròujiāmó（熟肉三明治，通常是猪肉）、羊肉串、港式鸡蛋华夫饼或西瓜。留意街边的爆米花小贩，这些爆米花是在传统的火焰加热爆米花机中用老式的爆米花烹制而成的。

McDonalds and KFC are everywhere in towns and cities; Burger King was a rarity but is increasingly common. Dicos (德克士; Dekeshi) is a decent Chinese alternative with tasty rice lunch sets.
麦当劳和肯德基在城镇无处不在;汉堡王很少见，但越来越普遍。迪科斯 （ 德克士 ;Dekeshi）是一个不错的中式替代品，提供美味的米饭午餐套餐。

Picky eaters might be tempted by Chinese standbys like boiled dumplings (水饺; shuǐjiǎo) and egg-fried rice (蛋炒; dàn chǎofàn). Rice porridge (粥; zhōu) is plain, soothing and ideal for infants.
挑食的人可能会被中国人备用的东西所诱惑，比如水饺（水饺 ; 舒教 ）蛋炒饭（ 蛋炒 ;dàn chǎofàn）。米粥（ 粥 ;zhōu）素色、舒缓，非常适合婴儿。

Open Spaces
开放空间

Despite China’s size there’s often a surprising lack of free and accessible open space, and you often need to pay to access what you discover. Much land is used for agriculture and most national parks levy an admission fee, while city parks are often quite synthetic places, without lawns or grass.
尽管中国幅员辽阔，但经常缺乏免费和无障碍的开放空间，而且您通常需要付费才能访问您发现的内容。许多土地用于农业，大多数国家公园都收取入场费，而城市公园通常是相当合成的地方，没有草坪或草地。

Transport
运输

Kids should love overnight train journeys. Long-distance bus journeys may be more fraught – limit their liquid intake as buses stop only rarely for the call of nature. Think about hiring a car or a taxi for the day to get to sights outside town. Prepare for seat-belt-free rear seats in taxis.
孩子们应该喜欢过夜的火车旅行。长途巴士旅行可能更令人担忧——限制他们的液体摄入量，因为公共汽车很少因大自然的召唤而停靠。考虑租一辆汽车或出租车前往城外的景点。为出租车中不系安全带的后座做好准备。

Crossing the Road
过马路

China’s roads are chaotic, so exercise great care when crossing the road and keep a close eye on the very small ones.
中国的道路杂乱无章，过马路时要格外小心，并密切关注非常小的道路。

Health
健康

This will require a bit more planning than many destinations as China’s disparities – altitude and climate as well as economic – can throw in some unexpected surprises.
这需要比许多目的地更多的规划，因为中国的海拔、气候以及经济差异可能会带来一些意想不到的惊喜。
