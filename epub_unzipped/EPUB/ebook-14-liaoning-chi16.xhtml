Why Go?
为什么要去？

Liaoning (辽宁, Liáoníng) may be the smallest province in Dongbei, but it’s a little marvel, with a long, winding coastline and some fine historical cities.
辽宁（辽宁 ，Liáoníng）可能是东北最小的省份，但它有点奇迹，拥有漫长而蜿蜒的海岸线和一些精美的历史名城。

Hanging out over the Yellow Sea is Dalian, an attractive and relatively cosmopolitan former port turned resort city. Russian and Japanese forces clashed near here at Lushun in the early 20th century, but these days the warmer months see an influx of international tourists (many from Russian and Japan) for a rocking beer festival, affordable luxury hotels and perhaps China’s finest stretch of golden-sand beaches.
大连坐落在黄海之上，是一个迷人且相对国际化的前港口，现已改建为度假城市。20世纪初，俄罗斯和日本军队在旅顺附近发生冲突，但如今温暖的月份，国际游客（其中许多来自俄罗斯和日本）涌入，参加摇滚啤酒节、价格实惠的豪华酒店，也许还有中国最好的金色沙滩。

In Dandong, regional tensions of recent history are causing some spine-tingling at the border with North Korea. The Yalu River here brings you within glimpsing distance of the hermit kingdom from a boat deck or halfway across a bridge. Nearby, ancient history buffs can get their Great Wall fix away from the crowds further west.
在丹东，近代历史上的地区紧张局势正在引起与朝鲜边境的脊背发麻。这里的鸭绿江让您从船甲板或桥的一半处瞥见隐士王国。在附近，古代历史爱好者可以远离更西边的人群，修复他们的长城。

History
历史

The region formerly known as Manchuria, including the provinces of Liaoning, Jilin and Heilongjiang, plus parts of Inner Mongolia, is now called Dongbei (the Northeast).
以前被称为满洲的地区，包括辽宁省、吉林省和黑龙江省，以及内蒙古的部分地区，现在被称为东北。

The Manchurian warlords of this northern territory established the Qing dynasty, which ruled China from 1644 to 1911. From the late 1800s to the end of WWII, when western powers were busy carving up pieces of China for themselves, Manchuria was occupied alternately by the Russians and the Japanese.
这片北方领土的满洲军阀建立了清朝，清朝于 1644 年至 1911 年统治中国。从 1800 年代末到二战结束，当西方列强忙于为自己瓜分中国的碎片时，满洲被俄国人和日本人交替占领。

Today there is a push to transform the flagging steel industry carried over from the 1950s into a tech hub. Despite the province’s recent revival, a number of Communist Party officials were sanctioned for exaggerating the strength of Liaoning’s economy by up to 20%.
如今，人们正在推动将 1950 年代遗留下来的低迷钢铁业转变为技术中心。尽管该省最近复苏，但一些共产党官员因夸大辽宁经济实力高达20%而受到制裁。

Language

辽宁人几乎每个人都说标准普通话，尽管口音明显。在丹东和靠近朝鲜边境的地区，经常听到韩语。

Surrounded by the Yellow Sea, Dalian (Dàlián) is one of China’s most cosmopolitan cities. Its temperate climate, clean air and early-20th-century architecture alone attract the attention of international travellers from the region. But the second-tier city’s tree-lined streets and impressive, swimmable seaside beaches, flanked by an undulating walkway, combine to create a seductive effect on China-hardened travellers and first-time visitors on three-day visa-free stopovers.
大连（Dàlián）四面环黄海，是中国最国际化的城市之一。仅其温和的气候、清新的空气和 20 世纪初的建筑就吸引了该地区国际游客的注意。但这座二线城市绿树成荫的街道和令人印象深刻的、适合游泳的海滨海滩，两侧是起伏的人行道，共同对中国顽固的旅行者和为期三天的免签证中途停留的首次游客产生了诱人的效果。

With a decent bar and seafood scene – and status as a Special Economic Zone to attract foreign investment – Dalian is a fine place to soak up a few days. But after lazing on the beaches and strolling along the southwest coastline, pay a visit to the historic port town of Lushun. The old battlefields and cemeteries offer a rare first-hand glimpse into some of the north’s most turbulent days.
大连拥有不错的酒吧和海鲜场所，以及吸引外国投资的经济特区地位，是享受几天的好去处。但在海滩上慵懒地漫步并沿着西南海岸线漫步之后，您可以参观历史悠久的港口城市旅顺。古老的战场和墓地提供了难得的第一手资料，让我们一睹北方一些最动荡的日子。

Fisherman’s WharfVILLAGE
渔人码头村

(map; 渔人码头, Yúrén Mǎtóu; 66 Binhai Donglu, 滨海东路66号) F

One of the wonders of modern China is its ability to rework certain western aesthetics into a recognisable, and eerily satisfying, contemporary kitsch. Fishermen’s Wharf is a seaside community built in the style of an American east-coast village from the early 20th century. An afternoon in a wine shop, snapping photos of your surreal pan-cultural existence, can make your holiday. Take time to check out the perfect replica of the 1853 German Bremen Port Lighthouse, built with bricks from razed local villages.
现代中国的奇迹之一是它能够将某些西方美学重新加工成一种可识别的、令人毛骨悚然的满足感的当代媚俗。渔人码头是一个以 20 世纪初美国东海岸村庄风格建造的海滨社区。在葡萄酒商店度过一个下午，拍摄您超现实的泛文化生活的照片，可以让您度过愉快的假期。花点时间看看 1853 年德国不来梅港灯塔的完美复制品，它是用被夷为平地的当地村庄的砖块建造的。

Jinshi TanBEACH
金石潭海滩

(金石滩, Golden Pebble Beach) F
（ 金石滩 ，金卵石滩） F

The coast around Jinshi Tan, 50km northeast of the city, has been turned into a domestic tourism mecca with a number of theme parks and rock formations commanding inflated entrance fees. The long pebbly beach itself is free and quite pretty, set in a wide bay with distant headlands. For those who prefer their water chlorinated, there’s a beautiful new public swimming pool (¥5, open 6am to 8pm) beside the sand.
位于城市东北 50 公里处的金石潭周围的海岸已成为国内旅游圣地，拥有许多主题公园和岩层，门票价格高昂。长长的鹅卵石海滩本身是自由的，而且非常漂亮，坐落在一个宽阔的海湾中，有远处的岬角。对于那些喜欢氯化水的人来说，沙滩旁边有一个美丽的新公共游泳池 （5 日元，早上 6 点至晚上 8 点开放）。

To get here take the light rail, known by the locals as Line 3 (轻轨三号线, Qīngguǐ Sānhàoxiàn), from the depot on the east side of Victory Sq, behind the Dalian Train Station (¥8, 50 minutes) to its terminus ‘Jin Shi Tan’. The ticket machine is in Chinese only, but just select the most expensive ticket, then the number of tickets and insert your money.
要到达这里，从胜利广场东侧的车站（8 日元，50 分钟）到达其终点站“金石潭”，当地人称之为 3 号线（ 轻轨三号线 ）。售票机只有中文，但只需选择最贵的票，然后选择票数并投入您的钱。

From the beach station it’s a 10-minute walk to the beach, or catch a hop-on/hop-off tourist shuttle bus (¥20), which winds round the coast first before dropping you off at the beach. Tickets for the shuttle bus are available from the visitor centre (open 8.30am to 5pm), to the right of the train station as you exit, which sometimes has English-speaking staff if you need help.
从海滩站步行 10 分钟即可到达海滩，或乘坐随上随下旅游班车（20 日元），先绕海岸一圈，然后在海滩下车。穿梭巴士的车票可在游客中心（上午 8：30 至下午 5 点开放）购买，该中心位于您离开火车站右侧，如果您需要帮助，有时会说英语的工作人员。

(map; 傅家庄海滩, Fùjiāzhuāng Hǎitān) F
（ 地图 ; 傅家庄海滩 ，Fùjiāzhuāng Hǎitān） F

At the height of summer, you’ll see bathers 20 deep at this pretty pebble beach, which often features in crazy crowds in China photos. It’s not always like that though – even in spring you can enjoy clean, clear water and plenty of space in the adjacent park. Junks float just offshore in a deep bay, small broken islands dot the horizon, and loads of families come here for no other reason than to have fun.
盛夏时节，您会在这片美丽的鹅卵石海滩上看到 20 深的游泳者，这里经常出现在中国照片中的疯狂人群中。但情况并非总是如此——即使在春天，您也可以在邻近的公园享受干净、清澈的水和充足的空间。帆船漂浮在近海的深湾中，破碎的小岛点缀在地平线上，许多家庭来到这里的原因无他，只是为了玩得开心。

Bus 5 leaves from Jiefang Lu (¥1, 20 to 30 minutes) and drops you off across from the beach.
5路公交车从解放路出发（1日元，20至30分钟），在海滩对面下车。

大连宾馆是一家建于 1914 年的庄严酒店，当时称为大连大和酒店，出现在电影《 末代皇帝》 中。

Dalian’s southwest coastline is the city’s most alluring natural destination. History
历史

Shenyang’s roots go back to 300 BC, when it was known as Hou City. By the 11th century it was a Mongol trading centre, before reaching its historical high point in the 17th century when it was the capital of the Manchzzu empire. With the Manchu conquest of Beijing in 1644, Shenyang became a secondary capital under the Manchu name of Mukden, and a centre of the ginseng trade.
沈阳的根源可以追溯到公元前300年，当时它被称为侯城。到 11 世纪，它已成为蒙古贸易中心，然后在 17 世纪达到历史顶峰，当时它是满洲祖帝国的首都。1644年满清征服北京，沈阳成为满族名号奉天的第二首都，也是人参贸易的中心。

Throughout its history Shenyang has often changed hands, dominated by warlords, the Japanese (1931), the Russians (1945), the Kuomintang (1946) and finally the Chinese Communist Party (CCP; 1948).
纵观其历史，沈阳经常易手，由军阀、日本人（1931 年）、俄罗斯人（1945 年）、国民党（1946 年）以及最后的中国共产党（CCP;1948 年）主导。

THE ‘MUKDEN INCIDENT’
“奉天事件”

By 1931 Japan was looking for a pretext to occupy Manchuria. The Japanese army took matters into its own hands by staging an explosion on the night of 18 September at a tiny section of a Japanese-owned railway outside Mukden, the present-day city of Shenyang. Almost immediately, the Japanese attacked a nearby Chinese army garrison and then occupied Shenyang the following night. Within five months, they controlled all of Manchuria and ruled the region until the end of WWII.
到 1931 年，日本正在寻找占领满洲的借口。9 月 18 日晚上，日军在奉天（今沈阳市）郊外的一条日资铁路的一小段发生爆炸，自行动手。几乎立即，日本人袭击了附近的中国军队驻地，然后在第二天晚上占领了沈阳。在五个月内，他们控制了整个满洲，并统治了该地区，直到二战结束。

Xingcheng (兴城, Xīngchéng) makes for a historian’s dream beach holiday. Local travellers are waking up to its potential – a booming swimwear industry has put it on the fashion map – but few foreigners have caught on to the unkempt charm of this Ming dynasty city replete with its entire outer wall.
兴城（兴城 ，Xīngchéng）是历史学家梦想中的海滩度假胜地。当地游客正在意识到它的潜力——蓬勃发展的泳装业使它登上了时尚版图——但很少有外国人能注意到这座充满整个外墙的明代城市蓬头垢面的魅力。

Adding to the contrast, Xingcheng also has the oldest surviving temple in all of northeastern China and some of the best seafood north of Beijing.
更令人反感的是，兴城还拥有中国东北地区现存最古老的寺庙和北京北部一些最好的海鲜。
