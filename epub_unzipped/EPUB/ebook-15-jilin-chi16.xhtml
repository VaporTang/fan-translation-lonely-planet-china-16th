Why Go?
为什么要去？

While Jilin (吉林, Jílín) may not make typical China itineraries, there is cause for inspiration beyond the province’s well-known rustbelt. For nature lovers, Jilin is an increasingly popular ski destination and boasts China’s largest nature reserve at Changbai Shan – the journey to Baihe from Yanji is one of Dongbei’s most bucolic. Inside the reserve, Heaven Lake, a stunning, deep-blue volcanic crater lake, is one of China’s most mesmerising natural wonders.
虽然吉林（吉林 ，Jílín）可能不是典型的中国行程，但除了该省著名的铁锈带之外，还有理由获得灵感。对于大自然爱好者来说，吉林是一个越来越受欢迎的滑雪胜地，在长白山拥有中国最大的自然保护区——从延吉到白河的旅程是东北最田园风光的山庄之一。保护区内的天堂湖是一个令人惊叹的深蓝色火山口湖，是中国最迷人的自然奇观之一。

Culture buffs also have grounds for inspiration. From the Japanese architecture of Changchun, once the capital of Manchukuo and the headquarters of the puppet emperor Puyi, travellers can move east to the ruins of an ancient Korean kingdom known as Koguryo. In fact, much of the far-eastern region comprises the little-known Korean Autonomous Prefecture, home to more than one million ethnic Koreans. Kimchi and cold noodles dominate the menu here and there’s an easy acceptance of outsiders.
文化爱好者也有灵感的依据。从曾经是满洲国首都和傀儡皇帝溥仪的总部的长春的日本建筑出发，游客可以向东前往被称为高句丽的古朝鲜王国的废墟。事实上，远东地区的大部分地区包括鲜为人知的朝鲜族自治州，这里居住着超过一百万朝鲜族。泡菜和冷面在这里的菜单上占主导地位，外人很容易接受。

History
历史

Korean kings once ruled parts of Jilin, and the discovery of important relics from the ancient Koguryo kingdom (37 BC–AD 668) in the small southeastern city of Ji’an has resulted in the area being designated a World Heritage Site by Unesco.
韩国国王曾经统治过吉林的部分地区，在东南小城吉安发现了古代高句丽王国（公元前 37 年至公元 668 年）的重要遗迹，导致该地区被联合国教科文组织指定为世界遗产。

The Japanese occupation of Manchuria in the early 1930s pushed Jilin to the world’s centre stage. Changchun became the capital of what the Japanese called Manchukuo, with Puyi (the last emperor of the Qing dynasty) given the role of figurehead of the puppet government. In 1944 the Russians wrested control of Jilin from the Japanese and, after stripping the area of its industrial base, handed the region back to Chinese control. For the next several years Jilin would pay a heavy price as one of the front lines in the civil war between the Kuomintang and the Chinese Communist Party (CCP).
1930年代初期，日本对满洲的占领将吉林推向了世界舞台的中心。长春成为日本人所说的满洲国的首都，溥仪（清朝末代皇帝）被赋予傀儡政府的傀儡角色。1944年，俄国人从日本人手中夺取了吉林的控制权，并在剥夺了该地区的工业基础后，将该地区交还给中国控制。在接下来的几年里，吉林作为国民党和中国共产党内战的前线之一，将付出沉重的代价。

Climate
气候

Jilin is bitterly cold during its long winter, with heavy snow, freezing winds and temperatures as low as -20°C (32° F). In contrast, summer is pleasantly warm, especially along the coastal east, but short. Rainfall is moderate.

Changbai Shan Nature Reserve 长白山
长白山长白山

Dominating the eastern fringes of Jilin, the 2100-sq-km Changbai Shan (Chángbái Shān, Ever-White Mountains), which also straddles the China–North Korea border, has long captured the imagination of the Chinese. It could be the thick green forest running to the horizon, or perhaps spellbinding Heaven Lake, where the evil guaiwu creature is said to lurk just beneath the surface. The reality of the lake is just as mystical, especially in summer when its frozen cover cracks to reveal an icy blue set inside an ocean-sized volcanic crater.
占地 2100 平方公里的长白山（Chángbái Sān，常白山脉）也横跨中朝边境，占据吉林东部边缘，长期以来一直吸引着中国人的想象力。它可能是延伸到地平线的茂密绿色森林，也可能是迷人的天堂湖，据说邪恶的刮乌生物潜伏在地表之下。该湖的现实同样神秘，尤其是在夏天，当其冰冻的覆盖物破裂时，露出海洋大小的火山口内的冰蓝色。

Koreans refer to the area as Mt Paekdu, or Paekdusan. North Korea claims that Kim Jong-il was born here (although he’s believed to have entered the world in Khabarovsk, Russia).
韩国人将该地区称为白头山或白头山。朝鲜声称金正日出生在这里（尽管据信他是在俄罗斯哈巴罗夫斯克出生的）。

Though you can visit most of the year, the best time to see the crater (and be assured the roads are open) is from June to early September.
虽然您可以在一年中的大部分时间参观，但观看火山口的最佳时间（并确保道路畅通）是 6 月至 9 月初。

About 15km from the local airport, Wanda Changbai Shan International Resort (万达长白山国际度假区, Wàndá Chángbái Shān Guójì Dùjià Qū; %0400 098 7666; 455 Baiyun Lu, 白云路455号) has 20 runs over two mountains with some decent hiking in summer. The ski runs are unlikely to challenge more experienced alpinists, but the set-up is world class and the crowds are often far smaller than you might expect.
万达长白山国际度假区 （Wàndá Chángbái Shān Guójì Dùjià Qū; % 0400 098 7666;白云路 455 号 ） 有 20 条雪道，翻越两座山，夏季有一些不错的徒步旅行。滑雪道不太可能挑战更有经验的登山者，但设置是世界一流的，人群通常比您想象的要少得多。

The gateway town for the northern slope is Baihe and for the western slope it is Songjianghe; accommodation is widely available at both. It is possible to take buses or trains here from Changchung, Yanji, Tonghua and Shenyang.
北坡的门户镇是白河，西坡是松江河;两者都有广泛的住宿。可以从长中、延吉、通化和沈阳乘坐公共汽车或火车到这里。

Changbai Shan Airport, halfway between the reserve and Songjianghe, has flights to/from Shanghai, Changchun and Beijing.
长白山机场位于保护区和松江河之间，有往返上海、长春和北京的航班。

Heaven LakeLAKE
天堂湖 LAKE

(天池, Tiān Chí)
（ 天池 ， Tiān Chí）

The jewel of the area, this heavenly blue lake seems impossibly elevated by a ring of 16 mountainous peaks. The dormant crater lake, 13km in circumference, was formed around AD 969. A fixed route takes you around part of the crater lip with panoramic views of its glorious mirrored surface, at such an altitude (2194m) that it feels other-worldly. Legend has it that the lake is home to a large, but shy, beastie with the magical power to blur any photo taken of him.
这个天堂般的蓝色湖泊是该地区的明珠，似乎被 16 座山峰环状高耸得不可思议。休眠火山口湖周长 13 公里，形成于公元 969 年左右。一条固定的路线将带您绕过火山口边缘的一部分，欣赏其辉煌的镜面全景，海拔如此之高（2194米），让人感觉超凡脱俗。传说这个湖是一只体型庞大但害羞的野兽的家园，它拥有神奇的力量，可以模糊他拍摄的任何照片。

Small Heaven LakeLAKE
小天湖 LAKE

(小天池, Xiǎo Tiān Chí)
（ 小天池 ，Xiǎo Tiān Chí）

Grab a bus from the Changbai Waterfall to Small Heaven Lake. Nowhere near the size or majesty of the main crater lake, this is instead a placid lake (or large pond) worth circling. You could venture off into the surrounding forests for a short hike, but don’t get lost and be careful not to cross into North Korea! A boardwalk takes you along a fissure stream to the Green Deep Pool.
从长白瀑布乘坐巴士前往小天堂湖。与主火山口湖的大小或雄伟相去甚远，而是一个值得绕圈的平静湖泊（或大池塘）。您可以冒险进入周围的森林进行短途徒步旅行，但不要迷路，小心不要进入朝鲜！木板路将带您沿着裂缝溪流到达绿色深池。
