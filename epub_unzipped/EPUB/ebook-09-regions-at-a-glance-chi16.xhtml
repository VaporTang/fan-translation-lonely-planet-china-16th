地区一览

The high-altitude, far west of China, including Tibet, Qinghai and west Sichuan, gradually and fitfully steps down to level out as it nears the prosperous and well-irrigated canal-town provinces of Jiangsu and Zhejiang, and the dazzling metropolis of Shanghai in the east. The lion’s share of scenic showstoppers and hiking territory resides in the mountainous interior of China, while in the mighty northwest, peaks and deserts meet in dramatic fashion. Minority culture is a speciality of the west and southwest, and of the remote border regions. Different cuisines range across the entire nation, from the hardy northeast to the warm jungles of the steamy southwest.
中国遥远的西部高海拔地区，包括西藏、青海和四川西部，随着它靠近繁荣和灌溉良好的运河小镇江苏和浙江，以及东部令人眼花缭乱的大都市上海，逐渐逐渐平坦。风景秀丽和徒步旅行的大部分地区都位于中国的山区内陆，而在雄伟的西北地区，山峰和沙漠以戏剧性的方式交汇。少数民族文化是西部和西南部以及偏远边境地区的特色。不同的美食遍布全国，从耐寒的东北部到闷热的西南部温暖的丛林。

Beijing
北京

Beijing’s imperial pedigree (and the Great Wall) assures it a rich vein of dynastic history, balanced by splendid seams of temple and hutong (narrow alleyway) architecture. Wining and dining is a further attraction, with the capital home to a resourceful restaurant scene.
北京的帝国血统（和长城）确保了它丰富的王朝历史脉络，与寺庙和衚衕 （狭窄的小巷）建筑的壮丽接缝相平衡。美酒佳肴是另一个吸引力，首都拥有足智多谋的餐厅。

地区一览

Tianjin & Hebei
天津和河北

Tianjin’s spruced-up foreign concession streetscapes echo stylish Shanghai, and some standout pagodas and temples can be found in Hebei, where the rural side of China – peppered with rustic village getaways – comes to the fore.
天津经过精心装饰的外国租界街景与时尚的上海相呼应，在河北可以找到一些引人注目的宝塔和寺庙，那里是中国乡村的一面——点缀着质朴的乡村度假胜地——脱颖而出。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Liaoning
辽宁

In history-rich Liaoning, imperial relics contend with the legacy of Russian and Japanese colonialism. The North Korean border at Dandong is a sobering contrast to the wild beer festival at Dalian.
在历史悠久的辽宁，皇家遗迹与俄罗斯和日本殖民主义的遗产作斗争。丹东的朝鲜边境与大连的野啤酒节形成了鲜明的对比。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Jilin
吉林

Boasting China’s largest nature reserve, and a top ski destination, Jilin exerts a pull on the nature lover. On the trail of the exotic? Head to Ji’an for the ruins of the ancient Korean Koguryo empire.
吉林拥有中国最大的自然保护区和顶级滑雪胜地，对大自然爱好者有着吸引力。在异国情调的踪迹上？前往吉安，参观古代韩国高句丽帝国的遗址。

地区一览

Heilongjiang
黑龙江

Fire and ice are the highlights in this province where volcanic explosions have left one of China’s most mesmerising landscapes, and the winter’s bitter climate provides the raw materials for a spectacular icesculpture festival.
火和冰是这个省份的亮点，火山爆发留下了中国最迷人的景观之一，而冬季严酷的气候为壮观的冰雕节提供了原材料。

地区一览

Shandong
山东

Shandong groans under the weight of its historical heavy hitters: Confucius’ home and tomb at Qufu, and sacred Tai Shan. Then there’s the home of Tsingtao beer, Qingdao, a breezy, laid-back, modern and sophisticated port city.
山东在其历史重量级人物的重压下呻吟：孔子的家和曲阜的陵墓，以及神圣的泰山。然后是青岛啤酒的故乡青岛，这是一座微风、悠闲、现代而精致的港口城市。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Jiangsu
江苏

Jiangsu’s awash with cute-as-pie canal towns – all reachable as day trips from neighbouring Shanghai. The provincial capital, Nanjing, has history in spades, with its fabulous Ming wall and epic past as former national capital.
江苏到处都是可爱的运河小镇——从邻近的上海一日游即可到达。省会南京拥有悠久的历史，拥有神话般的明朝城墙和作为前国家首都的史诗般的历史。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Shanghai
上海

Shanghai exudes a unique style unlike anywhere else in China. There’s plenty to do, from non-stop shopping and skyscraper-hopping to standout art, fantastic eats and touring the city’s elegant art deco heritage.
上海散发着不同于中国其他任何地方的独特风格。这里有很多事情可以做，从不间断的购物和摩天大楼跳跃到杰出的艺术、美味的美食和游览这座城市优雅的装饰艺术遗产。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Zhejiang
浙江

Hangzhou is one of China’s most appealing cities, while traditional charm abounds in the canal town of Wuzhen. Further south, the gorgeous villages of Xinye and Zhuge, and the island of Putuoshan, are stunning pastoral escapes.
杭州是中国最具吸引力的城市之一，而运河小镇乌镇则充满了传统魅力。再往南，美丽的新野村庄和诸葛村庄，以及普陀山岛，都是令人惊叹的田园度假胜地。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Fujian
福建

Fujian is Hakka heartland and home to the intriguing tǔlóu – massive packed stone, wood and mud structures once housing hundreds of families. Gulang Yu, a tiny, hilly island off Xiamen, is decorated with crumbling colonial villas.
福建是客家的中心地带，也是迷人的 tǔlóu 的故乡——巨大的石头、木头和泥浆结构，曾经居住着数百个家庭。鼓浪屿是厦门附近的一个丘陵小岛，装饰着摇摇欲坠的殖民时期别墅。

地区一览

Shanxi
山西

Repository to the superlative Buddhist grottoes at Yungang, Shanxi also brings you the beautiful Buddhist mountain, Wutai Shan; the intact walled city of Pingyao; the rebuilt city fortifications of Datong; and time-worn sections of the Great Wall.
山西云港最高级的佛教石窟的宝库还为您带来了美丽的佛教山——五台山;完整的平遥城寨;重建的大同城市防御工事;以及长城的陈旧部分。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Shaanxi
陕西

Archaeological sites lie scattered across the plains surrounding Shaanxi’s magnificent walled capital, Xi’an, where museums galore await. Blow off all that ancient dust with a trip to Hua Shan, one of China’s five holy Taoist peaks.
考古遗址散布在陕西宏伟的围墙首都习安周围的平原上，那里有大量的博物馆等待着您。前往中国五大道教圣峰之一的华山，吹去所有古老的灰尘。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Anhui
安徽

The charming World Heritage–listed Hui villages of Hongcun and Xidi are some of China’s best-preserved settlements. But let’s not forget that astonishing mountain, Huangshan, where soaring granite peaks and ethereal mists have inspired legions of poets and painters.
迷人的世界遗产名录回族村庄宏村和西底村是中国保存最完好的聚落之一。但我们不要忘记那座令人惊叹的山峰——黄山，那里高耸的花岗岩山峰和空灵的薄雾激发了大批诗人和画家的灵感。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Henan
河南

Henan’s overture of dynastic antiquity is balanced by some excellent mountain escapes and the quirky allure of Nanjiecun, China’s last Maoist collective. The province’s wǔshù (martial arts) credentials come no better: the Shaolin Temple is here.
河南对王朝古代的序曲与一些出色的山间度假胜地和中国最后一个毛派集体南界村的古怪魅力相平衡。该省的武术证书也好不到哪里去：少林寺就在这里。

地区一览

Hubei
湖北

Slashed by the mighty Yangzi River, history-rich Hubei is one of the gateways to the Three Gorges, but Taoist martial artists may find themselves drawn to Wudang Shan, home of taichi and scenic views.
湖北被浩瀚的扬子江所横扫，历史悠久，是通往三峡的门户之一，但道教武术家可能会发现自己被武当山所吸引，武当山是太极拳和风景的故乡。

地区一览

Jiangxi
江西

Communists herald it as the mythic starting point of the Long March, but it’s the spectacular mountain scenery and hiking trails past preserved villages and terraced fields that should pop Jiangxi into your travel plans.
共产党人将其誉为长征的神话起点，但壮观的山景和经过保存完好的村庄和梯田的远足小径应该将江西纳入您的旅行计划。

Hunan
湖南

Home to one of China’s most enjoyable ancient towns, Fenghuang (beautifully illuminated come nightfall), as well as the sacred mountain of Heng Shan, the other-worldly peaks of Zhangjiajie, and secluded Miao and Dong villages.
这里有中国最令人愉快的古镇之一凤凰（夜幕降临时灯火通明）、衡山神山、张家界超凡脱俗的山峰以及僻静的苗族和侗族村庄。

Hong Kong
香港

This culinary capital offers the best of China and beyond, while a seductive mix of vintage and cutting-edge fashion attracts armies of shoppers. Meanwhile, leafy mountains, shimmering waters, skyscrapers and tenements make an unlikely but poetic match.
这座美食之都提供中国及其他地区的精华，而复古和前卫时尚的诱人组合则吸引了大批购物者。与此同时，绿树成荫的山脉、波光粼粼的海水、摩天大楼和公寓构成了一种不太可能但充满诗意的搭配。

Macau
澳门

Marrying flavours from five continents, Macanese cooking is as unique as the cityscape, where Taoist temples meet baroque churches on cobbled streets with Chinese names. It’s also a billionaires playground where casino-resorts and other luxuries vie for space.
土生葡人烹饪融合了五大洲的风味，与城市景观一样独特，道教寺庙与带有中国名字的鹅卵石街道上的巴洛克式教堂相遇。它也是亿万富翁的游乐场，赌场度假村和其他奢侈品在这里争夺空间。

地区一览

Guangdong
广东

A strong gastronomic culture offers travellers the chance to savour world-renowned Cantonese cuisine. Guangdong’s seafaring temperament has brought the region diverse, exotic architectural styles, including the World Heritage–listed watchtowers of Kaiping.
浓厚的美食文化为旅客提供了品尝世界知名粤菜的机会。广东的航海气质为该地区带来了多样化的异国情调的建筑风格，包括列入世界遗产名录的开平瞭望塔。

Hainan
海南

When it comes to golden-sand beaches and warm clear waters, this tropical island doesn’t disappoint. An ideal cycling destination, Hainan attracts in-the-know adventurers with its good roads, balmy winters, varied landscape and surfing spots.
说到金色的沙滩和温暖清澈的海水，这个热带岛屿不会让人失望。海南是一个理想的自行车目的地，以其良好的道路、温暖的冬季、多样的景观和冲浪点吸引着知情的冒险家。

Guangxi
广西

Famed for the dreamy karst landscapes of Yangshuo, Guangxi also offers lush green valleys, charming folksy villages and countless walking, cycling and rafting opportunities, as well as Weizhou Island, a short trip from sleepy Beihai.
广西以阳朔梦幻般的喀斯特地貌而闻名，还拥有郁郁葱葱的绿色山谷、迷人的民间村庄和无数的步行、骑自行车和漂流机会，以及距离沉睡的北海仅几步之遥的涠洲岛。

地区一览

Guizhou
贵州

With more folk festivals than anywhere else in China, you can celebrate here with locals year-round. Throw in an abundance of waterfalls for nature lovers, lovely Zhenyuan and countless wooden Dong and Miao minority villages.
这里的民俗节日比中国其他任何地方都多，您可以在这里与当地人一起庆祝一年四季。为大自然爱好者提供丰富的瀑布、可爱的镇远和无数木制的侗族和苗族少数民族村庄。

Yunnan
云南

Yunnan has it all: towering Himalayan mountains, tropical jungle, sublime rice terraces and over half of China’s minority groups, plus historic, little-visited villages such as Nuodeng and Heijing, gorgeous and ancient Lijiang, fantastic trekking and great food.
云南拥有一切：高耸的喜马拉雅山脉、热带丛林、壮丽的水稻梯田和中国一半以上的少数民族，以及诺登和黑井等历史悠久、鲜为人知的村庄、华丽而古老的丽江、美妙的徒步旅行和美味的食物。

Sichuan
四川

Stay in central or southern Sichuan for steamy bamboo forests and Ming dynasty villages. Head north for stunning lakes and alpine scenery. Venture west for remote Tibetan grasslands and towering peaks. Spicy Sichuan cuisine is an abundant pleasure.
住在四川中部或南部，享受热气腾腾的竹林和明代村庄。向北行驶，欣赏迷人的湖泊和高山风光。向西冒险，欣赏偏远的藏族草原和高耸的山峰。麻辣的川菜是一种丰富的乐趣。

地区一览

Chongqing
重庆

A unique city with a unique location, hilly Chongqing hugs cliffs overlooking the Yangzi, bursts with old-China energy, offers some fascinating day trips to historic villages and is home to Chongqing hotpot – the spiciest dish on the planet.
重庆是一座独特的城市，地理位置得天独厚，山丘拥抱悬崖，俯瞰扬子江，充满旧中国的活力，提供前往历史村庄的迷人一日游，并且是重庆火锅的故乡——地球上最辣的菜肴。

Xinjiang
新疆

Bazaars, kebabs, camels and Uyghur culture are just a few of the things that hint at your arrival in Central Asia. Ancient Silk Road towns include Turpan, Kashgar and Hotan, while hikers gravitate to Kanas Lake and the Tian Shan range.
集市、烤肉串、骆驼和维吾尔文化只是暗示您抵达中亚的一小部分。古老的丝绸之路城镇包括吐鲁番、喀什和和丹，而徒步旅行者则被喀纳斯湖和天山山脉所吸引。

地区一览

Gansu
甘肃

Gansu is about diversity: colourful Tibetan regions in the southwest, desert dunes in the northwest and a rich accumulation of Silk Road culture through the middle. Think deserts, mountains, Buddhist temples, camels, yaks, pilgrims and nomads.
甘肃是关于多样性的：西南部是丰富多彩的藏区，西北部是沙漠沙丘，中间是丝绸之路文化的丰富积累。想想沙漠、山脉、佛教寺庙、骆驼、牦牛、朝圣者和游牧民族。

Ningxia
宁夏

In the designated homeland of the Muslim Hui, visit the great tombs of the Xixia, nomadic rock art and the enormous Buddhas of Xumi Shan. For camel trekking or sliding down sand dunes, head for the Tengger Desert.
在穆斯林回族的指定故乡，参观西夏的大墓、游牧岩画和须弥山的巨大佛像。如果想骑骆驼徒步旅行或滑下沙丘，请前往腾格里沙漠。

地区一览

Inner Mongolia
内蒙古

Ride a Mongolian horse alongside the Russian border, roam across verdant grasslands or sit down to a steaming Mongolian hotpot. Further-flung western Inner Mongolia is a stunning landscape of towering sand dunes, desert lakes and ancient ruins.
在俄罗斯边境骑蒙古马，漫步在青翠的草原上，或者坐下来吃一顿热气腾腾的蒙古火锅。内蒙古西部更远的地方是高耸的沙丘、沙漠湖泊和古代遗址的壮丽景观。

地区一览

Qinghai
青海

A land of monasteries, sublime mountain and grassland vistas and Tibetan culture, vast and remote Qinghai – way up on the Tibetan plateau – is for those who like their travel rough. Don’t expect a hot shower and a coffee every morning.
青海省拥有寺院、壮丽的山地和草原景观以及藏族文化，广阔而偏远的青海省位于青藏高原上，适合那些喜欢崎岖旅行的人。不要指望每天早上洗个热水澡和一杯咖啡。

Regions at a Glance
地区一览

Tibet
西藏

The ‘Roof of the World’ is a stunningly beautiful high plateau of turquoise lakes, desert valleys and Himalayan peaks, dotted with monasteries, yaks and sacred Buddhist sites. Tight and ever-changing travel regulations can easily derail travel plans.
“世界屋脊”是一个令人惊叹的美丽高原，由碧绿的湖泊、沙漠山谷和喜马拉雅山峰组成，点缀着寺院、牦牛和佛教圣地。严格且不断变化的旅行法规很容易破坏旅行计划。
