Why Go?
为什么要去？

Guangxi (广西, Guǎngxī) conjures up visions of cycling and bamboo-rafting upon shimmering river waters beneath the sublime karst peaks of Yangshuo and hiking between ethnic villages in the lofty Longji Rice Terraces. That’s not all though: you can take selfies in front of the dramatic Danxia landscape (a type of landform) at Tianmen Mountain and Bajiaozhai National Geopark, and get sprayed by the mighty waterfall of Detian or splashed by live seafood in Beihai’s Vietnamese quarter.
广西（ 广西 ，Guǎngxī）让人联想到在阳朔壮丽的喀斯特山峰下在波光粼粼的河水中骑自行车和竹筏漂流，在高耸的龙脊梯田的民族村庄之间徒步旅行。但这还不是全部：您可以在天门山和八角寨国家地质公园壮观的丹霞景观（一种地貌）前自拍，在德天雄伟的瀑布中被喷洒，或者在北海的越南区被活海鲜溅到。

What’s more, you’ll be contemplating the 2000-year-old Huashan cliff murals from a boat and comparing the poetry of the Dong villages at Chengyang to the beauty of Japan’s Kyoto.
更重要的是，您将在船上凝视拥有 2000 年历史的华山悬崖壁画，并将城阳侗族村庄的诗意与日本京都的美景进行比较。

After you’ve had your fill of wonders above ground, you’ll be plunging into the subterranean forests of Leye and soaking your feet in the underground streams of Tongling Grand Canyon. Doing it all in reverse also makes sense, for Yangshuo is the perfect conclusion to any expedition.
在您饱览了地上的奇观后，您将跳入乐业的地下森林，将双脚浸泡在铜陵大峡谷的地下溪流中。反向进行这一切也是有道理的，因为阳朔是任何探险的完美结局。

History
历史

In 214 BC a Qin dynasty army attempted to assimilate the Zhuang people, living in what is now called Guangxi, into their newly formed Chinese empire. But while the eastern and southern parts submitted, the western extremes remained largely controlled by hill-tribe chieftains.
公元前 214 年，秦朝军队试图将生活在现在广西的壮族同化为他们新成立的中华帝国。但是，虽然东部和南部地区屈服了，但西部极端仍然主要由山地部落酋长控制。

Major tribal uprisings occurred in the 19th century, the most significant being the Taiping Rebellion (1850–64), which became one of the bloodiest civil wars in human history, resulting in 20 million deaths.
19世纪发生了重大的部落起义，其中最显著的是太平天国起义（1850-64年），它成为人类历史上最血腥的内战之一，造成2000万人死亡。

Communist bases were set up in Guangxi following the 1929 Baise Uprising led by Deng Xiaoping, although they were eventually destroyed by Kuomintang forces. Much of Guangxi fell briefly under Japanese rule following highly destructive WWII invasions.
1929年邓小平领导的百色起义后，共产党在广西建立了根据地，但最终被国民党军队摧毁。在二战中破坏性极强的入侵后，广西大部分地区短暂地沦为日本统治。

Today the Zhuang, China’s largest minority group, makes up 32% of Guangxi’s population, which led to the province being reconstituted in 1955 as the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. As well as Zhuang, Miao and Yao, Guangxi is home to significant numbers of Dong people.
如今，中国最大的少数民族壮族占广西人口的 32%，这导致该省于 1955 年改组为广西壮族自治区。除了壮族、苗族和瑶族之外，广西还是大量侗族的家园。

Language
语言

Travellers with a grasp of Mandarin (Pǔtōng-huà) will have few problems navigating Guangxi’s vast sea of languages. Cantonese (Guǎngdōnghuà), known as Báihuà in these parts, is the language of choice in Nanning, Pingxiang and Daxin, but most people also understand Mandarin. Visitors will also hear a number of minority languages being spoken, such as Zhuang, Dong and Yao.
掌握普通话（Pǔtōng-huà）的旅行者在广西广阔的语言海洋中航行时几乎没有问题。粤语（Guǎngdōnghuà），在这些地区被称为白华，是南宁、萍乡和大新的首选语言，但大多数人也懂普通话。参观者还将听到许多少数民族语言，例如壮族语、侗族和瑶族。

Guilin 桂林
Guilin 桂林

%0773 / Pop 5.19 million
% 0773 / 人口 519 万

Guangxi’s second-largest city, Guilin (Guìlín) has the hallmarks of most Chinese megapolises, but it feels much more relaxed given its spectacular setting among the jaggedpeak limestone karsts that surround it. It was China’s first city to develop tourism after 1949, and for decades, children’s textbooks proclaimed ‘Guilin’s landscape is the best under heaven’ (桂林山水甲天下). It was the darling of Chinese politicians, the star city proudly presented to visiting dignitaries. Today Guilin’s natural endowments still amaze, yet, thanks to imperfect urban planning, there is a pervasive feeling that the city is past its prime.
广西第二大城市桂林（桂林）拥有大多数中国大都市的特征，但由于其周围锯齿状石灰岩喀斯特地貌的壮观环境，它给人的感觉要轻松得多。它是 1949 年后中国第一个发展旅游业的城市，几十年来，儿童教科书宣称“桂林山水甲天下 ”。它是中国政客的宠儿，这座星城自豪地呈现给来访的政要。今天，桂林的自然天赋仍然令人惊叹，然而，由于城市规划不完善，人们普遍认为这座城市已经过了鼎盛时期。

Guilin serves as a convenient base to plan trips to the rest of the province. It’s clean, modern and offers plenty of greenery among its city lakes and the majestic Li River. Star Wars fans may recognise the surrounding karst peaks from Revenge of Sith, which was used as the set location for the Wookiee planet, Kashyyyk.
桂林是计划前往该省其他地区旅行的便利基地。它干净、现代，在城市湖泊和雄伟的漓江之间拥有充足的绿色植物。《星球大战》的粉丝可能会认出《 西斯的复仇》 中周围的喀斯特山峰，这里曾被用作伍基星球卡希克的拍摄地。

Jiangtouzhou Ancient Town 江头洲古城
江头洲古城

The 1000-year-old village of Jiangtouzhou (Jiāngtóuzhōu Gǔchéng; ¥20) is tucked away among farmland 32km north of Guilin. The mostly abandoned village has layers of old-world charm, with its labyrinth of cobblestone alleyways lined with stone houses dating from the Ming and Qing dynasties, and where blocks of tofu are laid out to set in the courtyards. Its residents are descendants of the philosopher Zhou Dunyi (周敦颐), who is famed for his essay on virtue, ‘Love of the Water-Lily’.
拥有 1000 年历史的江头州村 （Jiāngtóuzhōu Gǔchéng;¥20） 隐藏在桂林以北 32 公里的农田中。这个大部分废弃的村庄拥有层层旧世界的魅力，迷宫般的鹅卵石小巷两旁是明清时期的石屋，庭院里摆放着豆腐块。这里的居民是哲学家周敦颐的后裔，周敦颐以论美德的散文《睡莲之恋》而闻名。

这朵花是整个村庄和祠堂内的装饰图案。从村庄对面步行不远，就是一个风景如画的荷花花园，这里是绝佳的拍照地点，背景是喀斯特山峰若隐若现;入场费为 10 日元，但从外面看到的景色同样美丽。

There are multiple viewing spots along hiking trails in the park, including a U-shaped deck with a transparent floor – a 1½- hour return walk or you can take the cable car.

This part of Guangxi is famous for its breathtaking vistas of terraced paddy fields cascading in swirls down into a valley. For hundreds of years, the paddy fields of Longji Rice Terraces (Lóngjǐ Tītián) remained unknown to travellers, then everything changed in the 1990s when a photographer named Li Yashi (李亚石) moved here. His images of the scenery amazed the world and put Longji (literally ‘Dragon’s Back’) firmly on the tourist trail.
广西的这一地区以其梯田的壮丽景色而闻名，梯田在漩涡中倾泻而下，进入山谷。数百年来，龙脊梯田（Lóngjǐ Tītitián）的水田一直不为旅行者所知，然后在 1990 年代，当一位名叫李亚石 （Li Yashi） 的摄影师搬到这里时，一切都发生了变化。他对风景的描绘惊艳了世界，并将龙脊（字面意思是“龙背”）牢牢地放在了旅游路线上。

You’ll find the most spectacular views around the villages of Ping’an (平安, Píng’ān), a Zhuang settlement; Dazhai (大寨, Dàzhài), a mesmerising Yao village; and Tiantouzhai (田头寨, Tiántóuzhài), which sits slightly further above Dazhai.
您会在壮族定居点平安 （ 平安 ，Píng'ān）村庄周围找到最壮观的景色; 大寨 （ 大寨 ，Dàzhài），一个迷人的瑶族村庄;田头寨 （ 田头寨 ，Tiántóuzhài），位于大寨上方稍远。

As hiking is a way of life here, bring a day pack and leave your luggage in Guilin or at the main ticket office in Dazhai or Ping’an (storage per day ¥15). Otherwise, villagers will carry your bags for ¥50 apiece.

A drum tower resembles a flamboyant, multi-eaved pagoda plonked on a rectangular pavilion; many are found in Dong villages in the region. The taller ones are built entirely of cedar. CHOW DOWN IN OVERSEAS TOWN
在海外小镇吃东西

Beihai is the place to feast on fresh seafood – it’s excellent and abundant. Yet at the most visible seafood restaurants, especially those near Silver Beach, customers pay through the nose for a plate of squid.
北海是享用新鲜海鲜的地方——它美味而丰富。然而，在最显眼的海鲜餐厅，尤其是银滩附近的海鲜餐厅，顾客要花鼻子买一盘鱿鱼。

This is because cab and pedicab drivers get a cut for bringing customers to these places – up to 50% of the bill! With that kind of incentive, some drivers will work hard to lure you to the spots that pay them. Insist on going elsewhere and they may feign ignorance of the location, or take you to their pet eatery and pretend it’s the one you’re after.
这是因为出租车和三轮车司机因将客户带到这些地方而获得分成——高达账单的 50%！有了这种激励，一些司机会努力引诱你到付钱给他们的地方。坚持去其他地方，他们可能会假装不知道这个位置，或者带你去他们的宠物餐馆，假装这是你要找的那家。

But there are exceptions to the rule. Seafood restaurants that don’t overcharge can be found in Overseas Town (侨港镇, Qiáogǎng Zhèn), 4km away from Silver Beach. OT was established in 1979 to settle Vietnamese Chinese refugees who had arrived near the shores of Beihai. Sinkholes (天坑. tiānkēng), literally ‘sky pits’ in Chinese, are depressions in the land caused by the collapse of the surface layer. This happens when bedrock made of a soluble substance such as limestone is eroded by underground water, forming caves that perforate the interior rock, which may eventually collapse inwards. Some sinkholes have openings into the caves below or to the underground rivers that have eroded its interior. Some are also carpeted by primeval forests that have sought purchase upon them.
天坑（天坑 .tiānkēng），中文直译为“天坑”，是表层塌陷造成的土地凹陷。当由石灰岩等可溶性物质制成的基岩被地下水侵蚀，形成洞穴，使内部岩石穿孔，最终可能向内坍塌时，就会发生这种情况。一些天坑有通往下方洞穴或侵蚀其内部的地下河的开口。有些还被原始森林覆盖，这些森林寻求购买它们。
