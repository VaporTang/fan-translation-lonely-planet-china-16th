Why Go?
为什么要去？

Mongolia. The name alone stirs up visions of nomadic herders, thundering horses and, of course, the warrior-emperor Genghis Khaan.
蒙古。光是这个名字就让人联想到游牧民、雷鸣般的马匹，当然还有武士皇帝成吉思汗。

Travellers heading north of the Great Wall might half expect to see Mongol hordes galloping through the vast grasslands. The reality is rather different: the 21st-century Chinese province of Inner Mongolia (内蒙古, Nèi Měnggǔ) is a wholly separate place from the neighbouring country of Mongolia itself. The more-visited south of the province is industrialised, prosperous and very much within the realm of China’s modern economic miracle.
前往长城以北的旅行者可能会期待看到蒙古人大军在广阔的草原上驰骋。现实却大不相同：21 世纪的中国内蒙古（ 内蒙古，Nèi Měnggǔ）与邻国蒙古本身完全不同。该省游客较多的南部地区工业化、繁荣，在很大程度上属于中国现代经济奇迹的范畴。

Having said that, Inner Mongolia is more than nine times the size of England and the Mongolia of your dreams can be found off the tourist route, amid the shimmering sand dunes of the Badain Jaran Desert or the vast grasslands in the north. Some effort is required to reach these areas, but the spectacular scenery can make it an unforgettable journey.
话虽如此，内蒙古的面积是英格兰的九倍多，而您梦想中的蒙古可以在旅游路线之外找到，在巴丹吉兰沙漠波光粼粼的沙丘或北部广阔的草原中。到达这些地区需要付出一些努力，但壮观的风景可以使其成为一次难忘的旅程。

History
历史

The nomadic tribes of the northern steppes have always been at odds with the agrarian Han Chinese, so much so that the Great Wall was built to keep them out. But it acted more like a speed bump than an actual barrier to the Mongol hordes.
北方草原的游牧部落一直与农耕汉族不和，以至于修建长城将他们拒之门外。但它更像是一个减速带，而不是蒙古部落的实际屏障。

Genghis Khan and grandson Kublai rumbled through in the 13th century, and after conquering southern China in 1279 Kublai Khan became the first emperor of the Yuan dynasty. But by the end of the 14th century the Mongol empire had collapsed, and the Mongols again became a collection of disorganised roaming tribes. It was not until the 18th century that the Qing emperors finally gained full control of the region.
成吉思汗和孙子忽必烈在13世纪隆隆作响，1279年征服中国南方后，忽必烈成为元朝的第一位皇帝。但到了14世纪末，蒙古帝国已经崩溃，蒙古人再次成为杂乱无章的漫游部落的集合体。直到18世纪，清朝皇帝才终于完全控制了该地区。

A divide-and-conquer policy by the Qing led to the creation of an ‘Inner’ and ‘Outer’ Mongolia. The Qing opened up Inner Mongolia to Han farmers, and waves of migrants came to cultivate the land. Outer Mongolia was spared this policy and, with backing from the USSR, it gained full independence in 1921.
清朝的分而治之政策导致了“内蒙古”和“外蒙古”的建立。清朝向汉族农民开放内蒙古，一波又一波的移民前来耕种土地。外蒙古没有受到这一政策的影响，在苏联的支持下，它于 1921 年获得完全独立。

Now Mongolians make up only 15% of Inner Mongolia’s population. Most of the other 85% are Han Chinese, with a smattering of Hui, Manchu, Daur and Ewenki.
现在蒙古人只占内蒙古人口的15%。其他 85% 的大多数是汉族，还有少量回族、满族、达斡尔族和鄂温基族。

Inner Mongolia’s economy boomed in recent years thanks to extensive mining of both coal and rare earth minerals. That growth came at great environmental cost. The mines swallowed up pastureland at alarming rates and desertification has been the root cause of the dust storms that envelop Beijing each spring. Only the far north of the region, where the economy is largely based on cattle ranching and tourism, has escaped heavy industrialisation.
近年来，内蒙古的经济蓬勃发展，这要归功于煤炭和稀土矿物的广泛开采。这种增长是以巨大的环境代价代价的。矿山以惊人的速度吞噬了牧场，荒漠化是每年春天笼罩北京的沙尘暴的根本原因。只有该地区最北部的经济主要以养牛场和旅游业为基础，摆脱了重工业化。

For a look at how Inner Mongolia fared during the Cultural Revolution and how traditional ways of life were impacted by politics and ideology, find a copy of the semiautobiographical novel Wolf Totem (2009) by Jiang Rong.
要了解内蒙古在文化大革命期间的表现以及传统生活方式如何受到政治和意识形态的影响，请查看江蓉的半自传体小说《 狼图腾 》（2009）。

Climate
气候

Siberian blizzards and cold air currents rake the Mongolian plains from November to March. June to August brings pleasant temperatures, but the west is scorching hot during the day.
11月至3月，西伯利亚暴风雪和冷气流席卷蒙古平原。6 月至 8 月气温宜人，但西部白天炎热。

The best time to visit is between July and September, particularly to see the grasslands, which are green only in summer. Make sure you bring warm, windproof clothing, as even in midsummer it’s often windy, and evening temperatures can dip to 10°C or below.
最佳游览时间是 7 月至 9 月，尤其是看到只有夏季才绿意盎然的草原。请务必携带保暖、防风的衣服，因为即使在盛夏也经常刮风，晚上气温可能会降至 10°C 或以下。

Language
语言

The Mongolian language is part of the Altaic linguistic family, which includes the Central Asian Turkic languages and the now defunct Manchurian. Although the vertical Mongolian script (written left to right) adorns street signs, almost everyone speaks standard Mandarin.
蒙古语是阿尔泰语系的一部分，该语系包括中亚突厥语系和现已不复存在的满洲语系。虽然路牌上装饰着竖直的蒙古文字（从左到右书写），但几乎每个人都会说标准普通话。

8Getting There & Away
8 到达那里和离开

Inner Mongolia borders Mongolia and Russia. There are border crossings at Erenhot (Mongolia) and Manzhouli (Russia), which are stopovers on the Trans-Mongolian and Trans-Manchurian Railways, respectively. To Mongolia, you can also catch a local train to Erenhot, cross the border and take another local train to Ulaanbaatar (with the appropriate visa). Possible international air connections include Hohhot to Ulaanbaatar or Haila’er to Ulaanbaatar.
内蒙古与蒙古和俄罗斯接壤。二连浩特（蒙古）和满洲里（俄罗斯）设有边境口岸，分别是横贯蒙古和横贯满洲铁路的中途停留地。前往蒙古，您还可以乘坐前往二连浩特的当地火车，越过边境，然后乘坐另一趟前往乌兰巴托的当地火车（持有适当的签证）。可能的国际航空连接包括呼和浩特至乌兰巴托或海拉尔至乌兰巴托。

Trains and long-distance buses reach Hohhot, Baotou and other large towns from neighbouring provinces.
火车和长途巴士从邻近省份到达呼和浩特、包头和其他大城镇。

Inner Mongolia is vast and stupendously long from east to west, so you may find yourself flying at least once, especially when accessing the northeast of the province and the far west. Otherwise you’ll be relying on a mixture of trains and long-distance buses. The far west of Inner Mongolia has airports at Ejina Qi, Alashan Youqi and Bayanhot, collapsing distances within the region; buses are another option for the far west (but there are no train lines).
内蒙古幅员辽阔，东西长得惊人，因此您可能会发现自己至少飞行一次，尤其是在进入该省东北部和最西部时。否则，您将依赖火车和长途巴士的混合。内蒙古最西端的额济纳齐、阿拉善尤奇和巴彦浩特设有机场，在该地区的距离遥远;巴士是远西部的另一种选择（但没有火车线路）。

Hohhot 呼和浩特
Hohhot 呼和浩特

%0471 / Pop 2.86 million
% 0471 / 人口 286 万

Founded by Altan Khan in the 16th century, the good-looking capital of Inner Mongolia is an increasingly prosperous city. Hohhot (known in Mandarin as 呼和浩特, Hūhéhàotè) means ‘Blue City’ in Mongolian, a reference to the arching blue skies over the grasslands. Streets are attractively tree-lined (although the roads are traffic-snarled) and there are some truly astonishing Tibetan Buddhist temples in town – more than enough to keep you busy for a day or two before heading to the hinterlands. Helpfully, most of the sights congregate in the same part of town, making sightseeing a doddle. Note that the cumbersome name of the city is often colloquially shortened to 呼市 (Hūshì).
内蒙古这座好看的首都由阿勒坦汗于 16 世纪建立，是一座日益繁华的城市。呼和浩特（普通话称为呼和浩特 Hūhéhàotè）在蒙古语中的意思是“蓝色之城”，指的是草原上拱形的蓝天。街道绿树成荫（尽管道路交通拥堵），镇上有一些真正令人惊叹的藏传佛教寺庙——足以让你在前往腹地之前忙碌一两天。有用的是，大多数景点都聚集在城镇的同一部分，使观光变得轻而易举。请注意，繁琐的城市名称通常通俗地缩写为呼市 （Hūshì）。

oDà ZhàoMONASTERY
o 大昭寺

(map Google map; 大召; Danan Jie, 大南街; admission ¥35, guide ¥90, joint ticket with Xílìtú Zhào ¥50; h8am-7pm)
（ 地图谷歌地图 ; 大召 ;大南街， 大南街 ;门票 35 日元，导游 90 日元，与西里图赵联票 50 日元; h 上午 8 点至晚上 7 点）

This spectacular Tibetan Buddhist temple is the oldest and largest temple in the city. Also called ‘Immeasurable Temple’ (无量寺, Wúliàng Sì) in Chinese, the complex was originally built in the 16th century and much enhanced in the following century. A very sacred place and a fascinating introduction to the mysterious ways of Tibetan Buddhism, Dà Zhào attracts pilgrims from across the land, who prostrate themselves fully in prayer on boards in front of the magnificent altars through the temple.
这座壮观的藏传佛教寺庙是该市最古老、最大的寺庙。该建筑群在中文中也被称为“ 无量寺”（无量寺 ，Wúliàng Sì），最初建于 16 世纪，并在下个世纪得到了很大的改进。大昭是一个非常神圣的地方，也是对藏传佛教神秘方式的迷人介绍，吸引了来自全国各地的朝圣者，他们在寺庙宏伟的祭坛前的木板上俯伏祈祷。

Look for the amazing-looking and blue-faced Medicine Buddha (药师佛, Yàoshī Fó) seated in his namesake hall and garbed in the most astonishing fashion. Also seek out the many-armed golden deity in the esoteric Mizong Hufa Hall (密宗护法殿, Mìzōng Hùfǎ Diàn), the central tantric deity within the Shengle Jingang Hall (胜乐金刚殿, Shènglè Jīngāng Diàn) and the huge jade Buddha residing in the Jade Buddha Hall (玉佛殿, Yùfó Diàn). The temple is also home to a 2.55m silver effigy of Sakyamuni, contained within the Buddha Hall (佛堂, Fótáng). Note the Changshuo Stupa (长寿佛塔, Chángshòu Fótǎ), draped in prayer flags. In other halls, pilgrims walk in clockwise fashion around altars, twirling prayer wheels, lost in prayer.
寻找长相惊人、脸色发青的药师佛 （ 药师佛 ，Yàoshī Fó）坐在他同名的大厅里，穿着最惊人的服装。还可以在密宗护法殿 （ 密宗护法殿 ，Mìzōng Hùfǎ Diàn）中寻找多臂金神，胜乐金刚殿（胜乐金刚殿，Shènglè Jīngāng Diàn）内的中央密宗神，以及居住在玉佛殿（玉佛殿 ，Yùfó Diàn）中的巨大玉佛。该寺庙还供奉着佛堂内 2.55 米高的释迦牟尼银像。 请注意长寿佛塔（长寿佛塔 ，Chángshòu Fótǎ），上面挂着经幡。 在其他大厅里，朝圣者以顺时针的方式绕着祭坛走，旋转转经轮，沉浸在祈祷中。

The plaza south of the temple is popular with kite flyers and is great for peoplewatching. There’s an attractive decorative archway and a vast statue of Altan Khan (1507–82), the Mongol founder of the city and ruler who began building the temple.
寺庙南边的广场很受放风筝的人欢迎，非常适合观赏人群。这里有一个迷人的装饰性拱门和一尊巨大的阿尔坦汗雕像，他是这座城市的蒙古创始人和开始建造这座寺庙的统治者。

(map; 五塔寺, Wǔtǎ Sì; Wutasi Houjie, 五塔寺后街; ¥35; h8am-6pm)

Rising up at the rear of the Five Pagoda Temple, this striking, Indian-influenced, five-tiered pagoda was completed in 1732. Its main claim to fame is the Mongolian star chart around the back (protected behind glass), though the engraving of the Diamond Sutra (in Sanskrit, Tibetan and Mongolian), extending around the entire base of the structure, is in much better condition. Another fascinating aspect of the temple is the ‘Temple Culture Exhibition of Hohhot’ Hall, containing a mesmerising array of tantric statues.

(map Google map; 席力图召; Da’nan Jie, 大南街; ¥25; h7.30am-6.30pm)

East across the road from the Dà Zhào temple is this simple, peaceful monastery, which is also known as Xiǎo Zhào or Yanshou Temple (延寿寺, Yánshòu Sì). The temple is the official residence of Hohhot’s 11th Living Buddha (he actually works elsewhere). Monks chant here at 9am and 3pm.

Inner Mongolia MuseumMUSEUM
内蒙古博物馆 MUSEUM

(内蒙古博物院, Nèi Měnggǔ Bówùyuàn; 27 Xinhua Dongdajie, 新华东大街27号; h9am-5.30pm Tue-Sun) F

This massive museum in the northeastern section of town has a distinctive sloping roof supposed to resemble the vast steppes of Mongolia. It’s one of the better provincial museums, with a focus on Mongolian culture, from an excellent dinosaur exhibition to Genghis Khan and the space age.
这座位于城镇东北部的大型博物馆有一个独特的倾斜屋顶，应该类似于蒙古广阔的草原。它是最好的省级博物馆之一，专注于蒙古文化，从优秀的恐龙展览到成吉思汗和太空时代。

NaadamSPORTS

The week-long summer festival known as Naadam (literally meaning ‘Games’ in Mongolian) is the most famous knees-up in Inner Mongolia, featuring traditional Mongolian sports such as archery, wrestling and horse racing. The competitions and festivities take place at Gegentala and at various grassland areas in early July when the grass is green. Book local tours at your accommodation.
为期一周的夏季节日被称为那达慕（蒙古语的字面意思是“游戏”）是内蒙古最著名的跪地活动，以射箭、摔跤和赛马等蒙古传统运动为特色。比赛和庆祝活动于 7 月初在 Gegentala 和各个草原地区举行，此时草地绿意盎然。在您的住宿预订当地旅游。

History

Conceived by Kublai Khan, grandson of Genghis and the first Yuan emperor, Shangdu’s lifespan as the summer capital was relatively brief. Construction of the city started in 1252 and lasted four years, serving as Kublai Khan’s summer palace away from his palace in Zhongdu (Khanbaliq), the Yuan dynasty capital in today’s Beijing. Shangdu was overrun and destroyed by Ming forces in 1369.
上都由成吉思汗的孙子、元朝第一位皇帝忽必烈构思，作为夏季首都的寿命相对较短。这座城市的建设始于 1252 年，历时四年，是忽必烈在中都（可汗八里克）宫殿之外的颐和园，即今天的元朝首都。1369年，上都被明军占领并被摧毁。

Shangdu actually consisted of three distinct cities: the outer city, the imperial city and the palace city. The site was listed as a Unesco World Heritage site in June 2012.

这座神话般的四合院宫殿是当地王子阿拉善秦王的故居。这座宫殿是一座经过精心修复的清代建筑群，由建筑和庭院组成，有末代王子（1903-68 年）及其家人的照片，以及他们的一些个人物品，但抢尽风头的是华丽的传统布局和建筑。

FAR WEST INNER MONGOLIA
内蒙古远西

The golden deserts, shimmering lakes and ruined cities of western Inner Mongolia are fantastic places for adventures far from the beaten track. With airports in the three major towns across the region – Bayanhot, Alashan Youqi (阿拉善右旗) and Ejina Qi (额济纳旗) – providing links to Hohhot and Xi’an, the Badain Jaran Desert and ancient town of Khara Khoto is more accessible than before. Yet this remains a vast and infrequently explored area offering some of the most photographic landscapes you can hope to locate in China.
内蒙古西部的金色沙漠、波光粼粼的湖泊和废墟城市是远离人迹罕至的地方进行冒险的绝佳去处。该地区的三个主要城镇——巴彦浩特、 阿拉善右旗和额济纳旗 ——设有机场，提供前往呼和浩特和习安的连接，巴丹吉兰沙漠和卡拉霍托古镇比以前更容易到达。然而，这仍然是一个广阔且鲜为人知的地区，提供了一些您希望在中国找到的最有摄影色彩的风景。

The remote but stunning Badain Jaran Desert (巴丹吉林沙漠, Bādānjílín Shāmò) (the world’s fourth-largest desert) is a mysterious 49,000-sq-km landscape of desert lakes, Buddhist temples and towering dunes. The dunes here are among the tallest in the world, some topping 460m (taller than the Empire State Building). The tallest are static with a solid core, and do not move. The closest town in the region, Alashan Youqi (阿拉善右旗), is a 30-minute drive from the dunes.
偏远但令人惊叹的巴丹吉林沙漠 （ 巴丹吉林沙漠 ，Bādānjílín Shāmò）（ 世界第四大沙漠）是一个占地 49,000 平方公里的神秘景观，由沙漠湖泊、佛教寺庙和高耸的沙丘组成。这里的沙丘是世界上最高的沙丘之一，有些沙丘高达 460 米（比帝国大厦还高）。最高的是静态的，有一个实心核心，并且不会移动。该地区最近的城镇阿拉善右旗距离沙丘有 30 分钟车程。

The ruined Tangut city of Khara Khoto (黑城, Hēichéng, Black City; ¥10; h8am-7pm) was built in 1032 and captured by Genghis Khan in 1226 (his last great battle). Khara Khoto continued to thrive under Mongol occupation, but in 1372 an upstart Ming battalion starved the city of its water source, killing everyone inside. Six hundred years of dust storms nearly buried Khara Khoto, until the Russian explorer PK Kozlov excavated and mapped the site, recovering hundreds of Tangut-era texts (now kept at the Institute of Oriental Manuscripts in St Petersburg). Located about 25km southeast of Ejina Qi, the allure of Khara Khoto is the remoteness of the site and surrounding natural beauty. A great time to visit is late September to early October when the poplar trees are changing colours; but be warned that every hotel room in Ejina Qí will be booked out at this time.
被毁坏的唐古特城市卡拉霍托 （ 黑城 ，Hēichéng，黑城;10 日元; h 上午 8 点至晚上 7 点） 建于 1032 年，并于 1226 年被成吉思汗占领（他的最后一场大战役）。喀拉霍托在蒙古占领下继续繁荣昌盛，但在 1372 年，一个新贵的明朝营使该市的水源匮乏，杀死了城内的所有人。六百年的沙尘暴几乎掩埋了卡拉霍托，直到俄罗斯探险家 PK 科兹洛夫挖掘并绘制了该遗址的地图，发现了数百份唐古特时代的文献（现保存在圣彼得堡东方手稿研究所）。Khara Khoto 位于 Ejina Qi 东南约 25 公里处，其魅力在于该地点的偏远和周围的自然美景。游览的好时机是 9 月下旬至 10 月初，此时杨树正在变色;但请注意，此时额济纳奇的每间酒店房间都会被预订一空。

There are hotels that accept foreigners in Bayanhot and Alashan Youqi, but not all hotels accept foreigners in Ejina Qi (额济纳旗) and sometimes permits are required to visit (though not at the time of writing); it’s perhaps best to visit the town as a day trip or arranged through a tour company such as China Hiking (%1565 2200 950; www.chinahiking.cn).
巴彦浩特和阿拉善游旗有接受外国人的酒店，但并非所有酒店都接受额济纳旗的外国人，有时需要许可证才能参观（尽管在撰写本文时尚未）;最好以一日游的形式游览该镇，或通过旅游公司安排，例如 China Hiking（ % 1565 2200 950;www.chinahiking.cn）。
