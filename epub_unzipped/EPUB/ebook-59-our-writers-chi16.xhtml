OUR STORY
我们的故事

A beat-up old car, a few dollars in the pocket and a sense of adventure. In 1972 that’s all Tony and Maureen Wheeler needed for the trip of a lifetime – across Europe and Asia overland to Australia. It took several months, and at the end – broke but inspired – they sat at their kitchen table writing and stapling together their first travel guide, Across Asia on the Cheap. Within a week they’d sold 1500 copies. Lonely Planet was born.
一辆破旧的旧车，口袋里有几块钱，还有一种冒险精神。1972 年，这就是托尼·惠勒 （Tony Wheeler） 和莫琳·惠勒 （Maureen Wheeler） 一生难忘的旅行所需要的一切——穿越欧洲和亚洲，通过陆路到达澳大利亚。花了几个月的时间，最后——破产但受到启发——他们坐在厨房的桌子旁写下并装订了他们的第一本旅行指南《 廉价穿越亚洲 》。一周之内，他们就卖出了 1500 张。孤独星球诞生了。

     Today, Lonely Planet has offices in Tennessee, Dublin, Beijing and Delhi, with a network of over 2000 contributors in every corner of the globe. We share Tony’s belief that ‘a great guidebook should do three things: inform, educate and amuse’.
如今，孤独星球在田纳西州、都柏林、北京和德里设有办事处，在全球各个角落拥有 2000 多名贡献者网络。我们和托尼一样相信“一本伟大的旅游指南应该做三件事：信息、教育和娱乐”。

OUR WRITERS
我们的作家

Damian Harper
达米安·哈珀

Gansu, Qinghai, Inner Mongolia
甘肃、青海、内蒙古

Born off the Strand within earshot of Bow Bells (favourable wind permitting), Damian grew up in Notting Hill way before it was discovered by Hollywood. A one-time Shakespeare and Company bookseller and radio presenter, Damian has been authoring guidebooks for Lonely Planet since the late 1990s. He lives in South London with his wife and two kids, and frequently returns to China (his second home). Damian also wrote the Plan Your Trip, Understand and Survival chapters.
达米安出生在弓钟的耳边（风力允许的情况下），在诺丁山被好莱坞发现之前，他在诺丁山长大。达米安曾是莎士比亚剧团的书商和电台主持人，自 1990 年代后期以来一直为《孤独星球》撰写指南。他与妻子和两个孩子住在伦敦南部，并经常返回中国（他的第二故乡）。达米安还撰写了“计划你的旅行”、“理解”和“生存”章节。

Jade Bremner
杰德·布雷姆纳

Shanghai
上海

Jade has been a journalist for more than 15 years. She has lived in and reported on four different regions. It’s no coincidence many of her favourite places have some of the best waves in the world. Jade has edited travel magazines and sections for Time Out and Radio Times and has contributed to the Times, CNN and the Independent. She feels privileged to share tales from this wonderful planet we call home and is always looking for the next adventure. @jadebremner.
Jade 从事记者工作已超过 15 年。她曾在四个不同的地区生活并报道过。她最喜欢的许多地方都有世界上最好的海浪，这并非巧合。Jade 曾编辑旅游杂志和《Time Out》和《Radio Times》的版块，并为《 泰晤士报 》、《CNN》和《 独立报 》撰稿。她很荣幸能够分享这个我们称之为家的美妙星球的故事，并且一直在寻找下一次冒险。@jadebremner。

Stuart Butler
斯图尔特·巴特勒

Sichuan
四川

Stuart has been writing for Lonely Planet for a decade and during this time he’s come eye to eye with gorillas in the Congolese jungles, met a man with horns on his head who could lie in fire, huffed and puffed over snowbound Himalayan mountain passes, interviewed a king who could turn into a tree, and had his fortune told by a parrot. Oh, and he’s met more than his fair share of self-proclaimed Gods. When not on the road for Lonely Planet he lives on the beautiful beaches of Southwest France with his wife and two young children. His website is www.stuartbutlerjournalist.com..
斯图尔特为《孤独星球》撰稿已有十年，在此期间，他与刚果丛林中的大猩猩见面，遇到了一个头上长着角的人，可以躺在火中，气喘吁吁地越过白雪皑皑的喜马拉雅山口，采访了一位可以变成树的国王，并让鹦鹉占了命。哦，他遇到的自称神灵的人数比他应得的还要多。当不去孤独星球旅行时，他与妻子和两个年幼的孩子住在法国西南部美丽的海滩上。他的网站是 www.stuartbutlerjournalist.com.。

Piera Chen
陈倩娟

Hong Kong, Macau, Shanxi, Ningxia
香港、澳门、山西、宁夏

Piera is a travel writer who divides her time among Hong Kong (hometown), Taiwan and Vancouver when not on the road. She has authored over a dozen travel guides and contributed to as many travel-related titles. Piera has a BA in literature from Pomona College. Her early life was peppered with trips to Taiwan and China to visit relatives, and then to Southeast Asia where her father was working. But it was during her first trip to Europe that dawn broke. She remembers fresh off a flight, looking around her in Rome, thinking, ‘I want to be doing this everyday.’ And she has. Since then Piera has been to Europe, the Americas and all over Asia – solo in the past, and more recently with her young daughter in tow.
Piera 是一位旅行作家，不在路上时，她会在香港（家乡）、台湾和温哥华之间分配时间。她撰写了十几本旅游指南，并为许多与旅行相关的书籍做出了贡献。Piera 拥有波莫纳学院的文学学士学位。她的早年生活包括去台湾和中国探亲，然后去她父亲工作的东南亚。但正是在她第一次去欧洲的时候，黎明破晓了。她记得刚从飞机上下来，在罗马环顾四周，心想，'我想每天都这样做。她做到了。从那时起，皮埃拉去过欧洲、美洲和整个亚洲——过去是独自一人，最近是带着她年幼的女儿。

Xinjiang
