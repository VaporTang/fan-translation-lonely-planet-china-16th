LGBTIQ+ TRAVELLERS
LGBTIQ+ 旅行者

Hong Kong has a small but growing LGBTIQ+ scene and the annual Pride Parade in November now attracts rainbow flag-wavers by the thousands. That said, Hong Kong Chinese society remains fairly conservative, and it can still be risky for gay and lesbian individuals to come out to family members or their employers. It is not common to see LGBTIQ+ couples making displays of affection in public.
香港的 LGBTIQ+ 场景规模虽小，但不断增长，一年一度的 11 月骄傲游行现在吸引了数千名挥舞彩虹旗的人。尽管如此，香港华人社会仍然相当保守，同性恋者向家人或雇主出柜仍然存在风险。LGBTIQ+ 夫妇在公共场合表达爱意并不常见。

In 1991 the Crimes (Amendment) Ordinance removed criminal penalties for homosexual acts between consenting adults over the age of 18. Since then LGBTIQ+ groups have been lobbying for legislation to address the issue of discrimination on the grounds of sexual orientation, but the government has been criticised for failing to recognise and embrace the community.
1991 年，《刑事罪行（修订）条例》取消了对 18 岁以上成年人之间同意的同性恋行为的刑事处罚。从那时起，LGBTIQ+ 团体一直在游说立法解决基于性取向的歧视问题，但政府因未能承认和包容社区而受到批评。

In July 2018 there was a landmark ruling by Hong Kong’s Court of Final Appeal, stating that the same-sex partner of a British expat should be granted a spousal visa – a move that has given the local LGBTIQ+ community hope that change (and, importantly, greater equality) is on the horizon.
2018 年 7 月，香港终审法院做出了一项具有里程碑意义的裁决，规定英国侨民的同性伴侣应获得配偶签证——此举给当地 LGBTIQ+ 群体带来了希望，即改变（更重要的是，更大的平等）即将到来。

BUS
总线

Hong Kong’s extensive bus system will take you just about anywhere in the territory, but it’s not always easy to follow. Since Kowloon and the northern side of Hong Kong Island are so well served by the MTR, most visitors use the buses primarily to explore the southern side of Hong Kong Island, the New Territories and Lantau Island.
香港广泛的巴士系统几乎可以带您前往全港任何地方，但并不总是那么容易。由于港铁服务九龙和香港岛北面非常便利，大多数游客主要乘坐巴士探索香港岛南面、新界和大屿山。

Departures Most buses run from 5.30am or 6am until midnight or 12.30am. There are a small number of night buses that run from 12.45am to 5am or later, designated with the letter ‘N’.
离开大多数巴士的运行时间为早上 5.30 或早上 6 点，直到午夜或凌晨 12.30。有少量夜间巴士从凌晨 12.45 运行至凌晨 5 点或更晚，标有字母“N”。

Fares Bus fares cost HK$4.50 to HK$47, depending on the destination. Fares for night buses cost from HK$7.50 to HK$33. You will need exact change or an Octopus card.
票价巴士票价为 4.50 港元至 47 港元，具体取决于目的地。夜行巴士的票价由港币 7.50 元至 33 元不等。您需要找零或八达通卡。

Bus stations On Hong Kong Island the most important bus stations are the terminus in Central underneath Exchange Sq and the one on Queensway at Admiralty. From these stations you can catch buses to Aberdeen, Repulse Bay, Stanley and other destinations on the southern side of Hong Kong Island. In Kowloon the Star Ferry bus terminal has buses heading up Nathan Rd and to the Hung Hom train station.
公交车站在香港岛，最重要的巴士站是中环总站、交易广场下方和金钟大道的巴士站。从这些车站，你可以乘坐巴士前往香港仔、浅水湾、赤柱和香港岛南侧的其他目的地。在九龙，天星小轮巴士总站有巴士前往弥敦道和红磡火车站。

CAR & MOTORCYCLE
汽车和摩托车

Hong Kong’s maze of one-way streets and dizzying expressways isn’t for the faint-hearted. Traffic is heavy and finding a parking space is difficult and very expensive. If you are determined to see Hong Kong under your own steam, do yourself a favour and rent a car with a driver.
香港迷宫般的单行道和令人眼花缭乱的高速公路不适合胆小的人。交通繁忙，很难找到停车位，而且非常昂贵。如果您决心自己游览香港，请帮自己一个忙，租一辆带司机的汽车。

Road rules Vehicles drive on the left-hand side of the road in Hong Kong, as in the UK, Australia and Macau, but not in mainland China. Seat belts must be worn by the driver and all passengers, in both the front and back seats. Police are strict and give out traffic tickets at the drop of a hat.
道路规则在香港，车辆在道路左侧行驶，就像英国、澳大利亚和澳门一样，但在中国大陆则不然 。驾驶员和所有乘客在前排和后排座椅上都必须系好安全带。警察很严格，会立即发出交通罚单。

Driving licence Hong Kong allows most foreigners over the age of 18 to drive for up to 12 months with a valid licence from home. It’s still a good idea to carry an International Driving Permit (IDP) as well. Car-rental firms accept IDPs or driving licences from your home country; drivers must usually be at least 25 years of age.
駕駛執照香港允许大多数 18 岁以上的外国人持有效驾照在家中驾驶长达 12 个月。携带国际驾照 （IDP） 仍然是一个好主意。汽车租赁公司接受来自您所在国家/地区的国内流离失所者或驾驶执照;司机通常必须年满 25 岁。

TRAIN
火车

The Mass Transit Railway is the name for Hong Kong’s rail system comprising underground, overland and Light Rail (slower tram-style) services. Universally known as the ‘MTR’, it is clean, fast and safe, and transports around four million people daily.
香港铁路是香港铁路系统的名称，包括地下、陆路和轻铁（慢速电车式）服务。它被称为“港铁”，干净、快速、安全，每天运送约 400 万人。

It costs only slightly more than bus travel (fares HK$5 to HK$55), and is the quickest way to get to most destinations in Hong Kong. Routes, timetables and fares can be found at www.mtr.com.hk.
它仅比巴士旅行（票价 5 至 55 港元）略高，是前往香港大多数目的地的最快方式。路线、时刻表和票价可在 www.mtr.com.hk 找到。

There are around 90 stations on nine underground and overland lines, and a Light Rail network that covers the northwest New Territories. Smoking, eating and drinking are not permitted in MTR stations or on the trains, and violators are subject to a fine of HK$2000 to HK$5000.
九条地下和陆路线路约有90个车站，轻铁网络覆盖新界西北部。港铁站或火车内禁止吸烟、饮食，违者可被罚款港币2000元至5000元。

Departures Trains run every two to 14 minutes from around 6am to sometime between midnight and 1am.
离开火车从早上 6 点左右到午夜至凌晨 1 点之间的某个时间，每两到十四分钟一班。

Exits MTR exit signs use an alphanumerical system and there can be as many as a dozen to choose from. There are easy-to-navigate maps of the local area in each ticket hall; use them to decipher which exit will serve you best.
出口港铁出口标志采用字母数字系统，最多可有十几个可供选择。每个售票大厅都有易于导航的当地地图;使用它们来破译哪个出口最适合您。

Fares Tickets are extremely cheap compared with those in many other world cities: between HK$5 and HK$30, though fares to stations bordering mainland China (Lo Wu and Lok Ma Chau) cost up to HK$55. Children aged between three and 11 years and seniors over 65 pay half-fare. Ticket machines accept notes and coins, and dispense change.
票价与许多其他世界城市相比，车票非常便宜：在 5 至 30 港元之间，但前往与中国大陆接壤的车站（罗湖和落马洲）的票价高达 55 港元。3 至 11 岁的儿童和 65 岁以上的老年人支付半价。售票机接受纸币和硬币，并发放零钱。

Tickets Once you’ve passed through the turnstile to begin a journey you have 90 minutes to complete it before the ticket becomes invalid. If you have underpaid (by mistake or otherwise), you can make up the difference at an MTR service counter next to the turnstiles.
票一旦您通过十字转门开始旅程，您有 90 分钟的时间完成旅程，然后车票就会失效。如果您少付了钱（错误或其他原因），您可以在旋转门旁边的港铁服务柜台补足差额。

Peak hours If possible, it’s best to avoid the rush hours: 7.30am to 9.30am and 5pm to 7pm weekdays.
高峰时段如果可能的话，最好避开高峰时段：工作日上午 7.30 至上午 9.30 和下午 5 点至晚上 7 点。
