This excellent arts festival installs itself into various parts of the city for a few months every two years, presenting thought-provoking exhibitions and events on issues faced by rapidly urbanising cities like Shenzhen. Previous event venues include Futian Railway Station, and Nantou Old Town, one of Shenzhen’s ‘urban villages’ – congested communities of transient workers from rural communities.
这个优秀的艺术节每两年在城市各个地方举办几个月，就深圳等快速城市化城市所面临的问题举办发人深省的展览和活动。此前的活动地点包括福田火车站和深圳的“城中村”之一南投老城区——来自农村社区的临时工人拥挤的社区。

With multiple disparate centres, the conundrum in Shenzhan is choosing where to stay. High-rise Futian District has the most luxury hotels, while scruffier Luohu District is stuffed with budget and midrange business hotels – the best orbit Laojie metro. Shekou, in the city’s west, has decent midrange and upmarket options, but is further from the sights. Consider anywhere in range of OCT-LOFT, one of the most pleasant, central areas, though with pricier digs.
由于有多个不同的中心，深站的难题是选择住宿地点 。高层的福田区拥有最多的豪华酒店，而邋遢的罗湖区则挤满了经济型和中档商务酒店——最好的轨道老街地铁。蛇口位于城市西部，有不错的中档和高档选择，但距离景点较远。考虑 OCT-LOFT 范围内的任何地方，这是最宜人的中心区域之一，尽管挖掘价格更高。

Locals say that for the best food, go north to Guangzhou or south to Hong Kong. That’s doing Shenzhen a disservice, which, given its migrant population, is home to a huge diversity of food from all over China. Dim sum and Cantonese fare is ubiquitous too, with most of the famous Guangdong chains represented.

You have to dig a little for great nightlife in Shenzhen, but when you do you’re sure to strike gold. Boutique cocktail speakeasies are opening faster than you can muddle a mojito. That’s Shenzhen (www.thatsmags.com/shenzhen) is a handy source on what’s hot and happening.
你必须在深圳挖掘一点精彩的夜生活，但当你这样做时，你一定会发现金子。精品鸡尾酒地下酒吧的开业速度比你喝莫吉托还要快。这就是深圳 （www.thatsmags.com/shenzhen） 是了解热门和正在发生的事情的便捷来源。

One of the liveliest concentrations of bars and clubs in Shenzhen can be found in Coco Park, an upmarket shopping complex in high-rise Futian District, which has its own, fairly commercial bar street and plenty of restaurants.
深圳最热闹的酒吧和俱乐部集中地之一是位于福田区高层住宅区的高档购物中心可可公园，拥有自己的商业酒吧街和众多餐馆。

肇庆堂 （ 肇庆堂 ，Zhàoqìng Táng;¥30; h 上午 8.30 至中午和下午 2 点至 5 点） 是一座温暖的庭院住宅（约 1914 年），拥有石头、木材和陶瓷雕刻，以及来自意大利的彩色玻璃窗。它前面庄严的两层建筑是家庭年轻成员的书房。

An eye-catching, early-20th-century mansion, the Haiyuan Inn (海源客棧, Hăiyuán Kèzhàn) combines Southeast Asian features, Western details and the attributes of a hakka (走马楼; zǒumǎ lóu), a two-storey residence with a wide wooden corridor that keeps the rooms safe and dry.
海源客栈（海源客栈 ，Hăiyuán Kèzhàn ）是一座引人注目的 20 世纪初豪宅，结合了东南亚特色、西方细节和客家（ 走马楼 ;zǒumǎ lóu），这是一座两层楼的住宅，有一条宽阔的木制走廊，保持房间安全和干燥。

Although there is loads of potential for a tourist town, options for sleeping here are virtually nil as most people still move on and stay in nearby Meizhou.
尽管旅游小镇有很大的潜力，但在这里睡觉的选择几乎为零，因为大多数人仍然继续前进并留在附近的梅州。

There are a handful of simple places to eat in the Baihou Tourist Area (百侯旅游区, Băihóu Lǚyóu Qū) and around the bus station (百侯客运站, Bǎihóu Kèyùn Zhàn), 1km north.
在百侯旅游区 （Băihóu Lǚyóu Qū）和汽车站（ 百侯客运站 ，Bǎihóu Kèyùn Zhàn）以北 1 公里处，有一些简单的餐饮场所。

所有从大埠出发的南行巴士都会经过白侯（7 日元）。告诉司机您要在百侯旅游区 （Băihóu Lǚyóu Qū）下车。从大部出发，20 分钟（11 公里）。

GUANGDONG’S LARGEST CIRCULAR EARTHEN CASTLE
广东最大的圆形土堡

The majestic 400-year-old ‘house of calyx’, 120km east of Meizhou, is the largest circular earthen castle (花萼楼; 151 Xiang Dao, 乡道151; admission ¥10; h9am-4pm) in Guangdong. It comes complete with three rings and stone walls more than 1m thick. There’s no public transport to Huā’è Lóu. A taxi from Dabu costs ¥100 and takes about an hour to travel 36km on neglected hilly roads.
位于梅州以东 120 公里处，有 400 年历史的雄伟“花萼楼”，是最大的圆形土堡 （ 花萼楼 ;乡道 151 号 ;门票 10 日元; h 上午 9 点至下午 4 点）。 它配有三个环和厚度超过 1m 的石墙。没有公共交通工具前往华楼。从大布乘坐出租车费用为 100 日元，在被忽视的丘陵道路上行驶 36 公里大约需要一个小时。
