PRACTICALITIES
实用性

Newspapers The standard English-language news-paper is the China Daily (www.chinadaily.com.cn). China’s highest-circulation Chinese-language daily is the People’s Daily (人民日报, Rénmín Rìbào), which has an English-language edition at http://en.people.cn. Imported English-language newspapers can be bought from five-star-hotel bookshops.
报纸标准的英文报纸是 《中国日报》（www.chinadaily.com.cn）。中国发行量最高的中文日报是《 人民日报 》，http://en.people.cn 有英文版。进口英文报纸可以从五星级酒店书店买到。

Internet Radio & TV Listen to the BBC World Service (www.bbc.co.uk/worldserviceradio) or Voice of America (www.voa.gov), but you will need to have a VPN installed for access as both websites are blocked in China. Chinese Central TV (CCTV) has two English-language channels: CCTV9 and CCTV NEWS. Your hotel may have ESPN, Star Sports, CNN or BBC News 24.
网络、广播和电视收听 BBC 世界服务 （www.bbc.co.uk/worldserviceradio） 或美国之音 （www.voa.gov），但您需要安装 VPN 才能访问，因为这两个网站在中国都被屏蔽。中国中央电视台（CCTV）有两个英文频道：CCTV9 和 CCTV NEWS。您的酒店可能有 ESPN、Star Sports、CNN 或 BBC News 24。

Smoking Can happen everywhere in China, even around children, in elevators and in supposedly non-smoking hotels. It is easier to try to avoid it rather than show annoyance. For example, take the high-speed trains, which strictly enforce smoking bans. Smokers can buy cigarettes everywhere, though visitors might find Chinese brands very pungent and strong.
吸烟在中国任何地方都可能发生，甚至在儿童周围、电梯里和据称是禁烟的酒店里。试图避免它比表现出烦恼更容易。例如，乘坐高铁，它们严格执行禁烟令。吸烟者到处都可以买到香烟，尽管游客可能会觉得中国品牌非常刺鼻和强烈。

Weights & Measures China officially uses the international metric system, but you will also encounter the ancient Chinese weights and measures system that features the liǎng (两; tael; 50g) and the jīn (斤; catty; 500g). There are 10 liǎng to the jīn.
度量衡中国正式使用国际公制，但您也会遇到以 liǎng（ 两 ; 两;50g）和斤 （ 斤 ; 斤;500g）。有 10 liǎng 到 jīn。

Discounts Seniors over the age of 65 are frequently eligible for discounts and those over 70 get free admission, so make sure you take your passport when visiting sights.
折扣 65 岁以上的老年人通常有资格享受折扣，70 岁以上的人可以免费入场，因此参观景点时请务必携带护照。

An International Student Identity Card (ISIC; www.isic.org) can net students half-price discounts at many sights, but you may have to insist – and you may have as much luck with your home country’s student card.
国际学生证（ISIC;www.isic.org）可以在许多景点为学生提供半价折扣，但您可能不得不坚持——而且您可能拥有本国学生卡的运气。

Accessible Travel
无障碍旅行

China is not easy to navigate for travellers with limited mobility, but travel in a wheelchair is possible in the large cities by staying at top-end accommodation (with lots of preparation and prebooking). High-speed trains are generally far more accessible than older rolling stock. Many urban metro systems are quite accessible as they are relatively modern systems, so a fair number of stations have lifts. Accessible toilets can be found in shopping malls in large cities and also at airports, but squat loos elsewhere can make travelling very difficult.
对于行动不便的旅行者来说，中国并不容易导航，但在大城市，通过入住高端住宿（需要大量准备和预订），可以乘坐轮椅旅行。高速列车通常比旧的机车车辆更容易到达。许多城市地铁系统都很容易到达，因为它们是相对现代的系统，因此相当多的车站都有电梯。大城市的购物中心和机场都可以找到无障碍厕所，但其他地方的蹲厕所会使旅行变得非常困难。

Booking Services
预订服务

Booking online can help you secure a room and obtain a good price, but remember that you should be able to bargain down the price of your room at hotel reception (except at youth hostels and the cheapest hotels) or over the phone. To secure accommodation, always plan ahead and book your room in advance during the high season. Airports at major cities often have hotel-booking counters that offer discounted rates.
在线预订可以帮助您找到房间并获得优惠的价格，但请记住，您应该能够在酒店接待处（青年旅舍和最便宜的酒店除外）或通过电话讨价还价。为了确保住宿，请务必提前计划并在旺季提前预订房间。主要城市的机场通常设有提供折扣价的酒店预订柜台。

Camping
露营

There are few places where you can legally camp, and as most of China’s flat land is put to agricultural use, you will largely be limited to remote, hilly regions. Camping is more feasible in wilder and less-populated parts of west China, but be aware it is not strictly legal as you should register your whereabouts with the PSB.
可以合法露营的地方很少，而且由于中国大部分平地都用于农业用途，因此您将主要局限于偏远的丘陵地区。在中国西部较荒凉和人口较少的地区露营更为可行，但请注意，这并不严格合法，因为您应该向 PSB 登记您的行踪。

In certain destinations with camping possibilities, travel agencies and hotels will arrange overnight camping trips or multiday treks, in which case camping equipment will be supplied. Camping on the Great Wall is technically illegal, but the watchtowers are often used for pitching tents or rolling out a sleeping bag (if you do, make sure to clean up after yourself and take care of the Wall).
在某些可以露营的目的地，旅行社和酒店会安排过夜露营旅行或多日徒步旅行，在这种情况下，将提供露营设备。在长城上露营从技术上讲是非法的，但瞭望塔通常用于搭帐篷或铺睡袋（如果这样做，请务必自己清理并照顾长城）。

Courtyard Hotels
万怡酒店

Largely confined to Beijing, courtyard hotels are a good choice for a more traditional experience. Arranged around traditional sìhéyuàn (courtyards), rooms are on ground level. Courtyard hotels are charming and romantic, but are often expensive and rooms are small, in keeping with the dimensions of courtyard residences. Facilities will be limited, so don’t expect a swimming pool, gym or subterranean garage.
四合院酒店主要局限于北京，是享受更传统体验的不错选择。客房围绕着传统的 sìhéyuàn（庭院）而布置，位于地面层。四合院酒店迷人而浪漫，但通常价格昂贵，房间较小，与四合院住宅的尺寸保持一致。设施将受到限制，因此不要指望游泳池、健身房或地下车库。

Budget Business Chain Hotels
经济型商务连锁酒店

Dotted around much of China, budget business chain hotels can sometimes be a decent alternative to old-school two- and three-star hotels, with rooms around the ¥180 to ¥300 mark. Their sheer universality means you can usually find accommodation (but look at the rooms first). They often have membership/loyalty schemes, or online deals, which make rooms cheaper. Breakfast is sometimes included.
经济型商务连锁酒店遍布中国大部分地区，有时可以成为老式二星级和三星级酒店的不错替代品，客房价格在 180 至 300 日元左右。它们的纯粹普遍性意味着您通常可以找到住宿（但首先要看看房间）。他们通常有会员/忠诚度计划或在线交易，这使得房间更便宜。有时房价包括早餐。

Guesthouses
宾馆

The cheapest of the cheap are China’s ubiquitous and rather grimy guesthouses (招待所, zhāodàisuǒ), often found clustered near train or bus stations, but also dotted around cities and towns. The majority are upstairs, accessed through a small door leading up narrow stairs from the street. Not all guesthouses accept foreigners, and Chinese-language skills may be crucial in securing a room. Rooms (doubles, twins, triples, quads) are primitive and grey, with tiled floors and possibly a shower room or shabby bathroom. Showers may be communal.
最便宜的是中国无处不在且相当肮脏的宾馆（ 招待所 ，zhāodàisuǒ），通常聚集在火车站或汽车站附近，但也散布在城镇周围。大多数都在楼上，通过一扇小门从街道上通往狭窄的楼梯。并非所有宾馆都接受外国人，中文技能对于确保房间至关重要。房间（双人间、双床间、三人间、四人间）是原始的灰色，铺有瓷砖地板，可能还有淋浴间或破旧的浴室。淋浴可以是公共的。

Homesteads
家园

In more rural destinations, small towns and villages, you should be able to find a homestead (农家, nóngjiā) with a small number of rooms in the region of ¥50. Bargaining is possible; you will not need to register. The owner will be more than happy to cook up meals for you as well. Showers and toilets are generally communal.
在更多的乡村目的地、小城镇和村庄，您应该能够在 50 日元左右找到房间数量较少的宅基地（ 农家 ，nóngjiā）。讨价还价是可能的;您无需注册。业主也很乐意为您做饭。淋浴和厕所通常是公共的。

TRAVELLER RESTRICTIONS
旅客限制

The majority of hotels in China still do not have the authorisation to accept foreigners as guests. This can be a source of frustration when you find yourself steered towards pricier midrange and top-end lodgings. As a useful general rule, if a hotel can be booked on major websites such as booking.com and Agoda, it will usually be able to accept foreign guests.
中国大多数酒店仍然没有授权接受外国人作为客人。当您发现自己被引导到价格更高的中档和高端住宿时，这可能会让您感到沮丧。作为一个有用的一般规则，如果可以在 booking.com 和 Agoda 等主要网站上预订酒店，它通常可以接受外国客人。

To see if a hotel accepts foreign guests, ask ‘zhègè bīnguǎn shōu wàiguórén ma?’ (这个宾馆收外国人吗?).

Hostels
宿舍

If you’re looking for efficiently run budget accommodation, turn to China’s youth-hostel sector. Hostelling International (www.yhachina.com; English functionality) hostels are generally well run. Other private youth hostels scattered around China are unaffiliated and standards at these may be variable. Book ahead in popular towns as rooms can go fast.
如果您正在寻找高效运营的经济型住宿，请转向中国的青年旅舍行业。 国际青年旅舍 （www.yhachina.com;英语功能）旅馆通常经营良好。其他散布在中国各地的私人青年旅舍是无关联的，这些旅舍的标准可能有所不同。在热门城镇提前预订，因为房间可以很快入住。

Superb for meeting like-minded travellers, hostels are typically staffed by youthful English speakers who are also well informed on local sightseeing and transport. The foreigner-friendly vibe in hostels stands in marked contrast to many stiffer Chinese hotels. Double rooms in youth hostels are frequently better than midrange equivalents and often just as comfortable and better located. These places may be cheaper (but not always: some hostels have prices similar to or higher than midrange hotels, so do compare), or can arrange better-value tours. Laundry, book lending, kitchen facilities, bike rental, lockers and a noticeboard, bar and cafe should all be available, as well as possibly a pool, ping pong, movies, game consoles and other forms of entertainment. Soap, shower gel and toothpaste are generally not provided, though you can purchase them at reception; the same is often (but not always) the case with towels. Be aware that some so-called ‘International Youth Hostels’ do not actually accept foreigners, so check.
旅舍非常适合结识志同道合的旅行者，通常由年轻的英语工作人员组成，他们也对当地的观光和交通了如指掌。旅馆对外国人友好的氛围与许多僵硬的中国酒店形成鲜明对比。青年旅舍的双人间通常比中档同类酒店更好，而且通常同样舒适且位置更好。这些地方可能更便宜（但并非总是：一些旅馆的价格与中档酒店相似或更高，所以一定要比较），或者可以安排更超值的旅游。洗衣房、图书借阅、厨房设施、自行车租赁、储物柜和布告栏、酒吧和咖啡馆都应该一应提供，可能还有游泳池、乒乓球、电影、游戏机和其他形式的娱乐活动。通常不提供肥皂、沐浴露和牙膏，但您可以在前台购买;毛巾通常（但并非总是）也是如此。请注意，一些所谓的“国际青年旅舍”实际上并不接受外国人，因此请检查。

Dorms usually cost between ¥40 and ¥55 (with discounts of around ¥5 for members). They typically come with bunk beds, but may have standard beds. Most dorms won’t have en-suite showers, though some do; they should have air-con (except in cooler regions). Many hostels also have doubles, singles, twins and sometimes even family rooms. Prices vary but are often around ¥100 to ¥250 for a single or double (again, with discounts for members). Hostels can arrange ticketing or help you book a room in another affiliated youth hostel.
宿舍的价格通常在 40 日元到 55 日元之间（会员可享受 5 日元左右的折扣）。它们通常配有双层床，但也可能有标准床。大多数宿舍没有套间淋浴，但有些宿舍有;他们应该有空调（凉爽地区除外）。许多旅馆还设有双人间、单人间、双床间，有时甚至有家庭房。价格各不相同，但单人或双人通常在 100 至 250 日元左右（同样，会员有折扣）。旅舍可以安排票务或帮你预订另一家附属青年旅舍的房间。

Book ahead – online if possible – as rooms are frequently booked out, especially at weekends or in busy holiday periods. In popular destinations (such as Hangzhou), hostels may charge elevated rates on Friday and Saturday.
如果可能的话，提前在线预订，因为房间经常被预订一空，尤其是在周末或繁忙的假期。在热门目的地（例如杭州），旅馆可能会在周五和周六收取较高的价格。

Hotels
酒店

Hotels vary wildly in quality within the same budget bracket. The star rating system employed in China can sometimes be misleading: hotels may be awarded four or five stars when they are patently a star lower in ranking. Deficiencies may not be immediately apparent, so explore and inspect the overall quality of the hotel – viewing the room up front pays dividends.
在同一预算范围内，酒店质量差异很大。中国采用的星级评定系统有时会产生误导：当酒店的排名明显较低时，它们可能会被授予四星或五星。缺陷可能不会立即显现出来，因此请探索和检查酒店的整体质量——提前查看房间会带来回报。

Breakfast at many older-style midrange hotels is often Chinese: porridge (粥, zhōu), fried vegetables and noodles, buns (馒头, mántou) and boiled eggs. Coffee is usually provided and perhaps bread, a toaster, jam, marmalade and butter. At more international hotels, western-style breakfast will be provided.
许多老式中档酒店的早餐通常是中式的：粥（ 粥 、zhōu）、炒蔬菜和面条、包子（ 馒头 、mántou）和煮鸡蛋。通常提供咖啡，也许还有面包、烤面包机、果酱、果酱和黄油。在更多的国际酒店，将提供西式早餐。

China has few independent hotels of real distinction, so it’s generally advisable to select chain hotels that offer a proven standard of international excellence. Shangri-La, Marriott, Hilton, St Regis, Ritz-Carlton, Marco Polo and Hyatt all have a presence in China and can generally be relied upon for high standards of service and comfort.
中国很少有真正杰出的独立酒店，因此通常建议选择提供经过验证的国际卓越标准的连锁酒店。香格里拉、万豪、希尔顿、瑞吉、丽思卡尔顿、马可波罗和凯悦都在中国开展业务，通常可以提供高标准的服务和舒适度。

Temples & Monasteries
寺庙和修道院

Some temples and monasteries (especially on China’s sacred mountains) provide accommodation. They are cheap but austere, may not have running water or electricity, and have very early check-out times.
一些寺庙和寺院（尤其是中国的神山）提供住宿。它们价格便宜但简朴，可能没有自来水或电，退房时间很早。

Checking In & Out
入住和退房

At check-in you will need your passport; a registration form will ask what type of visa you have. For most travellers the visa will be L (travel visa). A deposit (押金, yājīn) is required at most hotels, which will be paid either with cash or by providing your credit-card details. International credit cards are generally only accepted at midrange hotels or chain express hotels and top-end accommodation, so always have cash just in case. If you pay your deposit in cash, you will be given a receipt and the deposit will be returned to you when you check out.
办理登机手续时，您需要护照;注册表会询问您持有什么类型的签证。对于大多数旅行者来说，签证将是 L（旅行签证）。大多数酒店都需要支付押金（押金 ，yājīn），押金将以现金或提供信用卡详细信息支付。国际信用卡通常只在中档酒店或连锁快捷酒店和高端住宿中接受，因此请随身携带现金以防万一。如果您以现金支付押金，您将收到一张收据，押金将在您退房时退还给您。

Rooms & Prices
房间和价格

On the whole, China’s accommodation choices offer superb value for money. In most top-ticket towns it’s not hard to find a comfortable and spacious room for between ¥100 and ¥200.
总体而言，中国的住宿选择物超所值。在大多数顶级城镇，不难找到一个舒适宽敞的房间，价格在 100 日元到 200 日元之间。

Accommodation is divided by price category, identified by the symbols ¥ (budget), ¥¥ (midrange) or ¥¥¥ (top end) – accommodation prices vary across China, so one region’s budget breakdown may differ from another. It is common for hotel receptions to have a wall-mounted board with hyperinflated prices that aren’t actually in use. We list the ‘true’ rack rate, which generally reflects the most you are ever expected to pay. However, at most times of the year discounts are in effect, which can range from 10% to 60% off.
住宿按价格类别划分，由符号 ¥（预算）、¥¥（中档）或 ¥¥¥（高端）标识——中国各地的住宿价格各不相同，因此一个地区的预算明细可能与另一个地区不同。酒店接待处通常会有一块价格过高但实际上并未使用的壁挂式板。我们列出了“真实”的货架费率，它通常反映了您期望支付的最高金额。但是，在一年中的大多数时间，折扣都会生效，折扣范围为 10% 到 60%。

Rooms come with private bathroom or shower room, unless otherwise stated. Rooms are generally easy to procure, but reserve ahead in popular tourist towns (such as Hangzhou), especially for weekend visits or during the hectic holiday periods when rooms fill up quickly.
除非另有说明，否则客房均设有私人浴室或淋浴间。房间通常很容易获得，但在热门旅游城镇（如杭州）要提前预订，尤其是在周末游览或房间很快就会爆满的繁忙假期期间。

HOTEL DISCOUNTS
酒店折扣

Always ignore the rack rate and ask for the discounted price or bargain for a room, as discounts usually apply everywhere but youth hostels (except for hostel members) and the cheapest accommodation. Discounts of 10% to 60% off the tariff rate (30% is typical) are the norm, available by simply asking at reception on arrival, by phoning in advance to reserve a room, or by booking online at Trip.com or Booking.com.
始终忽略货架价格，要求折扣价或讨价还价的房间，因为折扣通常适用于除青年旅舍（青年旅舍会员除外）和最便宜的住宿之外的所有地方。关税率 10% 至 60% 的折扣（通常为 30%）是常态，只需在抵达时向接待处询问、提前致电预订房间或在 Trip.com 或 Booking.com 在线预订即可获得。

Apart from during the busy holiday periods (the end of April and first few days of May, the first week of October and Chinese New Year), rooms should be priced well below the rack rate and are rarely booked out. In some towns (such as Hangzhou or Guangzhou) there may be a pricier weekend rate (Friday and Saturday).
除了繁忙的假期（4月底和5月的头几天、10月的第一周和农历新年）外，房间的价格应远低于货架价格，并且很少被预订一空。在某些城镇（例如杭州或广州），周末价格（周五和周六）可能会更贵。

Food
食物

Cooking plays a central role in both Chinese society and the national psyche. When Chinese people meet, a common greeting is ‘Nǐ chīfàn le ma?’ (‘Have you eaten yet?’). Work, play, romance, business and family all revolve around food. The catalysts for all manner of enjoyment, meals are occasions for pleasure and entertainment, to clinch deals, strike up new friendships and rekindle old ones. To fully access this tasty domain on home soil, all you need is a visa, a pair of chopsticks, an adventurous palate and a passion for the unusual and unexpected.
烹饪在中国社会和民族心理中都扮演着核心角色。中国人见面时，常见的问候语是“Nǐ chīfàn le 马 ？（“你吃饭了吗？工作、娱乐、浪漫、商业和家庭都围绕着食物。用餐是各种享受的催化剂，是快乐和娱乐的场合，是达成交易、建立新友谊和重燃旧友谊的场合。要在家乡充分进入这个美味的领域，您所需要的只是签证、一双筷子、冒险的味觉以及对不寻常和意想不到的事物的热情。

保险

Carefully consider a travel-insurance policy to cover theft, loss, trip cancellation and medical eventualities. Travel agents can sort this out for you, though it is often cheaper to find good deals with an insurer online or with a broker.
仔细考虑旅行保险单，以涵盖盗窃、丢失、旅行取消和医疗意外情况。旅行社可以为您解决这个问题，尽管在线或经纪人处找到保险公司的优惠通常更便宜。

Some policies specifically exclude ‘dangerous activities’ such as scuba diving, skiing and even trekking/hiking. Check that the policy covers ambulances and an emergency flight home.
一些政策明确排除了“危险活动”，例如水肺潜水、滑雪甚至徒步旅行/远足。检查保单是否涵盖救护车和紧急航班回家。

您可能更喜欢直接向医生或医院付款的保单，而不是事后报销您的支出。如果您以后必须索赔，请确保保留所有文件。

Internet Access
上网

Wi-fi accessibility in hotels, cafes, restaurants and bars is generally good. The best option is to bring a smartphone, tablet or laptop, or purchase a local SIM card on arrival. Chain restaurants and cafes with free wi-fi sometimes still require a Chinese phone number to receive a login code and the prompt for that may just be in Chinese.
酒店、咖啡馆、餐馆和酒吧的 Wi-Fi 接入性总体良好。最好的选择是携带智能手机、平板电脑或笔记本电脑，或在抵达时购买当地 SIM 卡。提供免费 Wi-Fi 的连锁餐厅和咖啡馆有时仍然需要中国电话号码才能接收登录代码，而登录码的提示可能只是中文。

The Chinese authorities maintain strong controls on internet access. Around 10% of websites are blocked. The list is constantly changing, but includes sites and apps such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Google-owned sites (YouTube, Google Maps, Gmail, Google Drive), WhatsApp, Snapchat, Dropbox and many international media outlets, so plan ahead.
中国当局对互联网接入保持着强有力的控制。大约 10% 的网站被屏蔽。该列表不断变化，但包括 Facebook、Twitter、Instagram、Google 拥有的网站（YouTube、Google 地图、Gmail、Google Drive）、WhatsApp、Snapchat、Dropbox 和许多国际媒体等网站和应用程序，因此请提前计划。

Without a VPN (virtual private network) on your phone, tablet or laptop, expect to go cold turkey on social media (and even emails) for the duration of your trip, unless you use you own carrier’s roaming service, which may be prohibitively expensive. Bear in mind that many popular VPN services themselves become blocked, so ask around or check online before committing to a service. The government (which uses VPNs itself) had vowed to block all such services eventually, but at time of research was yet to do so.
如果您的手机、平板电脑或笔记本电脑上没有 VPN（虚拟专用网络），预计在您的旅行期间会在社交媒体（甚至电子邮件）上陷入困境，除非您使用自己的运营商的漫游服务，这可能非常昂贵。请记住，许多流行的 VPN 服务本身都会被阻止，因此在使用服务之前请四处询问或在线查看。政府（本身使用 VPN）曾发誓最终会阻止所有此类服务，但在研究时尚未这样做。

The VPN you use may sometimes interfere with your wi-fi connection and make it harder to get online when using public wi-fi, in which case you will need to turn off the VPN in order to access wi-fi. A VPN must be installed on devices before departing for China. VPNs can function less well or stop working entirely during politically sensitive times on the annual calendar. It’s a good idea to use two VPNs so you have a back-up in case one is twitchy or not working.
您使用的 VPN 有时可能会干扰您的 Wi-Fi 连接，并使使用公共 Wi-Fi 时更难上网，在这种情况下，您需要关闭 VPN 才能访问 Wi-Fi。在出发前往中国之前，必须在设备上安装 VPN。VPN 在年历上的政治敏感时期可能会运行较差或完全停止工作。最好使用两个 VPN，这样您就可以在其中一个出现抽搐或无法正常工作时有一个备份。

Language Courses
语言课程

Learning Chinese in China is big business. Weigh up the course fees and syllabus carefully and check online reviews – some schools are pricey and may use teaching methods unsuited to westerners. Consider where you would like to study: the Beijing accent and setting has obvious cachet, but a course in a setting such as Yangshuo can be delightful. For Cantonese courses, head to Hong Kong, Macau or Guangzhou.
在中国学习中文是一门大生意。仔细权衡课程费用和教学大纲并查看在线评论——有些学校价格昂贵，并且可能使用不适合西方人的教学方法。考虑一下您想在哪里学习：北京口音和环境具有明显的声望，但在阳朔这样的环境中的课程可能会令人愉快。对于粤语课程，请前往香港、澳门或广州。

Legal Matters
法律事务

China does not officially recognise dual nationality or the foreign citizenship of children born in China if one of the parents is a PRC national. If you have Chinese and another nationality you may, in theory, not be allowed to visit China on your foreign passport. In practice Chinese authorities are unlikely to know if you own two passports, and should accept you on a foreign passport. Dual-nationality citizens who enter China on a Chinese passport are subject to Chinese laws and are legally not allowed consular help. If over 16 years of age, carry your passport with you at all times as a form of ID (in recent years, passport checking in China has become far more common).
如果父母一方是中国公民，则中国不正式承认双重国籍或在中国出生的孩子的外国国籍。如果您有中国和其他国籍，理论上您可能不允许使用外国护照访问中国。实际上，中国当局不太可能知道您是否拥有两本护照，而应该接受您持有外国护照。持中国护照入境中国的双重国籍公民受中国法律约束，法律上不允许领事协助。如果年满 16 岁，请始终随身携带护照作为身份证件（近年来，中国的护照检查变得更加普遍）。

Gambling is officially illegal in mainland China, as is distributing religious material.
赌博在中国大陆是正式非法的，分发宗教材料也是如此。

China takes a particularly dim view of opium and all its derivatives; trafficking in more than 50g of heroin can lead to the death penalty. Foreign passport holders have been executed in China for drug offences. Random drug testing can be conducted on foreign nationals entering China. If you test positive you can be prosecuted irrespective of where the drugs were consumed. There can also be an uptick in arrests of foreigners for drug offences during times of tension between China and other nations.
中国对鸦片及其所有衍生物的看法特别黯淡;贩运超过 50 克海洛因可判处死刑。外国护照持有人因毒品犯罪在中国被处决。可以对入境中国的外国人进行随机药物检测。如果您的检测结果呈阳性，无论在哪里吸毒，您都可能被起诉。在中国与其他国家关系紧张的时期，因毒品犯罪被捕的外国人也可能增加。

The Public Security Bureau (PSB) likes to know where everyone is staying. Hotels will supply the PSB with the necessary documentation, including a photocopy of your passport information page and visa details as well as, sometimes, your onward travel plans. If, however, you are staying with a friend in China, you will need to register your place of residence with the PSB within 24 hours. This procedure is time-consuming as you will need to identify the PSB office responsible for registering foreign residents and you will need to take along proof of address, a copy of your friend’s ID and your passport.
公安局 （PSB） 喜欢知道每个人住在哪里。酒店将向 PSB 提供必要的文件，包括您的护照信息页和签证详细信息的复印件，有时还包括您的后续旅行计划。但是，如果您与中国的朋友住在一起，则需要在 24 小时内向 PSB 登记您的居住地。这个程序非常耗时，因为您需要确定负责登记外国居民的 PSB 办公室，并且您需要携带地址证明、朋友的身份证复印件和护照。

Be aware that there can be an increase in arrests of foreigners during times of international friction. After a senior Huawei senior executive was arrested in Canada for extradition to the US in December 2018, two Canadian citizens were arrested on spying charges and Chinese courts also sentenced two Canadians to death on drug smuggling charges.
请注意，在国际摩擦时期，逮捕外国人的人数可能会增加。2018年12月，华为一名高级管理人员因引渡到美国在加拿大被捕后，两名加拿大公民因间谍罪被捕，中国法院还以走私毒品罪判处两名加拿大人死刑。

The Chinese criminal justice system does not ensure a fair trial and defendants are not presumed innocent until proven guilty. If arrested, most foreign citizens have the right to contact their embassy.
中国的刑事司法系统不确保公平审判，被告在被证明有罪之前不会被推定为无罪。如果被捕，大多数外国公民有权联系他们的大使馆。

ATMs
自动取款机

Bank of China 24-hour ATMs are plentiful, and you can use Visa, MasterCard, Cirrus, Maestro Plus and American Express to withdraw cash. All ATMs accepting international cards have dual-language ability. The network is largely found in sizeable towns and cities. ATMs at the Industrial & Commercial Bank of China (ICBC) may work with international cards, but not everywhere. The exchange rate on ATM withdrawals is similar to that for credit cards, but there is a maximum daily withdrawal amount. Note that banks can charge a withdrawal fee for using the ATM network of another bank, so check with your bank before travelling.
ATM 取款的汇率与信用卡相似，但有每日最高取款金额。请注意，银行可能会对使用另一家银行的 ATM 网络收取取款费，因此请在旅行前咨询您的银行。

Cash
现金

The Chinese currency is the rénmínbì (RMB), or ‘people’s money’. The basic unit of RMB is the yuán (元, ¥), which is divided into 10 jiǎo (角), which is again divided into 10 fēn (分). Colloquially the yuán is referred to as kuài, and jiǎo as máo (毛). The fēn has so little value these days that it is rarely used.
中国货币是人民币（RMB），即“人民的钱”。人民币的基本单位是元（ 元 ，¥），分为 10 角，又分为 10fēn（ 分 ）。通俗地说，yuán 被称为 kuài，jiǎo 被称为 máo（ 毛 ）。如今，fēn 的价值很小，以至于很少使用。

The Bank of China issues RMB bills in denominations of ¥1, ¥2, ¥5, ¥10, ¥20, ¥50 and ¥100. Coins come in denominations of ¥1, 5 jiǎo, 1 jiǎo and 5 fēn. Paper versions of the coins remain in circulation.
中国银行发行人民币票据面额有 1 元、2 元、5 元、10 元、20 元、50 元和 100 元。硬币面额有 1 日元、5 jiǎo、1 jiǎo 和 5 fēn 两种面额。硬币的纸质版本仍在流通。

Hong Kong’s currency is the Hong Kong dollar (HK$), which is divided into 100 cents. Bills are issued in denominations of HK$10, HK$20, HK$50, HK$100, HK$500 and HK$1000. Copper coins are worth 50c, 20c and 10c, while the $5, $2 and $1 coins are silver and the $10 coin is nickel and bronze. The Hong Kong dollar is pegged to the US dollar at a rate of US$1 to HK$7.80, though it is allowed to fluctuate a little.
香港的货币是港币（HK$），分为 100 美分。票据的面额有港币 10 元、港币 20 元、港币 50 元、港币 100 元、港币 500 元及港币 1000 元。铜币价值 50 美分、20 美分和 10 美分，而 5 美元、2 美元和 1 美元的硬币是银币，10 美元的硬币是镍币和青铜币。港元与美元挂钩，汇率为 1 美元至 7.80 港元，但允许略有波动。

Macau’s currency is the pataca (MOP$), which is divided into 100 avos. Bills are issued in denominations of MOP$10, MOP$20, MOP$50, MOP$100, MOP$500 and MOP$1000. There are copper coins worth 10, 20 and 50 avos and silver-coloured MOP$1, MOP$2, MOP$5 and MOP$10 coins. The pataca is pegged to the Hong Kong dollar at a rate of MOP$103.20 to HK$100. In effect the two currencies are interchangeable and Hong Kong dollars, including coins, are accepted in Macau. Chinese rénmínbì is also accepted in many places in Macau at one-to-one. You can’t spend patacas anywhere else, however, so use them before you leave Macau. Prices quoted are in yuán unless otherwise stated.
澳门的货币是澳门元（MOP$），分为 100 阿沃斯。钞票面额有澳门币 10 元、澳门币 20 元、澳门币 50 元、澳门币 100 元、澳门币 500 元及澳门币 1000 元。有价值 10、20 及 50 牛油的铜币，以及澳门币 1 元、2 元、5 元及 10 元的银币。澳门元与港元挂钩，汇率为澳门元 103.20 元至 100 港元。实际上，这两种货币是可以互换的，澳门接受港元，包括硬币。澳门许多地方也接受一对一的中文 rénmínbì。但是，您不能在其他地方使用澳门元，因此请在离开澳门之前使用它们。除非另有说明，否则报价均以元为单位。

Credit Cards
信用卡

In large tourist towns, credit cards are relatively straightforward to use, but don’t expect to be able to use them everywhere and it’s always a good idea to carry enough cash. The exception is in Hong Kong, where international credit cards are accepted almost everywhere (though some shops may try to add a surcharge to offset the commission charged by credit-card companies, which can range from 2.5% to 7%). Check to see if your credit-card company charges a foreign transaction fee (usually between 1% and 3%) for purchases in China.
在大型旅游城镇，信用卡使用起来相对简单，但不要指望能够在任何地方使用它们，携带足够的现金总是一个好主意。香港是个例外，几乎所有地方都接受国际信用卡（尽管一些商店可能会尝试增加附加费以抵消信用卡公司收取的佣金，佣金从 2.5% 到 7% 不等）。检查您的信用卡公司是否对在中国购物收取国外交易费（通常在 1% 到 3% 之间）。

Where they are accepted, credit cards often deliver a slightly better exchange rate than banks. Money can also be withdrawn at certain ATMs in large cities on credit cards such as Visa, MasterCard and Amex.
在接受信用卡的地方，信用卡的汇率通常比银行稍好。也可以在大城市的某些 ATM 机上使用 Visa、MasterCard 和 Amex 等信用卡取款。

Mobile Payment Apps
移动支付应用程序

Their use is complicated for foreign visitors, but local Chinese use WeChat (微信, wēixìn, WeChat Pay) and Alipay (支付宝, zhīfùbǎo) apps to pay for virtually everything from taxi rides and market vendors, to hotels and even donating to beggars in the street. Cash is, for the moment, still widely accepted, but China is increasingly a cashless society. Neither WeChat nor Alipay was useful for visitors at the time of writing as it was necessary to link a Chinese bank account to your digital wallet before funds could be received from any source. Check the latest before you head to China as things could change. It is still fine to use cash (or credit cards, where they are accepted) in most instances.
对于外国游客来说，它们的使用很复杂，但当地的中国人使用微信（ 微信 、 微信支付）和支付宝（ 支付宝 、zhīfùbǎo） 应用程序来支付几乎所有费用，从乘坐出租车和市场摊贩到酒店，甚至向街上的乞丐捐款。目前，现金仍然被广泛接受，但中国正日益成为一个无现金社会。在撰写本文时，微信和支付宝对访问者都没有用处，因为必须先将中国银行账户链接到您的数字钱包，然后才能从任何来源收到资金。在前往中国之前查看最新情况，因为情况可能会发生变化。在大多数情况下，使用现金（或接受信用卡）仍然是可以的。

For a visitor the only accessible system that allows foreign cards is Apple Pay, which is accepted where you see the Apple Pay or QuickPass logos. Payments are made by holding your compatible device against the payment machine and verifying with your fingerprint.
对于访客来说，唯一允许使用外国卡的可访问系统是 Apple Pay，当您看到 Apple Pay 或 QuickPass 徽标时，可以接受它。付款是将您的兼容设备靠在支付机上并用您的指纹进行验证。

Moneychangers
货币兑换商

It’s best to wait till you reach China to exchange money as the exchange rate will be better. Foreign currency and travellers cheques can be changed at border crossings, international airports, branches of the Bank of China, tourist hotels and some large department stores. Hours of operation for foreign-exchange counters are 8am to 7pm (later at hotels). Top-end hotels will generally change money for hotel guests only. The official rate is given almost everywhere and the exchange charge is standardised, so there is little need to shop around for the best deal.
最好等到到达中国再兑换货币，因为汇率会更好。外币和旅行支票可以在边境口岸、国际机场、中国银行分行、旅游酒店和一些大型百货公司兑换。外币兑换柜台的营业时间为上午 8 点至晚上 7 点（酒店营业时间较晚）。高端酒店一般只会为酒店客人兑换货币。几乎到处都有官方汇率，兑换费用也是标准化的，因此几乎没有必要货比三家以获得最优惠的价格。

Australian, Canadian, US, UK, Hong Kong and Japanese currencies and the euro can be changed in China. In some backwaters it may be hard to change lesser-known currencies; US dollars are still the easiest to change.
澳大利亚、加拿大、美国、英国、香港和日本的货币和欧元可以在中国兑换。在一些死水区，可能很难改变鲜为人知的货币;美元仍然是最容易兑换的。

Keep at least a few of your exchange receipts. You will need them if you want to exchange any remaining yuán you have at the end of your trip.
至少保留一些兑换收据。如果您想在旅行结束时兑换剩余的元，您将需要它们。

Taxes & Refunds
税费和退款

When shopping, tax is already included in the displayed prices. Nearly all of the major cities offer a tax refund for foreign tourists on purchases made in the previous 90 days; the list of provinces offering the service keeps expanding. The 11% tax is refunded at the airport and all items must leave China with you. Goods have a minimum purchase of ¥500 from the one store.
购物时，显示的价格中已包含税费。几乎所有主要城市都为外国游客提供过去 90 天内购物的退税;提供该服务的省份名单不断扩大。11% 的税费在机场退还，所有物品必须随身携带中国。商品在一家商店的最低购买额为 500 日元。

Travellers Cheques
旅行支票

With the prevalence of ATMs across China, travellers cheques are not as useful as they once were and cannot be used everywhere, so always ensure you carry enough ready cash. You should have no problem cashing travellers cheques at tourist hotels, but they are of little use in budget hotels and restaurants. Most hotels will only cash the cheques of guests. If cashing them at banks, aim for larger ones such as the Bank of China or ICBC.
随着自动柜员机在中国各地的普及，旅行支票不再像以前那样有用，也无法在任何地方使用，因此请始终确保携带足够的现成现金。在旅游酒店兑现旅行支票应该没有问题，但它们在经济型酒店和餐馆中几乎没有用处。大多数酒店只会兑现客人的支票。如果在银行兑现，请瞄准较大的银行，例如中国银行或中国工商银行。

Stick to the major companies such as Amex and Visa. In big cities travellers cheques are accepted in almost any currency, but in smaller destinations it’s best to stick to currencies such as US dollars or UK pounds. Keep your exchange receipts so you can change your money back to its original currency when you leave.

Crime

Travellers are more often the victims of petty economic crime, such as theft, than serious crime. Foreigners are natural targets for pickpockets and thieves – keep your wits about you and make it difficult for thieves to get at your belongings.
旅行者更常成为盗窃等轻微经济犯罪的受害者，而不是严重犯罪。外国人是扒手和小偷的天然目标——保持头脑清醒，让小偷难以拿走你的财物。

High-risk areas in China are train and bus stations, city and long-distance buses (especially sleeper buses), hard-seat train carriages and public toilets.
中国的高风险地区是火车站和汽车站、城市和长途公交车（尤其是卧铺公交车）、硬座火车车厢和公共厕所。

Women should be aware of the dangers when travelling solo. Even in Beijing, single women taking taxis have been taken to remote areas and robbed by taxi drivers.
女性应意识到独自旅行时的危险。甚至在北京，单身女性乘坐出租车也曾被带到偏远地区，被出租车司机抢劫。

If something of yours is stolen, report it immediately to the nearest Foreign Affairs Branch of the Public Security Bureau (PSB; 公安局, Gōng’ānjú). Staff will ask you to fill in a loss report before investigating the case.
如果您的物品被盗，请立即向最近的公安局外事处（PSB; 公安局 ，Gōng'ānjú）。工作人员会要求您在调查案件之前填写损失报告。

A loss report is crucial so you can claim compensation if you have travel insurance. Be prepared to spend many hours, perhaps even several days, organising it. Make a copy of your passport in case of loss or theft.
损失报告至关重要，因此如果您有旅行保险，您可以要求赔偿。准备好花费许多时间，甚至几天来组织它。复印您的护照，以防丢失或被盗。

Scams
诈骗

Watch out for any solicitations to go to teahouses or expensive cafes in cities such as Shanghai, as you could be left with a huge bill.
当心任何去上海等城市的茶馆或昂贵咖啡馆的招揽，因为您可能会留下巨额账单。

Taxi scams at Beijing’s Capital Airport are legendary – always join the queue at the taxi rank and insist that the taxi driver uses his or her meter. Try to avoid pedicabs and motorised three-wheelers wherever possible – there are widespread complaints against pedicab drivers who originally agree on a price and then insist on an alternative figure once you arrive at the destination (language complications will work against you).
北京首都机场的出租车诈骗是传奇的——总是在出租车站排队，坚持让出租车司机使用他或她的计价器。尽可能避免使用三轮车和机动三轮车——人们普遍抱怨三轮车司机最初就价格达成一致，然后在您到达目的地后坚持使用替代数字（语言复杂性会对您不利）。

Watch out for itinerant monks asking for donations to their temple as a fair number are bogus. If you wish to donate to a temple, visit the temple and do so there.
当心要求向他们的寺庙捐款的巡回僧侣，因为相当多的僧侣是假的。如果您想向寺庙捐款，请参观寺庙并在那里进行。

Be alert at all times if you decide to change money or buy tickets (such as train tickets) on the black market, which we can’t recommend.
如果您决定在黑市上兑换货币或购买车票（例如火车票），请随时保持警惕，我们不推荐这样做。

Always be alert when buying unpriced goods (which is a lot of the time): foreigners are frequently ripped off. Always examine your restaurant bill carefully for hidden extras and if paying by credit card, ensure there are no extra charges.
购买未定价的商品时要时刻保持警惕（很多时候）：外国人经常被敲诈。请务必仔细检查您的餐厅账单是否有隐藏的额外费用，如果使用信用卡付款，请确保没有额外费用。

Transport
运输

Traffic accidents are the major cause of death in China for people aged between 15 and 45, and the World Health Organization (WHO) estimates there are 700 traffic deaths per day in China (with 60% being vulnerable road users, such as pedestrians, cyclists and motorbike riders). The proliferation of fast e-bikes (electric bikes) added to the mortality rate (so much so that several cities, including Shenzhen, have either restricted their use or banned them).
交通事故是中国 15 至 45 岁人群死亡的主要原因，世界卫生组织 （WHO） 估计中国每天有 700 人交通死亡（其中 60% 是弱势道路使用者，如行人、骑自行车者和摩托车骑手）。快速电动自行车（电动自行车）的激增增加了死亡率（以至于包括深圳在内的几个城市要么限制使用，要么禁止使用）。

Seat-belt use on long-distance buses has improved greatly over recent years, and on-board announcements and checks ensure passengers have their seat belts fastened. Taxi drivers continue, however, to insist you don’t need to use seat belts – it can often be impossible to find seat belts in the rear seats of taxis. Sit in the front if there is space.
近年来，长途公交车上安全带的使用有了很大改善，车上的广播和检查确保乘客系好安全带。然而，出租车司机继续坚持认为您不需要系安全带——在出租车的后座上通常找不到安全带。如果有空间，坐在前面。

Your greatest danger in China will almost certainly be crossing the road, so develop 360-degree vision and a sixth sense. Electric cars and ‘hoverboards’ can approach quite silently. Crossing only when it is safe to do so could keep you perched at the side of the road in perpetuity, but don’t imitate the local tendency to cross without looking. Note that cars frequently turn on red lights in China, so the green ‘walk now’ figure does not always mean it is safe to cross.
在中国，你最大的危险几乎肯定是过马路，所以要培养 360 度视野和第六感。电动汽车和“气垫板”可以非常安静地接近。只有在安全的情况下才过马路可能会让你永远坐在路边，但不要模仿当地不看就过马路的趋势。请注意，在中国，汽车经常闯红灯，因此绿色的“立即步行”数字并不总是意味着可以安全过马路。

Tourist Information
旅游信息

Poor and entirely inadequate tourist information has always been one of the banes of travelling around China. An enduring and highly perplexing resistance to staffing tourist information centres with enthusiastic and polite English-speaking staff means that such information points are often little better than useless. While you may be able to find literature and maps, you may also discover yourself being steered (for commercial reasons) onto a tour. In this regard China compares most unfavourably with countries such as South Korea or Japan.
旅游信息匮乏且完全不足一直是中国旅游的祸根之一。在旅游信息中心配备热情和有礼貌的英语工作人员方面，这种持久且令人困惑的抵制意味着，这种信息点往往比无用好不到哪里去。虽然您也许能够找到文献和地图，但您也可能会发现自己（出于商业原因）被引导去旅行。在这方面，中国与韩国或日本等国家相比最为不利。

Far better information generally exists online or at youth hostels; the latter are frequently staffed by proficient English speakers who show a genuine interest in promoting travel within their region. It may be worth contacting the China National Tourist Office (www.cnto.org) prior to travelling to China, but don’t expect to see CNTO offices dotted helpfully around the country. Visas
签证

Check your local Chinese embassy website for the latest information on obtaining a visa to visit China during the Covid-19 pandemic. At the time of writing, only a limited range of people (for example those with residence permits for China) could obtain a visa to visit China; they would need to provide a health declaration form from the Chinese embassy (issued if evidence is supplied of negative nucleic acid and IgM antibody tests for Covid-19, taken no more than 48 hours before travel to China) or prove that they had been inoculated with a vaccine produced in China (unavailable in some countries). Entry to China is then followed by a 14-day quarantine.
查看您当地的中国大使馆网站，了解有关在 Covid-19 大流行期间获得访问中国签证的最新信息。在撰写本文时，只有少数人（例如持有中国居留许可的人）可以获得访问中国的签证;他们需要提供中国大使馆的健康申报表（如果提供证据证明 Covid-19 核酸和 IgM 抗体检测呈阴性，在前往中国前不超过 48 小时进行），则签发）或证明他们已接种中国生产的疫苗（在某些国家/地区不可用）。进入中国后将进行为期 14 天的隔离。

Travellers arriving in China will receive a health declaration form and an arrivals form to complete.
除此之外，入境中国的旅客并不存在特别困难。中国移民官员一丝不苟，高度官僚主义，但并不过分冒犯。主要要求是签证到期后六个月内有效旅行的护照和签证。抵达中国的旅客将收到一份健康申报表和一份入境表格。

Large numbers of westerners work in China with international development charities such as Voluntary Service Overseas (VSO). A placement in China offers an excellent opportunity to get first-hand experience of local life, while contributing to the community and learning Chinese at the same time.
大量西方人在中国为海外志愿服务协会（VSO）等国际发展慈善机构工作。在中国实习提供了一个绝佳的机会，可以亲身体验当地生活，同时为社区做出贡献，同时学习中文。

Adventure China (www.adventurechina.com) Various volunteering programmes in summer camps and beyond.
Adventure China （www.adventurechina.com） 夏令营及其他活动的各种志愿服务项目。

International Volunteer HQ (www.volunteerhq.org) Volunteer projects based in and around Xi’an, focussing mainly on English teaching, special-needs care and summer outreach teaching projects.
国际志愿者总部 （www.volunteerhq.org） 位于习安及其周边地区的志愿者项目，主要侧重于英语教学、特殊需求护理和暑期外展教学项目。

World Teach (www.worldteach.org) Volunteer teachers (mostly for English, but also other subjects and skills).
世界教学 （www.worldteach.org） 志愿者教师（主要是英语，但也有其他科目和技能）。

VSO (www.vsointernational.org) Improving education and livelihood and fighting poverty.
VSO （www.vsointernational.org） 改善教育和生计以及消除贫困。

Work
工作

It is essential to have the appropriate visa and paperwork to engage in work, and penalties for illegal employment can include detention and deportation.
必须拥有适当的签证和文件才能从事工作，对非法就业的处罚可能包括拘留和驱逐出境。

The English language is a resource that remains in high demand. With recognised ELT qualifications, such as TEFL, and/or experience, teaching in larger cities can be both financially rewarding and provide an opportunity to learn Chinese and travel within China. Salaries at schools in cities such as Beijing start from around ¥14,000 to ¥18,000 per month for qualified teachers; an accommodation stipend is also sometimes provided. You could also try approaching organisations such as the British Council (www.britishcouncil.org), which runs teacher placement programmes in China.
英语是一种需求量仍然很大的资源。凭借公认的 ELT 资格（例如 TEFL）和/或经验，在大城市任教既可以带来经济回报，又可以提供学习中文和在中国旅行的机会。北京等城市学校合格教师的工资从每月 14,000 日元到 18,000 日元不等;有时还提供住宿津贴。您也可以尝试与英国文化协会 （www.britishcouncil.org） 等组织联系，该组织在中国开展教师安置计划。
