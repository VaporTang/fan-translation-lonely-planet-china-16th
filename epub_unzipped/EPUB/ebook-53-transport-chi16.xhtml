China doesn’t have one single national airline, but has large airlines that operate both domestic and international flights. The biggest are Air China (www.airchina.com), Shanghai-based China Eastern Airlines (www.ceair.com), and China Southern Airlines (www.cs-air.com), based in Guangzhou. They fly to China from the US, Europe, Australia/New Zealand and other parts of Asia. Benefits can include a generous checked-luggage allowance, and sometimes a night’s accommodation when stopping over on the way to other destinations – great for visa-free travel. Multiple international carriers also fly to China along similar routes.

Tickets
票

The cheapest tickets to Hong Kong and China exist on price-comparison websites or in discount agencies in Chinatowns around the world. Budget and student-travel agents offer cheap tickets, but the real bargains are with agents that deal with the Chinese, who regularly return home. Airfares to China peak between June and September.
飞往香港和中国的最便宜的机票可以在比价网站或世界各地唐人街的折扣机构中找到。经济型旅行社和学生旅行社提供便宜的机票，但真正划算的是与定期回国的中国人打交道的旅行社。飞往中国的机票价格在 6 月至 9 月期间达到高峰。

The cheapest flights to China are with airlines requiring a stopover at the home airport, such as Air France to Beijing via Paris, or Malaysia Airlines to Beijing via Kuala Lumpur.
飞往中国最便宜的航班是需要在本国机场中途停留的航空公司，例如法航经巴黎飞往北京，或马来西亚航空经吉隆坡飞往北京。

The best direct ticket deals are available from China’s international carriers, such as China Eastern Airlines, Air China and China Southern Airlines.
中国的国际航空公司提供最优惠的直飞机票，例如中国东方航空、中国国际航空和中国南方航空。

Land
土地

China shares borders with Afghanistan, Bhutan, India, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Laos, Mongolia, Myanmar (Burma), Nepal, North Korea, Pakistan, Russia, Tajikistan and Vietnam – the borders with Afghanistan, Bhutan and India are closed. There are also official border crossings between China and its special administrative regions, Hong Kong and Macau.
中国与阿富汗、不丹、印度、哈萨克斯坦、吉尔吉斯斯坦、老挝、蒙古、缅甸、尼泊尔、朝鲜、巴基斯坦、俄罗斯、塔吉克斯坦和越南接壤，与阿富汗、不丹和印度的边界是关闭的。中国与其特别行政区香港和澳门之间也有官方边境口岸。

Lonely Planet China guidebooks may be confiscated by officials, primarily at the Vietnam–China border.
《孤独星球中国 》旅游指南可能会被官员没收，主要是在越南和中国边境。

Note that borders can suddenly close; this is an issue that can particularly affect border crossings in Xinjiang.
请注意，边界可能会突然关闭;这是一个特别可能影响新疆过境的问题。

Kazakhstan
哈萨克斯坦

Border crossings from Ürümqi to Kazakhstan are via border posts at Korgas, Alashankou, Tacheng and Jimunai. Ensure you have a valid Kazakhstan visa (obtainable, at the time of writing, in Ürümqi, or from Beijing) or China visa.
从乌鲁木齐到哈萨克斯坦的过境点是通过科尔加斯、阿拉山口、塔城和吉木奈的边境哨所。确保您持有有效的哈萨克斯坦签证（在撰写本文时可在乌鲁木齐或北京获得）或中国签证。

Apart from Alashankou, which links China and Kazakhstan by train, all other border crossings are by bus; you can generally also get a bike over, however. Two trains weekly (32 hours) run between Ürümqi and Almaty, and one train per week runs to Astana.
除了通过火车连接中国和哈萨克斯坦的阿拉山口外，所有其他过境点均乘坐公共汽车;但是，您通常也可以骑自行车过去。乌鲁木齐和阿拉木图之间每周有两趟列车（32 小时一班），每周有一趟列车开往阿斯塔纳。

Remember that borders open and close frequently due to changes in government policy; additionally, many are only open when the weather permits. It’s always best to check with the Public Security Bureau (PSB; 公安局; Gōng’ānjú; map; %0991 281 0452, ext 3456; 30 Nanhu Donglu; 南湖东路30号; h10am-1.30pm & 4-6pm Mon-Fri) in Ürümqi for the official line.
请记住，由于政府政策的变化，边境经常打开和关闭;此外，许多仅在天气允许时才开放。最好向公安局 （PSB; 公安局 ;Gōng'ānjú; 地图 ; % 0991 281 0452，分机 3456;30、南湖东路; 南湖东路 30 号 h 10am-1.30pm & 4-6pm 周一至周五） 在乌鲁木齐的官方线路。

Kyrgyzstan
吉尔吉斯斯坦

There are two routes between China and Kyrgyzstan: one between Kashgar and Osh, via the Irkeshtam Pass; and one between Kashgar and Bishkek, via the dramatic 3752m Torugart Pass.
中国和吉尔吉斯斯坦之间有两条路线：一条在喀什和奥什之间，途经伊尔克什塔姆山口;喀什和比什凯克之间，途经引人注目的 3752 米托鲁加特山口。

Laos
老挝

From the Mengla district in China’s southern Yunnan province, you can enter Laos via Boten in Luang Nam Tha province (from Mohan on the China side), while a daily bus runs between Vientiane and Kunming south bus station, and also from Jinghong to Luang Nam Tha and also to Luang Prabang.
从中国云南省南部的勐腊区出发，可经琅南塔省的博腾（从中国一侧的莫汉出发）进入老挝，万象和昆明南汽车站之间有每日巴士，也从景洪到琅南塔，也到琅勃拉邦。

On-the-spot visas for Laos are available at the border, the price of which depends on your nationality (you cannot get a China visa here). Alternatively, you can obtain a visa for Laos from the Lao consulate in Kunming.
老挝的现场签证可在边境获得，价格取决于您的国籍（您无法在这里获得中国签证）。或者，您也可以从老挝驻昆明领事馆获得老挝签证。

Mongolia
蒙古

From Beijing the Trans-Mongolian Railway trains and the K23 train run to Ulaanbaatar. There are also trains and regular buses between Hohhot and the border town of Erlian (Erenhot). Mongolian visas can be acquired in Beijing, Hohhot, Haila’er or Erlian.
从北京出发，横贯蒙古的铁路列车和 K23 列车开往乌兰巴托。呼和浩特和边境城镇二连（二连浩特）之间也有火车和定期巴士。蒙古签证可以在北京、呼和浩特、海剌儿或二连获得。

Myanmar (Burma)
缅甸（缅甸）

The famous Burma Road runs from Kunming in Yunnan province to the Burmese city of Lashio. The road is open to travellers carrying permits for the region north of Lashio, though you can legally cross the border in only one direction – from the Chinese side (Jiegao) into Myanmar. However, at the time of writing it was not possible for independent third-country nationals to travel across the border at Jiegao, though there were rumours that it may be possible as part of a tour group. The only way to go is by air from Kunming. Visas are available at the embassy in Beijing or the Myanmar consulate in Kunming.
著名的缅甸公路从云南省昆明一直延伸到缅甸城市腊戌。这条路对持有腊戌以北地区许可证的旅客开放，但您只能从一个方向合法越过边境——从中国一侧（界高）进入缅甸。然而，在撰写本文时，独立的第三国国民不可能在界高过境旅行，尽管有传言称作为旅行团的一部分可能是可能的。唯一的出路是从昆明乘飞机。签证可在驻北京大使馆或缅甸驻昆明领事馆办理。

Nepal
尼泊尔

The 865km road connecting Lhasa with Kathmandu is known as the Friendship Hwy, currently only traversable for foreign travellers by rented vehicle. It’s a spectacular trip across the Tibetan plateau, with the highest point being Gyatso-la Pass (5248m).
连接拉萨和加德满都的这条 865 公里的公路被称为友谊高速公路，目前只有外国游客可以租车穿越。这是一次穿越青藏高原的壮观之旅，最高点是嘉措拉山口（5248米）。

Visas for Nepal can be obtained in Lhasa, at the border at Kodari, or in Beijing.
尼泊尔签证可以在拉萨、科达里边境或北京获得。

When travelling from Nepal to Tibet, foreigners still have to arrange transport through tour agencies in Kathmandu. Access to Tibet can, however, be restricted for months at a time without warning.
从尼泊尔到西藏旅游，外国人还是要通过加德满都的旅行社安排交通。然而，进入西藏可能会在没有警告的情况下一次被限制数月。

CLIMATE CHANGE & TRAVEL
气候变化与旅行

Every form of transport that relies on carbon-based fuel generates CO2, the main cause of human-induced climate change. Modern travel is dependent on aeroplanes, which might use less fuel per kilometre per person than most cars but travel much greater distances. The altitude at which aircraft emit gases (including CO2) and particles also contributes to their climate change impact. Many websites offer ‘carbon calculators’ that allow people to estimate the carbon emissions generated by their journey and, for those who wish to do so, to offset the impact of the greenhouse gases emitted with contributions to portfolios of climate-friendly initiatives throughout the world. Lonely Planet offsets the carbon footprint of all staff and author travel.
每一种依赖碳基燃料的运输方式都会产生二氧化碳，这是人为引起的气候变化的主要原因。现代旅行依赖于飞机，与大多数汽车相比，飞机每人每公里使用的燃料可能更少，但飞行距离要远得多。飞机排放气体（包括 CO2）和颗粒物的高度也会影响其气候变化的影响。许多网站提供“碳计算器”，让人们可以估算他们旅途中产生的碳排放量，对于那些希望这样做的人来说，可以通过为世界各地的气候友好型倡议组合做出贡献来抵消排放的温室气体的影响。《孤独星球》抵消了所有员工和作者旅行的碳足迹。

North Korea
朝鲜

Visas for North Korea are not especially hard to arrange, though it is not possible to travel independently so you will need to be on a pre-planned tour. Those interested in travelling to North Korea on tours from Beijing should contact Koryo Tours (map; %010 6416 7544; www.koryogroup.com; 27 Beisanlitun Nan, 北三里屯南27号; bLine 2 to Dongsi Shitiao, exit C, or Line 10 to Tuanjiehu, exit A).
朝鲜的签证并不是特别难安排，但无法独立旅行，因此您需要参加预先计划的旅行。有兴趣从北京出发前往朝鲜旅游的人请联系高丽旅游 （ 地图 ; % 010 6416 7544;www.koryogroup.com; 北三里屯南 27 号线 2 号 b 线至东四石条 C 出口，或 10 号线至团结湖 A）出口 。

Four international express trains (K27 and K28) run between Beijing train station and Pyongyang.
四列国际特快列车（K27 和 K28）往返于北京火车站和平壤之间。

Pakistan
巴基斯坦

The exciting trip on the Karakoram Hwy, said to be the world’s highest public international highway, is an excellent way to get to or from Chinese Central Asia. There are buses from Kashgar for the two-day trip to the Pakistani town of Sost via Tashkurgan when the pass is open. Chinese customs and immigration procedures take place in Tashkurgan, not actually at the border.
喀喇昆仑高速公路（据说是世界上最高的公共国际高速公路）上的激动人心的旅程是往返中亚的绝佳方式。当通票开放时，有从喀什出发的巴士经塔什库尔干前往巴基斯坦索斯特镇的两天游。中国海关和移民手续在塔什库尔干进行，而不是在边境。

Pakistani visas are no longer available to tourists on arrival (and visas are difficult to get in Beijing), so the safest option is to arrive in China with a visa obtained in your home country. Check the current situation as this could change. Proof of a recent polio vaccination is required by the Chinese side, especially if you have spent some time in Pakistan.
巴基斯坦签证不再提供给游客落地（在北京很难获得签证），因此最安全的选择是持在本国获得的签证抵达中国。检查当前情况，因为这可能会发生变化。中方要求提供近期接种脊髓灰质炎疫苗的证明，特别是如果你在巴基斯坦呆过一段时间。

Russia
俄罗斯

The train from Harbin East to Vladivostok is no longer running, but you can take the train to Suifenhe and take an onward connection there.
从哈尔滨东到符拉迪沃斯托克的火车已停运，但您可以乘坐火车前往绥芬河，并在那里继续转乘。

The Trans-Mongolian (via Erlian) and Trans-Manchurian (via Harbin) branches of the Trans-Siberian Railway run from Beijing to Moscow. Obtain your Russian visa at the Russian embassy in Beijing.
西伯利亚大铁路的跨蒙古（经二连）和跨满洲（经哈尔滨）支线从北京开往莫斯科。在俄罗斯驻北京大使馆获得俄罗斯签证。

There are also border crossings 9km from Manzhouli and at Heihe.
距离满洲里和黑河 9 公里处还有边境口岸。

Tajikistan
塔吉克斯坦

In recent years it has been hard for foreigners to enter China here, but at the time of writing the Qolma (Kulma) Pass, linking Tashkurgan with Murghab, was open to foreign travellers (and has been since around 2017). The pass is not open on Saturday or Sunday. Obtain your visa at the Tajikistan Embassy in Beijing.
近年来，外国人很难进入中国，但在撰写本文时，连接塔什库尔干和穆尔加布的库尔马（库尔马）通行证已向外国游客开放（自 2017 年左右开始）。该通行证在周六或周日不开放。在塔吉克斯坦驻北京大使馆办理签证。

Vietnam
越南

Visas are unobtainable at border crossings. Vietnam visas can be acquired in Beijing, Kunming, Hong Kong and Nanning. China visas can be obtained in Hanoi.
在过境点无法获得签证。越南签证可以在北京、昆明、香港和南宁获得。中国签证可以在河内获得。

FRIENDSHIP PASS
友谊通行证

China’s busiest border with Vietnam is at the obscure Vietnamese town of Dong Dang, 164km northeast of Hanoi. The closest Chinese town to the border is Ping-xiang in Guangxi province, about 10km north of the actual border gate.
中国与越南最繁忙的边境位于河内东北 164 公里处的越南名不见经传的同党镇。距离边境最近的中国城镇是广西省的屏乡，距离实际边境口岸以北约 10 公里。

Seven Hanoi-bound buses run from Nanning via the Friendship Pass. Twice-weekly trains (T5 and T6) connect Beijing and Hanoi (via Nanning), while a daily train (T8701 and T8702) links Hanoi with Nanning.
七趟开往河内的巴士从南宁出发，经友谊通行证开往南宁。每周两班列车（T5 和 T6）连接北京和河内（途经南宁），而每天一班列车（T8701 和 T8702）连接河内和南宁。

HEKOU
河口

The Hekou–Lao Cai border crossing is 468km from Kunming and 294km from Hanoi. At the time of writing, the only way to reach Vietnam via Hekou was by bus from Kunming.
河口-老街边境口岸距昆明 468 公里，距河内 294 公里。在撰写本文时，经河口到达越南的唯一途径是从昆明乘坐巴士。

MONG CAI
向前看

A little-known border crossing is at Mong Cai in the northeast corner of Vietnam, just opposite the Chinese city of Dongxing and around 200km south of Nanning.
一个鲜为人知的过境点位于越南东北角的芒街，就在中国东兴市对面，南宁以南约200公里处。

River
河

The fast river ferries from Jinghong in Yunnan to Chiang Saen in Thailand have been suspended for many years now, though it is still possible to hitch a ride on a cargo boat.
从云南景洪到泰国清盛的快流渡轮已经停运多年，但仍可以搭上货船。

GETTING AROUND
交通

Air
空气

China’s air network is extensive and growing fast. Air safety and quality have improved considerably, but the speed of change and crowded skies can impact on reliability, and flight delays are very common. When deciding between flying and using high-speed rail, remember that trains almost always leave on time.
中国的航空网络广泛且发展迅速。航空安全和质量已大大提高，但变化的速度和拥挤的天空会影响可靠性，航班延误非常普遍。在决定乘坐飞机还是使用高铁时，请记住火车几乎总是准时发车。

Planes vary in style and comfort, but service has vastly improved over recent years. You may get a hot meal, or just a small piece of cake and an airline souvenir. On-board announcements are delivered in Mandarin and English.
飞机的风格和舒适度各不相同，但近年来服务有了很大的改善。你可能会得到一顿热饭，或者只是一小块蛋糕和一份航空公司的纪念品。机上公告以普通话和英语发布。

Shuttle buses to airports usually run from Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC; Zhōngguó Mínháng) offices, train stations, some hotels or bus stations in towns and cities throughout China, often running via other stops. For domestic flights, arrive at the airport two hours before departure.
前往机场的班车通常从中国民用航空局（CAAC;Zhōngguó Mínháng）的办公室、火车站、中国各地城镇的一些酒店或汽车站，通常通过其他站点运行。对于国内航班，请在起飞前两小时到达机场。

Remember to keep your baggage receipt label on your ticket as you might need to show it when you collect your luggage.
请记住将行李收据标签保留在机票上，因为您可能需要在领取行李时出示该标签。

Departure tax is included in the price of a ticket.
离境税已包含在机票价格中。

Tickets
票

Except during major festivals and holidays, tickets are easy to purchase, with an oversupply of airline seats. For the best deals, purchase tickets online. Discounts are common, except when flying into large cities such as Shanghai and Beijing on the weekend, when the full fare can be the norm. Fares are calculated according to one-way travel, with return tickets simply costing twice the single fare. If flying from Hong Kong or Macau to mainland China, note that these are classified as international flights – it is much cheaper to travel overland into Shenzhen, Zhuhai or Guangzhou and fly from there.
除重大节日和节假日外，机票很容易购买，航空公司座位供过于求。为了获得最优惠的价格，请在线购买门票。折扣很常见，除了周末飞往上海、北京等大城市时，全价可能是常态。票价是根据单程旅行计算的，往返机票的价格只是单程票价的两倍。如果从香港或澳门飞往中国大陆，请注意，这些航班被归类为国际航班——从陆路前往深圳、珠海或广州并从那里起飞要便宜得多。

Bicycle
自行车

Bikes (自行车, zìxíngchē) are an excellent method for getting around China’s cities and tourist sights. They can also be invaluable for exploring the countryside and surrounding towns.
自行车（ 自行车 ，zìxíngchē） 是游览中国城市和旅游景点的绝佳方式。它们对于探索乡村和周边城镇也是无价的。

Hire
雇

Station-less bike-sharing systems in cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, Chengdu, Lanzhou, Xi’an and Xiamen have the potential to be useful, but at the time of writing were very hard to use for travellers. At basic minimum you’ll need a smartphone with internet and SMS access (for verification, so data-only SIM cards are insufficient). You’ll also need to download the app and complete the registration process, which involves submitting photos of your passport. The final hurdle is the killer: you’ll need to use WeChat or Alipay apps, with credit loaded on them, and at the time of writing you needed a Chinese bank account to do this.
北京、上海、成都、兰州、习安和厦门等城市的无站共享单车系统有可能有用，但在撰写本文时，旅行者很难使用。在基本情况下，您至少需要一部可以访问互联网和短信的智能手机（用于验证，因此纯数据 SIM 卡是不够的）。您还需要下载该应用程序并完成注册过程，其中包括提交护照照片。最后一个障碍是杀手锏：你需要使用微信或支付宝应用程序，并在它们上加载信用，而在撰写本文时，你需要一个中国银行账户来做到这一点。

Other local public bike-hire schemes are, by and large, not foreigner-friendly either, so generally the best places to try are youth hostels, which rent out bicycles – as do many hotels, though the latter are more expensive.
当地其他公共自行车租赁计划总体上也不适合外国人，因此通常最好的尝试地点是青年旅舍，它们出租自行车——许多酒店也是如此，尽管后者更贵。

Touring
旅游

Cycling through China allows you to go when you want, to see what you want and at your own pace. It can also be an extremely cheap, as well as a highly authentic, way to see the land.
骑自行车穿越中国，您可以随时随地去，按照自己的节奏去看看自己想要的东西。这也是一种极其便宜且高度真实的土地观赏方式。

You will have virtually unlimited freedom of movement but, considering the size of China, you will need to combine your cycling days with trips by train, bus, boat, taxi or even planes, especially if you want to avoid particularly steep regions, or areas where the roads are poor or the climate is cold.
您将拥有几乎无限的行动自由，但考虑到中国的幅员辽阔，您需要将骑自行车日与乘坐火车、公共汽车、轮船、出租车甚至飞机旅行结合起来，特别是如果您想避开特别陡峭的地区，或道路较差或气候寒冷的地区。

A basic packing list for cyclists includes a good bicycle-repair kit, sunscreen and other sun protection, waterproofs, fluorescent strips and camping equipment. Ensure you have adequate clothing, as many routes will be taking you to considerable altitude. Road maps in Chinese are essential for asking locals for directions.
骑自行车者的基本装箱单包括良好的自行车修理包、防晒霜和其他防晒用品、防水用品、荧光灯条和露营设备。确保您有足够的衣服，因为许多路线将带您到达相当高的海拔高度。中文路线图对于向当地人问路至关重要。

Bike China Adventures (www.bikechina.com) arranges tours and is a good source of information for cyclists coming to China.
Bike ChinaAdventures （www.bikechina.com） 安排旅游，是来中国的自行车爱好者的一个很好的信息来源。

Boat
船

Boat services within China are very limited, especially with the massive growth of high-speed rail and expressways. They’re most common in coastal areas, where you are likely to use a boat to reach offshore islands such as Putuoshan or Hainan, or the islands off Hong Kong. The Yantai–Dalian ferry will probably survive because it saves hundreds of kilometres of overland travel, though a superlong undersea tunnel remains on the drawing board. Construction – if commenced – would take almost 20 years.
中国境内的船舶服务非常有限，尤其是在高速铁路和高速公路的大规模增长下。它们最常见于沿海地区，您可能会乘船到达普陀山或海南等近海岛屿，或香港附近的岛屿。烟台-大连渡轮可能会幸存下来，因为它节省了数百公里的陆路旅行，尽管一条超长的海底隧道仍在绘图板上。如果开始建设，将需要近 20 年的时间。

The best-known river trip is the three-day boat ride along the Yangzi (Cháng Jiāng) from Chongqing to Yichang. The Li River (Lí Jiāng) boat trip from Guilin to Yangshuo is a popular tourist ride.
最著名的河流之旅是从重庆到宜昌的扬子河（Cháng Jiāng）游船三天。从桂林到阳朔的漓江（李江）游船之旅是一种受欢迎的旅游游乐设施。

Hong Kong employs an out-and-out navy of vessels that connect with the territory’s myriad islands, and a number of boats run between the territory and other parts of China, including Macau, Zhuhai, Shekou (for Shenzhen) and Zhongshan.
香港拥有一支不折不扣的海军，与香港的众多岛屿相连，多艘船只往返于香港与中国其他地区之间，包括澳门、珠海、蛇口（深圳）和中山。

Bus
总线

Long-distance bus (长途公共汽车, chángtú gōnggòng qìchē) services are extensive and reach places you cannot access by train. With the increasing number of intercity highways, journeys are getting quicker. Some services have reduced frequencies or have vanished altogether due to the appearance of high-speed train routes, but in provinces with limited rail services (eg Qinghai), the bus is still king.
长途巴士（ 长途公共汽车 ，chángtú gōnggòng qìchē） 服务广泛，可以到达火车无法到达的地方。随着城际高速公路数量的增加，出行速度也越来越快。由于高铁路线的出现，一些服务的班次减少或完全消失，但在铁路服务有限的省份（例如青海），公交车仍然是王道。

The standard of buses and service has vastly improved in China over the last few years, partly due to competition from high-speed train routes but also as a general raising of the bar to meet more international levels. Routes between big cities sport large, clean and comfortable fleets of private buses, some equipped with toilets and hostesses handing out snacks, mineral water and checking you have fastened your seat belt. Shorter and more far-flung or distant routes can still rely on rattling minibuses into which as many fares as possible are crammed and which may only depart when full and then may trawl the streets looking for fares.
在过去的几年里，中国的公交车和服务水平有了很大的提高，部分原因是来自高速列车路线的竞争，但也因为标准普遍提高，以满足更多的国际水平。大城市之间的路线拥有大型、干净和舒适的私人巴士车队，其中一些配备了厕所，女服务员分发零食、矿泉水并检查您是否系好安全带。更短、更远或更远的路线仍然可以依靠嘎嘎作响的小巴，这些小巴里塞满了尽可能多的票价，并且可能只有在满员时才出发，然后可能会在街上拖网寻找票价。

Sleeper buses (卧铺客车, wòpù kèchē) ply popular long-haul routes, costing around double the price of a normal bus service. Many buses have been upgraded over the last few years, so you can now expect reasonable comfort, though they generally will not have on-board toilets and will stop for toilet breaks, when smokers will huddle in packs by the roadside. If an overnight train follows the same route, opt for that, for comfort and the conviviality of the experience. You may find older-style sleeper buses on other routes, in which case prepare for more cramped conditions and less cleanliness.
卧铺客车（卧铺客车 ，wòpù kèchē） 在受欢迎的长途路线上行驶，其价格大约是普通巴士服务的两倍。在过去的几年里，许多公共汽车都进行了升级，因此您现在可以期待合理的舒适度，尽管它们通常没有车上厕所，并且会在上厕所时停下来，此时吸烟者会成群结队地挤在路边。如果夜行列车遵循相同的路线，请选择该路线，以获得舒适和欢乐的体验。您可能会在其他路线上找到旧式卧铺巴士，在这种情况下，请为更狭窄的条件和更不干净的环境做好准备。

Bus journey times should be used as a rough guide only. You can estimate times for bus journeys on non-highway routes by calculating the distance against a speed of 25km/h.
巴士行程时间仅供粗略参考。您可以通过计算 25 公里/小时的速度的距离来估算非公路路线上的巴士行程时间。

All cities and most towns have one or more long-distance bus station (长途汽车站, chángtú qìchēzhàn), generally located in relation to the direction in which the bus heads. Where there are multiple stations, each station is generally named after a compass point (north bus station, east bus station etc). Most bus stations have a left-luggage counter. Your bags will be scanned either before you are allowed into the ticketing hall or before you board the bus, but checks are less stringent than on the rail network.
所有城市和大多数城镇都有一个或多个长途汽车站 （ 长途汽车站 ，chángtú qìchēzhàn）， 通常与公交车的方向相关。在有多个车站的情况下，每个车站一般都以一个指南针点命名（北公交站、东公交站等）。大多数巴士站都设有行李寄存柜台。在您被允许进入售票大厅之前或上车之前，您的行李将被扫描，但检查不如铁路网络严格。

In many cities the train station forecourt doubles as a bus station.
在许多城市，火车站前院兼作公交车站。

Tickets
票

Tickets are generally cheaper and easier to get than train tickets; turn up at the bus station and buy your ticket on the spot. The earlier you buy, the closer to the front of the bus you will sit, though you may not be able to buy tickets prior to your day of travel. You will need your passport to purchase a bus ticket.
车票通常比火车票便宜、更容易买到;出现在巴士站并当场购买车票。您越早购买，您就会坐在离巴士前面越近的位置，但您可能无法在旅行前购买车票。您需要护照才能购买巴士票。

Tickets can be harder to procure during national holiday periods.
在国定假日期间，门票可能更难购买。

To check the times of buses and ticket prices, consult either Ctrip (www.ctrip.com) or Bus365 (www.bus365.com), though both are Chinese-language only.
要查看公交车的时刻表和票价，请咨询携程（www.ctrip.com）或 Bus365（www.bus365.com），尽管两者都只有中文。

Dangers & Annoyances
危险与烦恼

Breakdowns can be a hassle, and some rural roads and provincial routes (especially in the southwest, Tibet and the northwest) remain in not-so-good condition. Precipitous drops, potholes, dangerous road surfaces and reckless drivers mean accidents remain common. Long-distance journeys can also be cramped and noisy. Lack of seat belts is not the issue it once was, though you may still find buses without seat belts in more far-flung areas.
故障可能会很麻烦，一些农村道路和省道（尤其是西南、西藏和西北）的状况仍然不太好。陡峭的跌落、坑洼、危险的路面和鲁莽的司机意味着事故仍然很常见。长途旅行也可能局促且嘈杂。没有安全带不再是以前的问题，尽管您可能仍然会在更偏远的地区发现没有安全带的公共汽车。

Note the following when travelling by bus:
乘坐巴士旅行时请注意以下事项：

ATake plenty of warm clothes on buses to high-altitude destinations in winter. A breakdown in frozen conditions can prove lethal for those unprepared.
A 冬季带上大量保暖的衣服乘坐巴士前往高海拔目的地。对于那些毫无准备的人来说，冰冻条件下的崩溃可能是致命的。

ATake a lot of extra water on routes across areas such as the Taklamakan Desert.
在穿越塔克拉玛干沙漠等地区的路线上 A 多取大量水。

Car & Motorcycle
汽车和摩托车

To hire a car or motorcycle in China you must hold a valid People’s Republic of China driving licence. Foreign driving licences or even those supported by an International Driving Permit (IDP) are simply not accepted. E-bikes and e-scooters generally do not have the same restrictions. If you wish to travel around by car, you will need to hire one with a driver or book a taxi for the day or half-day (or however long you wish). To drive in Hong Kong and Macau, you will need an IDP.
要在中国租用汽车或摩托车，您必须持有有效的中华人民共和国驾驶执照。外国驾驶执照甚至由国际驾驶许可证 （IDP） 支持的驾照根本不被接受。电动自行车和电动滑板车通常没有相同的限制。如果您想驾车四处旅行，您需要租一辆带司机的司机或预订一天或半天（或您希望多长时间）的出租车。要在香港和澳门开车，您需要 IDP。

Cars in China drive on the right-hand side of the road. Even skilled drivers will be unprepared for China’s roads – in the cities, cars lunge from all angles and chaos abounds.
中国的汽车在道路右侧行驶。即使是熟练的司机也会对中国的道路毫无准备——在城市里，汽车从各个角度冲来，混乱比比皆是。

Local Transport
本地交通

Long-distance transport in China is good, but local transport is less efficient, except in cities with metro systems. The choice of local transport is diverse but vehicles can be slow and overburdened, and the network confusing for visitors. Hiring a car is often impractical, while hiring a bike can be inadequate. Unless the town is small, walking is often too tiring.
中国的长途交通很好，但当地交通效率较低，除了有地铁系统的城市。当地交通工具的选择多种多样，但车辆速度慢且负担过重，而且网络会让游客感到困惑。租车通常是不切实际的，而租自行车可能不够用。除非小镇很小，否则步行往往太累。

On the plus side, local transport is cheap, taxis are usually ubiquitous and affordable, and clean and efficient metro systems continue to rapidly expand in large tourist towns.
从好的方面来说，当地交通便宜，出租车通常无处不在且价格实惠，清洁高效的地铁系统继续在大型旅游城镇迅速扩张。

Bus
总线

With extensive networks, buses are an excellent way to get around towns, but foreign travellers rarely use them and you will often be the only non-Chinese person on the bus. When boarding a bus, point to your destination on a map and the conductor (seated near the door) will sell you the right ticket. The conductor will usually tell you where to disembark, provided they remember (which they usually do). In conductor-less buses you put money for your fare into a slot near the driver as you embark. A variety of bus apps such as 车来了(chēláile; ‘the bus is coming’) can inform you of the arrival time of the next bus, but they are only available in Chinese.
公共汽车网络广泛，是游览城镇的绝佳方式，但外国游客很少使用它们，而且您通常是公共汽车上唯一的非中国人。登上巴士时，在地图上指向您的目的地，售票员（坐在门口附近）会向您出售合适的车票。售票员通常会告诉您在哪里下车，前提是他们记得（他们通常会这样做）。在无售票员的公共汽车中，您可以在上车时将车费放入司机附近的一个车位中。各种公交应用程序，例如车来了 （chēláile;'公交车来了'）可以通知你下一班公交车的到达时间，但只有中文。

AFares are very cheap (usually ¥1 to ¥2), but buses may be packed.
A 票价非常便宜（通常为 1 至 2 日元），但巴士可能会挤满人。

AIn cities such as Beijing, Shanghai and Hong Kong, a locally purchased transport card can be used on the buses.
A 在北京、上海和香港等城市，可以在巴士上使用当地购买的交通卡。

AAlipay and WeChat Pay can be used on many buses.
A 支付宝和微信支付可以在许多公交车上使用。

ANavigation is tricky for non-Chinese speakers as bus routes at bus stops are generally listed in Chinese, without pinyin.
A 对于不会说中文的人来说，导航很棘手，因为公交车站的公交路线通常以中文列出，没有拼音。

AIn Beijing, Shanghai and other large tourist cities and towns, stops will also be announced in English.
A 在北京、上海等旅游大城镇，站点也将以英文公布。

AAlways have change ready if there is no conductor on the bus.
A 如果公交车上没有售票员，请随时准备好零钱。

ABuses with snowflake motifs are air-conditioned.
带有雪花图案的 A 巴士都装有空调。

ATraffic can make things slow.
A 交通会使事情变慢。

ADisembark through the back door.
从后门 A 下船。

Subway, Metro & Light Rail
地铁、地铁和轻轨

Going underground or using light rail is fast, efficient and cheap. Most networks are either very new or relatively recent and can be found in a rapidly growing number of cities, including Beijing, Chengdu, Chongqing, Dalian, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Harbin, Hong Kong, Kunming, Qingdao, Shanghai, Shenyang, Shenzhen, Suzhou, Tianjin, Wuhan, Xiamen and Xi’an. Transport cards for use on metro systems can be purchased in large cities such as Hong Kong, Beijing and Shanghai, and many metro systems also take WeChat Pay and Alipay.
进入地下或使用轻轨既快速、高效又便宜。大多数网络要么非常新，要么相对较新，可以在快速增长的城市找到，包括北京、成都、重庆、大连、广州、杭州、哈尔滨、香港、昆明、青岛、上海、沈阳、深圳、苏州、天津、武汉、厦门和习安。在香港、北京、上海等大城市可以购买地铁系统使用的交通卡，许多地铁系统也使用微信支付和支付宝。

Taxi
出租车

Taxis (出租汽车, chūzū qìchē) are cheap and easy to find. Taxi rates per kilometre are clearly marked on a sticker on the rear side window of the taxi; flagfall varies from city to city, and depends on the size and quality of the vehicle. Most taxis have meters but they may only be switched on in larger towns and cities. If the meter is not used (on an excursion out of town, for example, or when hiring a taxi for the day or half-day), negotiate a price before you set off and write the fare down. If you want the meter used, ask for dǎbiǎo (打表). Also ask for a receipt (发票, fāpiào) – if you leave something in the taxi, you can have the car located by its vehicle number printed on the receipt. If paying in cash, you may find that drivers no longer carry much change; carry small notes when hailing a cab. You can generally pay the taxi driver using WeChat Pay or Alipay, if you wish. WeChat has a useful ride-hailing function through DiDi Chuxing (滴滴出行; www.didiglobal.com), the most popular mobile-phone taxi-hailing app on the China market (Uber sold its market share to DiDi Chuxing in 2016).
出租车（出租汽车 ，chūzū qìchē） 价格便宜且容易找到。每公里的出租车费率清楚地标明在出租车后侧窗的贴纸上;旗帜落下因城市而异，取决于车辆的尺寸和质量。大多数出租车都有计价器，但它们可能只在较大的城镇打开。如果不使用计价器（例如，在出城游览时，或在租用一天或半天的出租车时），请在出发前协商价格并记下票价。如果您想使用仪表，请要求 dǎbiǎo（ 打表 ）。还要索取收据（ 发票 ，fāpiào）——如果您在出租车上留下东西，您可以通过收据上打印的车号来定位汽车。如果用现金支付，您可能会发现司机不再携带太多零钱;叫出租车时携带小纸条。如果您愿意，您通常可以使用微信支付或支付宝向出租车司机付款。微信通过滴滴出行（滴滴出行 ;www.didiglobal.com），中国市场上最受欢迎的手机打车应用程序（Uber 在 2016 年将其市场份额出售给滴滴出行）。

A 出租车司机很少会说英语——用字符写下你的目的地。
