Why Go?
为什么要去？

Despite being a popular destination with domestic travellers, Guizhou (贵州, Guìzhōu) remains largely unknown to travellers outside China – and what a travesty of justice. The province has two of the country’s largest and most spectacular natural features – a waterfall and a cave – while outside the capital, Guiyang, it’s pretty much green hills and valleys, flowing rivers and limestone formations to the horizon.
尽管贵州（贵州 ，Guìzhōu）是国内游客的热门目的地，但中国以外的游客仍然不为人所知——这是对正义的嘲讽。该省拥有全国最大、最壮观的两个自然景观——瀑布和洞穴——而在首都贵阳之外，这里几乎是绿色的山丘和山谷，流淌的河流和石灰岩地层一直延伸到地平线。

Guizhou’s people are as diverse as its environment. Around 37% of the province’s population consists of more than 18 ethnic minorities. Flitting around the Dong and Miao villages in the east of the province is like an anthropological dream sequence. Nearby is the ancient riverside settlement of Zhenyuan, as striking as Guilin to the south, where Chinese tourists chuckle into their hot-and-spicy sour fish soup that they still have it all to themselves.
贵州的人民和环境一样多样化。该省约 37% 的人口由超过 18 个少数民族组成。在省东的侗族和苗族村庄周围飞来飞去，就像一个人类学的梦境序列。附近是镇远古老的河边聚落，与南边的桂林一样引人注目，中国游客在那里对着他们热辣的酸鱼汤咯咯地笑着，他们仍然拥有这一切。

History
历史

Chinese rulers set up an administration in this area as far back as the Han dynasty (206 BC–AD 220), but it was merely an attempt to maintain some measure of control over Guizhou’s non-Han tribes.
早在汉朝（公元前 206 年至公元 220 年），中国统治者就在该地区建立了行政机构，但这只是为了保持对贵州非汉族部落的某种程度的控制。

It wasn’t until the Sino-Japanese war, when the Kuomintang made Chongqing their wartime capital, that the development of Guizhou began. Most of this activity ceased at the end of WWII; industrialisation of the area wasn’t revived until the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) began construction of the railways.
直到甲午战争，国民党将重庆作为战时首都，贵州的发展才开始。大部分活动在二战结束时停止;直到中国共产党 （CCP） 开始修建铁路后，该地区的工业化才得以恢复。

Despite an expanding mining industry, Guizhou’s GDP per capita remains the lowest in all China.
尽管采矿业不断扩张，但贵州的人均 GDP 仍然是中国最低的。

Language
语言

Mandarin Chinese is spoken by the Han majority, although with a distinctive local accent. Thai and Lao are spoken by some, and Miao-Yao (Hmong-mien) dialects by the Miao and Yao.
汉语是汉人占多数的人，尽管带有独特的当地口音。有些人说泰语和老挝语，苗族和瑶族说苗族-瑶族（苗族）方言。

High-speed trains radiate out from Guiyang, connecting the city with other major destinations around China, including Guangzhou, Xi’an and Shanghai. It’s easy to combine southeastern Guizhou with a trip to Guangxi province – you can even start your journey in Hong Kong.
高铁从贵阳辐射而出，将这座城市与中国其他主要目的地连接起来，包括广州、习安和上海。贵州东南部与广西省之旅很容易结合起来——您甚至可以从香港开始您的旅程。

Sleeper trains to Chengdu, Kunming and Guilin are popular. You can enter Guizhou by train from Hunan through the back door from Huaihua to Zhenyuan.
前往成都、昆明和桂林的卧铺列车很受欢迎。从怀化乘坐火车从湖南经后门进入镇远。

8Getting Around
8 四处走走

The high-speed train is very handy for the southern half of the province, cutting journey times significantly between most of the major areas of interest. Regular trains serve smaller towns and cities, while buses are useful for northern Guizhou. New expressways access the more remote western areas of the province. Roads in the main tourist areas are generally good but between smaller cities and villages they remain a work in progress.
高铁在该省的南半部非常方便，大大缩短了大多数主要景点之间的旅行时间。定期列车服务于较小的城镇，而公共汽车则适用于贵州北部。新的高速公路通往该省更偏远的西部地区。主要旅游区的道路总体上很好，但在较小的城市和村庄之间，它们仍在进行中。

Guiyang 贵阳

Guiyang (Guìyáng) is an unpretentious, relatively youthful provincial capital under seemingly continual construction. While it may not leap out at the traveller, there are some interesting sights and affordable fine hotels, and the city’s location makes it a perfect base for exploring the surrounding southern countryside, especially Huangguoshu Falls, the ethnic villages around Kaili, and historic Zhenyuan.
贵阳（Guìyáng）是一个朴实无华、相对年轻的省会城市，看似在不断建设。虽然它可能不会让旅行者感到震惊，但这里有一些有趣的景点和价格实惠的高级酒店，而且城市的地理位置使其成为探索周围南部乡村的完美基地，尤其是黄果树瀑布、凯里周围的民族村庄和历史悠久的镇远。

The city passed through the hands of various warring states until it became established during the Yuan dynasty in 1283. While Guiyang has not featured prominently in the annals of Chinese history, it has since emerged from the smoke of the country’s industrial boom.
这座城市经历了各个战国的手中，直到 1283 年元朝时期才建立。虽然贵阳在中国历史上没有占据显着位置，但它已经从中国工业繁荣的硝烟中崛起。

Constructed during the Ming dynasty in 1598, this triple-roofed pavilion perched atop a boulder is Guiyang’s most iconic landmark. Built to celebrate the local people as ‘the finest and most talented under heaven’, the structure contains slabs of marble and an impressive art and calligraphy collection.
这座三层屋顶的凉亭建于 1598 年明朝，坐落在巨石之上，是贵阳最具标志性的地标。该建筑旨在庆祝当地人民是“天下最优秀、最有才华的人”，包含大理石板和令人印象深刻的艺术和书法收藏。

Minority celebrations are lively events that can last for days at a time, and often include singing, dancing, horse racing and buffalo fighting.
少数民族庆祝活动是热闹的活动，一次可以持续数天，通常包括唱歌、跳舞、赛马和水牛斗殴。

One of the biggest is the lusheng festival, held in either spring or autumn, depending on the village: a lúshēng is a reed instrument used by the Miao people. Other important festivals include the Dragon Boat Festival (端午节, Duānwǔ Jié; hJun), the hill-leaping festival and the ‘sharing the sister’s meal’ festival (equivalent to Valentine’s Day in the west). Bull or bird fights are held at many of the celebrations, which, while not usually to the death, might be distressing to animal-loving visitors. The Miao New Year is celebrated on the first four days of the 10th lunar month in Kaili, Guading, Zhouxi and other Miao areas.

所有少数民族节日都遵循农历，因此日期每年都不同。它们也会因村庄和萨满而异。

Longli 隆里

%0855 / Pop 180,000 (county level)
% 0855 / 人口 18 万（县级）

Stranded in splendid isolation amid fields and rice paddies near the Hunan border, Longli (Lónglǐ) is a former garrison town populated by the descendants of Han soldiers sent to ‘protect’ the Ming empire from the ‘pesky’ Miao. One of the province’s ‘ecomuseums’ – that is, a real-life village – Longli is fascinating for its stone buildings that have more in common with the architecture of east China’s Anhui and Jiangxi provinces than the wooden buildings of nearby Miao and Dong villages.
隆里（龙里）被困在湖南边境附近的田野和稻田中，与世隔绝，以前是驻军城镇，居住着被派去“保护”明帝国免受“讨厌”苗族侵害的汉兵的后裔。龙里是该省的“生态博物馆”之一，即现实生活中的村庄，其石头建筑与中国东部安徽省和江西省的建筑有更多的共同点，而不是附近苗族和侗族村庄的木制建筑。

Enter Longli via the ivy-covered Qingyang Gate (青阳门, Qīngyáng Mén) and take an hour’s stroll to savour its warren of narrow cobblestone streets, passing brightly painted frontages of the stone buildings, lovely courtyards, pavilions, temples and town walls. Be sure to walk along the remaining city wall, starting to the right of Qingyang Gate as you enter.
从常春藤覆盖的青阳门（青阳门 ，Qīngyáng Mén）进入龙里，步行一个小时，品味狭窄的鹅卵石街道，经过色彩鲜艳的石头建筑临街面、美丽的庭院、亭台楼阁、寺庙和城墙。一定要沿着剩下的城墙走，从青阳门的右侧开始进入。

After serving under the Ming emperors, who paid their army wages, the residents of Longli were largely abandoned by Qing dynasty rulers who had other military priorities. As a consequence, activity in the town shifted to education and Longbiao Academy (龙标书院, Lóngbiāo Shūyuàn) F was just one seat of learning set up to serve local scholars. Inside, there’s an interesting map depicting Longli at the height of its military power.
在明朝皇帝手下服役并支付军饷后，隆里居民基本上被清朝统治者抛弃了，他们有其他军事优先事项。因此，该镇的活动转向教育， 龙标书院只是 F 为当地学者服务而设立的学习场所之一。里面有一张有趣的地图，描绘了隆里军事力量鼎盛时期的地图。

If you want to stay overnight, Gǔchéng Jiǔdiàn (古城酒店; %13638554888, 0855 718 0018; next to the east gate entrance, 隆里古城东大门旁; tw ¥100; aW) has basic rooms with western-style toilets and is located in the square in front of the Qingyang Gate (eastern) entrance.
如果您想过夜，古城酒店 % 13638554888,0855 718 0018; 隆里古城东大门旁东门入口旁;100 元; a W ） 有带西式卫生间的基本客房，位于青阳门（东）入口前的广场上。

A few basic restaurants set up when the tourist buses pass through; otherwise, cuisine on offer caters to local tastes. You might see villagers selling live bamboo rats (野竹鼠, yězhú shǔ) to take home for the pot.
旅游巴士经过时设立的几家基本餐厅;除此之外，提供的美食迎合了当地人的口味。您可能会看到村民出售活竹鼠（野竹鼠 ，yězhú shǔ）带回家当锅。

要前往瀑布，您首先需要从赤水乘坐小巴（12日元，11/2小时，从早上6：50到下午4：30左右）到瀑布售票处。从那里，您可以乘坐班车（单程/往返 10/20 日元）前往瀑布–从下车点沿陡峭的台阶步行 10 分钟即可到达瀑布。或者，您可以沿着河对岸沿着阴凉的小路行走。您需要三到四个小时才能步行完成整个过程。
